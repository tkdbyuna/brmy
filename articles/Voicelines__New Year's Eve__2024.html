  • Release Period: December 27, 2024 00:00 JST - December 31, 2024 23:59 JST

Translated by Misora

Characters New Year's Eve 2024

Ai Kosaka 		年越しの瞬間ジャンプなんて、一体何があいつらをそうさせるんだ。おい由鶴、付き合うなよ
What exactly makes them think of jumping the moment the year changes? Oi, Yuzuru, you better not going.

Yuzuru Kise 		今年のものは今年のうちに。みんなが気持ちよく年を越せるように、もう一踏ん張りしよう
What needs to be done in this year should be done in this year. Let's do our best to finish them so that everyone can face the new year nicely.

Roka Suoh 		新年の目標は何か立てたかい？　僕はね‥‥‥みんなと楽しく過ごす！　もちろん言うまでもないだろうけど、君も含んでいるよ！
Have you thought of your new year goals? You see‥‥‥ mine will be spending fun time with everyone! I know I don't have to mention this but of course, you're included in it.

Kou Ayato 		ぜんっぜん今年終わる感ないわ。って毎年言ってるんだけどさ
It doesn't feel like the year is ending at all‥‥‥ Ah, that's what I say every year though.

Mao Ukyo 		鐘をついて煩悩が払えればわけないね。‥‥‥人間の欲も理想も底がないから、この商売が成り立つんだけど
Anything works as long as you can fulfill your worldly desires with money.‥‥‥ This business works because human desires are basically bottomless.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		日付変わる瞬間、一緒にジャンプせーへん？
Why don't we jump together the moment the date changes?

Yuragi Kanno 		ねむ‥‥‥寝てる間に年越しそう‥‥‥。べつに、それでもいっか
Sleepy‥‥‥ Seems like I'll be sleeping the moment the year changes‥‥‥ That's actually fine.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		もう年末ですか‥‥‥。本当にあっという間ですね。やり残したことはありませんか？
It's year end‥‥‥ It really went by in a blink of an eye. Do you have anything you have yet to do?

Haruhi Ichikawa 		大河？　今日は捕まんないんじゃない、大掃除だおせちだって張り切ってたし
Taiga? You probably won't see him today, I think? He was excited about cleaning up and osechi, etc.

Sei Okiya 		なんて寒い‥‥‥芯まで冷えて‥‥‥いえ、心まで凍えるようです‥‥‥うう
How cold‥‥‥ I'm chilled to the core‥‥‥ No, it feels like my heart is getting frozen‥‥‥ Uhh.

Shizuka Fushimi 		大晦日か。今年も早かったな‥‥‥センチメンタルになってるわけじゃない。此処にいると、言葉通り飛ぶように月日が過ぎてく
New Year's Eve. This year also went by fast‥‥‥ It's not that I feel sentimental of the sort but being here literally makes the days fly by.

Takeru Mikado 		つい最近年越した感じがしたんだけど‥‥‥ってヤダ！　今シワシワなこと言っちゃった、今のナシ！
I feel like the year changed recently but‥‥‥ Oh no! I just said something wrinkly, forget what I said just now!

Soyogu Shinkai 		大晦日だからって食ってすぐ寝んなよ。ま、怠惰に過ごしても俺がトレーニングで引き締めてやる
Don't sleep directly after you eat just because it's New Year's Eve. Well, I'll surely train you to keep you in shape, even if you spend time lazily.

Shinobu Aizawa 		大人になると１年があっという間すぎません？　貴方にとってどんな年でした？　俺はまあ、目標に向かって一歩前進って感じかな～
Don't you feel like a year passes by so fast when you're an adult? What kind of year was it for you? I'd say I managed to take a step closer to my goal l guess~

Kiho Arima 		この１年、どうだった？　締められそう？
How was this year? Do you feel you can close it up?

Kyoya Shido 		今年を振り返ると、君との出会いや小さなことから大きなことまで取り留めがないな。全部話しても？
Looking back on this year, there's so much to cover, like meeting you and the small, even big things that happened afterwards. Is it fine if I talk about all of them?

Riku Tateshina 		大晦日って感じしない‥‥‥って毎年言ってる気がするな、これ
It doesn't feel like New Year's Eve‥‥‥ Ah, I feel like I'm saying this every year.

Tomose Onda 		重要な任務だと聞いたが‥‥‥蕎麦打ち？　芦佳の考えることだ、意味があるんだろう
I heard that it was a crucial mission but‥‥‥ sobauchi[Note 1]? It must be Roka's idea. Should have a meaning

Yu Nina 		灯世に蕎麦打ちをさせようとしたのは誰だ。俺がやる
Who's the one making Tomose do sobauchi[Note 1]? I'll do it.

Kamiya 		あっという間の１年だったな‥‥‥。来年も何事もなく過ごせますように
It was a year that went by in a flash‥‥‥ May I spend next year without any bad things happening.

Urara Manami 		あけたからって何も変わんねえよ。昨日と同じ１日がまた始まるだけだ
Nothing will change even if the year changes. It's basically the same day as yesterday starting again.
