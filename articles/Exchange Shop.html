Exchange Shop (交換所, Kōkansho or CHANGE in english) is Break My Case's exchange place where players can exchange the game's currency to get item. To access Exchange Shop, tap MENU > Exchange Shop (交換所). To exchange items for other items, please visit Item for more details.
