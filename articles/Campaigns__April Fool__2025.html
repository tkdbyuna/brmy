Campaigns/April Fool/2025


Wake up, owner!

Kanji

オーナー起きて！

Translation

Wake up, owner!

Run Period

Start

April 01, 2026 00:00 JST

End

April 01, 2026 23:59 JST


Let’s all wake up Aporia’s owner, Roka Suoh, who is dreaming a mysterious dream.

A special April 1st–exclusive story campaign is now underway.

What did Roka see deep in his slumber…?

A limited‑time campaign page is available in‑game.

※ You can access the special page from the banner displayed either at the top right of the home screen or inside the “CHECK” button on the left side of the home screen.

During the campaign, by playing puzzles, special limited‑time stories will be unlocked according to the total number of “Wake‑Up Sets” collected by all users.

How to read the stories]

You can read the stories by pressing the book icon displayed on the special campaign page.

Please note: unlocked stories can only be read on April 1st, so don’t miss out!

On the home screen, you can listen to special voice lines from the staff, created especially for April Fools.

Characters April Fool 2025

Ai Kosaka 		背中に何かついてるだと‥‥‥？　この魚型‥‥‥犯人なんて１人しかいない‥‥‥芦佳！
Something on my back......? Hm, this fish pattern...... There can only be one culprit...... ROKA!

Yuzuru Kise 		逢さん、ああ見えてすっごく寝言が可愛いんです。嘘か本当かどっちだと思いますか？
Ai-san may look like that, but his sleep talk is very cute. Which do you think it is, a truth or a lie?

Roka Suoh 		まだ嘘を考えている途中でね。トモや有はんを驚かせる計画なんだ。越える壁は高い方がいいだろうそうだろうッ！
I'm still trying to come up with a lie. I plan to surprise Tomo and Yuhan. The higher the hurdle, the better, right? Right!?

Kou Ayato 		コンビニ行くけどいるもんある？　明星がエクレアだって揺にスクイーズ渡してさ。結果はご想像の通りで、それのカバーに
I'm going to the convenience store though, do you need anything? Akehoshi handed Yuragi an éclair squishy. It's what you think it is, I'm covering up for him.

Mao Ukyo 		エイプリルフールか。実は君と付き合ってる‥‥‥とか言ったら恋が困るだろうし、いい嘘じゃないね
April's Fool, huh. I'm actually going out with you...... if I said something like that, Kou would be troubled, so that's not a good lie.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		俺の目を７秒みたら好きになるで。よし、じゃあ見ててな‥‥‥って、吏来さんがめっちゃ見とる～。やめとくわ
Stare into my eyes for 7 seconds will make you like me. Alright, look closely...... Ah, Riku-san is glaring at me~ I'll stop.

Yuragi Kanno 		あんたがロッカーに置いてたお菓子、俺が食べた。‥‥‥嘘、なんだけど。もっと怒らないとだめ
I was the one who ate the sweets you left in the locker. ......It's a lie though, it's no use if you don't get angrier.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		実は昨日春日がランニングに行ったんですよ。‥‥‥嘘、バレました？　はは、春日のことももうわかってきてますね
Haruhi actually went for a run yesterday...... Did you figure out that it was a lie? Haha, you know Haruhi well already.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		アイコン変えて嘘動画出して、って‥‥‥どう考えても１日で消費されるべきコンテンツじゃなくね？
Change my profile picture and upload a lie video...... No matter how I think about it, aren't they just one-day limited contents?

Sei Okiya 		嘘というのは今日だけの特権でもないでしょうに。正直者達が非現実を味わう１日、といったところでしょうか
Even though lying isn't a privilege one can only have on this day. I suppose today is the day when honest people can have a taste of escaping reality.

Shizuka Fushimi 		俺、喋り出したら止まらないから普段は控えてるんだよね。‥‥‥ま、嘘だけど。信じた？
I won't stop talking when I start, so I usually hold myself back. ...... Well, it's a lie, did you believe it?

Takeru Mikado 		この前エッグい額、使っちゃったのよね～気になる？　お耳貸してちょうだい‥‥‥エイプリルフールの嘘って？　じゃあそういうことで♡
I spent a huge amount of money last time~ Are you curious? Lend me your ear then...... April's Fool lie, you said? Then it is what it is♡

Soyogu Shinkai 		くだんねー嘘考えてる暇があったら、頭か身体か鍛えた方がずっと為になるんじゃねーか？　おい、つまんねーって言ったやつ、ツラ貸せ
Wouldn't it be more beneficial to train your mind and body instead of wasting your time thinking of stupid lies? Oi, whoever said that it's boring, show me your face.

Shinobu Aizawa 		嘘を仕掛けるとしても、静さんは反応薄そうだしな‥‥‥グッチで様子見てミカ姉にお披露目して、ラスボス静さん。よし、レッツゴー！
Shizuka-san's would only give a little reaction to my lie......I should tell it to Gucchi first, unveil it to Mika-nee, and finally, the last boss Shizuka-san. Alright, let's go!

Kiho Arima 		嘘をつくことは、黙っていることより労力を費やすからね。俺は黙って過ごそうかな
Lying takes more effort than staying silent, so I'll stay silent today.

Kyoya Shido 		エイプリルフールか、特に人を騙したい願望はないな
It's April's Fool. I don't have any special desire to deceive people.

Riku Tateshina 		今日こそ交渉部の技術を最大限に発揮するべき時じゃない？　もちろん誰も傷つけない嘘で
Isn't today the perfect time to fully use my negotiation skill? Of course, I'll use a lie that won't hurt anyone.

Tomose Onda 		何かエイプリルフールの嘘を、と？　自分で言うのもなんだが、相手は合ってるか？
You're asking me for an April Fool's lie? Saying this on my own aside, are you asking the right person?

Yu Nina 		嘘？くだらないな
Lying? How frivolous.

Kamiya 		うーん、特に思い浮かばないな‥‥‥。騙すより、騙されている方が気が楽かも
Hmm, can't really come up with anything...... It might be easier for me to get deceived than to deceive someone.

Urara Manami 		大抵の人間が、今日だけじゃなくていつも嘘ついてんだろ
Most people lie not only for today but every day, don't they?
