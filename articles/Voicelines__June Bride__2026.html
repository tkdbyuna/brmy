Characters June Bride 2026

Ai Kosaka 		File:Voice kosaka 3 (vo home 135).mp3 この時期になると、遠回しにもわかりやすくも結婚にまつわる話題が浮上するが。毎年毎年、聞かれようが求められようが俺は変わらない
Around this time of year, topics related to marriage start to surface both indirectly or not. Whether I get questioned or pressured each year, my stance won't change.

Yuzuru Kise 		File:Voice kise 3 (vo home 135).mp3 Aporiaでも結婚にちなんだご依頼を受けることがありますが、どんな形でも、その人たちにとって新たな門出と思うと応援したくなります
Aporia also receives requests regarding marriage sometimes and no matter what form it takes, thinking about them getting a new beginning makes me want to support them.

Roka Suoh 		File:Voice suoh 3 (vo home 135).mp3 Aporiaのみんなにこういうお祝い事があったとしたらぜひ、この僕をスペシャルプレゼンターとして呼んでほしいネッ☆　思い出に残る最高の日を演出するよっ
If by any chance someone from Aporia does this kind of celebration, l'd like them to call me to be their special presenter☆ I'll definitely turn it into the most wonderful, memorable day!

Kou Ayato 		File:Voice ayato 3 (vo home 135).mp3 同級生が結婚式ラッシュで財布が痛むわ。　ま、おめでたいことだからしようがないんだけど
My former classmates are going through wedding season rush that my wallet is in critical condition now. Well, it can't be helped since it's a joyous occasion.

Mao Ukyo 		File:Voice ukyo 3 (vo home 135).mp3 ブーケを紫陽花にしたり、演出で傘を使う結婚式もあるらしいね。　その時々の状況を活かして楽しむって発想、個人的には結構好き
It seems like there are wedding receptions where they make hydrangeas into a bouquet or use umbrellas in the performance. Personally, I quite like the way they're making the most of the season.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		File:Voice hinomiya 3 (vo home 135).mp3 実際に結婚式とかみたら、俺も結婚したいなって思うんかなあ。あ、したくない派とかじゃないよ。ただ、このままでも十分楽しいから、今はええかなとも思うんよ
Would seeing an actual wedding make me want to get married? Ah, it's not like I don't want to. It's just that things are already fun enough, so I don't think I need it for now.

Yuragi Kanno 		File:Voice kanno 3 (vo home 135).mp3 代行の仕事で、結婚式は何度か出たことあるよ。ご飯がいつぱい出てくるから、結構大変
I've went to weddings for stand-in work. They have a lot of food, so I quite like it

Taiga Tsukimoto 		File:Voice tsukimoto 3 (vo home 135).mp3 ブライダル雑誌の付録って、たまに良い便利グッズがあるんですよね。買う勇気がないので、見ているだけなんですが‥‥‥
Bridal magazines would occasionally feature some nice and convenient freebies. I don't have the courage to buy it so I end up only looking at it, though.....

Haruhi Ichikawa 		File:Voice ichikawa 3 (vo home 135).mp3 たまたまブライダル関係のデザイン仕事が来て、おーそんな時期って感じたくらい。まあ結婚する人はお幸せに、とは思うよ
Sometimes I'd receive bridal-related design jobs that it makes me realize that the season has come, Well, I do wish happiness for the people getting married.

Sei Okiya 		File:Voice okiya 3 (vo home 135).mp3 婚姻とはドラマばかりを期待する関係ではなくなります。しかし何事もなくふたり安定した日々を過ごすということが何よりも劇的になる、とも言えるかもしれません
Marriage is a relationship where you no longer keep expecting a drama. However, I'd say that for the two of them to spend their days peacefully without any problem might be the most dramatic thing of all.

Shizuka Fushimi 		File:Voice fushimi 3 (vo home 135).mp3 結婚できる人‥‥‥っていうか、他人と住める人は尊敬する。嫌味じゃなくて、俺なら2日で限界が来る
I respect people who can get married.....Or rather, people who can live with other people. It's not a sarcasm, but I'll reach my limit in just 2 days.

Takeru Mikado 		File:Voice mikado 3 (vo home 135).mp3 結婚式の参列っていわゆる「ちょっとしたパーティ」よね。主役を引き立てるために何を着ていくか考えるのが本当に楽しいわ
Attending wedding reception is considered a "small party", yes? It's really fun to decide on what to wear to highlight the main characters of the day.

Soyogu Shinkai 		File:Voice shinkai 3 (vo home 135).mp3 その手段が結婚かどうかは置いといて幸せにしますって誓いをしたい気持ちは俺もわかる気がするわ。やっぱ大事な人は自分の手で幸せにしたいもんだろ
Leaving aside whether marriage is the only way or not, I feel like I understand the feeling of wanting to take a vow for making someone happy. As I thought, I want to make the person important to me to be happy with my own hands.

Shinobu Aizawa 		File:Voice aizawa 3 (vo home 135).mp3 結婚を「人生の墓場」っていうの、本来は結婚を前向きに捉えた言葉だったらしいですよ。結婚への価値観が時代で変化するみたいに、どこかで変わっちゃったんですかね
It seems like the phrase "grave of life" was originally used to describe marriage in a positive light. It probably changed somewhere just like how values regarding marriage change with the times.

Kiho Arima 		File:Voice arima 3 (vo home 135).mp3 『2人はすっと幸せ』という定めはあるよ。だけどそれに驕れば容易に歪むから、良い関係を維持する努力を怠らないことも前提に、運命って言うんじゃないかな
Theres an "idea" for the wedded couple to always be happy. But that can easily go wrong if you're complacent. As long as you don't neglect the effort to maintain a good relationship, you might just call that fate.

Kyoya Shido 		File:Voice shido 3 (vo home 135).mp3 教え子の結婚式でスピーチを頼まれた同僚がいてね、職員室がその話題で持ちきりだったよ。誰かの幸せを祈る時間というのは、やっぱり特別なものだね
A colleague of mine was asked to give a speech on their student's wedding and it became a hot topic in the staff room. As I thought, the time to pray for someone's happiness is special.

Riku Tateshina 		File:Voice tateshina 3 (vo home 135).mp3 結婚はゴールじゃないわけだけど。それでも通過点ではあると思うし、笑顔で送り出してあげたいよね
Marriage isn't exactly the final goal. Even so, I think it's still a passing point that makes me want to send them off with a smile.

Tomose Onda 		File:Voice onda 3 (vo home 135).mp3 生涯の誓いは極めて自主的な選択に思う。いずれ周りでその選択をとる者が現れるなら、祝いの言葉くらいはかける
I believe that making a lifelong vow is an extremely voluntary choice. If someone around me eventually makes that choice, I'll at least offer my congratulations to them.

Yu Nina 		File:Voice nina 3 (vo home 135).mp3 挙式での姿や結婚後の幸せな生活を、自分に置き換えて考えることはない。そういう幸福を思い描けるやつの元には、文字通りの幸福が訪れればいいとは思うが
I've never imagined myself as the one at a wedding ceremony or living a happy life after marriage. I do think those who envision such blessings should literally receive them.

Kamiya 		File:Voice kamiya 3 (vo home 135).mp3 ウ工ディングフォトって幸せの瑁間が閉じ込められてるから、後から見た時も幸せな気持ちになれるんだろうな。それって素敵なことだよね
A wedding photo captures a moment of happiness, so they can feel the happiness when they look at it again later. I think that's a wonderful thing.

Urara Manami 		File:Voice manami 3 (vo home 135).mp3 結婚？　んなもんの話オレに振るな
Marriage? Dont bring up that kind of thing to me.
