1.5th Anniversary BRMY Party

1.5th Anniversary BRMY Party (1.5th Anniversary ブレマイパーティ, 1.5th Anniversary Buremai Pāti?, lit. 1.5th Anniversary Bremai Party) is a Gacha Event happens alongside with the 1.5th Anniversary Event.

The cards illustrated by Utako Yukihiro, the game's character designer, for Haruhi Ichikawa and Soyogu Shinkai are introduced.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.

Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): November 06, 2026 16:00 JST - November 16, 2026 15:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "1.5th Anniversary BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 pulls for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 pulls for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.4)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.
