Description]

The Competent Arrogant King

A prideful perfectionist. A high-spec human who will do what he said will do. Doesn't like or hate people and treat them straightforwardly though not a friendly person. Hates guts and wants logic. Takes care of management instead of the happy-go-lucky owner.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Main Office[2]: Ai Kosaka 👑 & Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Roka Suoh 🦎

While there is a relationship of trust, they are not "close friends," so private interactions are limited, and there are no department-wide drinking parties. Suoh also never suggests "let's get together just the department," so relationships between individuals are deeper than those within the department itself.

  • Unspoken Understanding[3]: Ai Kosaka 👑 & Yuzuru Kise 🍴

The leader who commands the entire Aporia operation and the right-hand man who supports him. Kise respects Kosaka, and while Kosaka never openly favors or coddles Kise, he never fails to show consideration and gratitude. They understand each other deeply, communicating effortlessly without needing many words.

  • Shukuo Law School[4]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Kou Ayato 🎞️

The two of them attended Shukuo Law School and were in the same seminar. Kosaka liked how Ayato was neither intimidated by him nor tried to flatter him, and this became the spark for their friendship. Later, Kosaka invited Ayato to join Aporia in order to establish the Simulation Dept.

  • Oil and Water[5]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

Shinkai, who treats everyone the same regardless of age or status, and Kosaka, who values respect from others, are highly incompatible. They tend to criticize nearly everything the other does, each believing the other is at fault. However, they do share a level of trust when it comes to their work.

  • New and Old Emperors[6]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Riku Tateshina 🥃

Kosaka, who consistently assumes the role of the top authority, seeks advice from the esteemed older Tateshina. He values Tateshina’s character and work ethic, and Tateshina, in turn, has a fondness for Kosaka. Even though they are not related by blood, they share a brotherly bond.
