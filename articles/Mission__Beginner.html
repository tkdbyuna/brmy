Mission/Beginner

MissionsBeginner MissionDaily MissionMain MissionAchievementSpecial MissionBremile MissionEvent Mission

Beginner Mission (ビギナーミッション, biginaa misshon?) is a mission for beginners that you can challenge after finishing the tutorial. By progressing through them, you’ll learn how to play BREAK MY CASE.

Missions are released sequentially at 4:00 AM JST daily login. There are a total of 45 missions spread over 5 days. Clear all missions to GET Crystals x500!

There is no time limit for clearing them; you can proceed at your own pace.

If you don’t know how to proceed, you can check the Help [?] button.

Each mission has a Help section; press the Help button to see detailed instructions.
