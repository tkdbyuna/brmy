Introducing the 21 SSR card stories featured in the 1st Anniversary BRMY Party.
A grand commission unfolds aboard a luxury cruise ship, with all staff members taking part—an operation of interference on behalf of others.
Enjoy the special stories of the heroine’s encounters with the staff during this mission.

Card Story Introduction
To prepare a gift for a client, Kosaka gives instructions to Aizawa. Hidden in his choice of person is Kosaka’s own sense of “consideration.”
Yashiro and Kise borrow Shido’s help to think about a certain object. In the process, Shido also notices the virtues that Kise possesses.
Suoh decides to go around expressing gratitude to the members of Aporia. Yashiro and Ukyo witness Suoh’s free‑spirited honesty firsthand.
Ayato ends up teaching Tsukimoto a method for memorizing maps. Ayato’s “department‑head style,” with keen observation of people and spaces, leaves an impression…
Ukyo goes out on deck looking for someone to take an unfinished cocktail. There he meets Arima, who notices Ukyo acting differently than usual…
Yashiro searches for a lost hairpin, and Hinomiya offers to help. As they scour the ship, Manami gets dragged into it too…?
Kanno becomes grumpy when his desired cake disappears. The one who shows up is Fushimi, who happened to have that cake…?
Tsukimoto and Hinomiya talk about “being relied upon.” When Hinomiya is hassled by a drunk, Tsukimoto shows his reliability.
On the night deck, Ichikawa gazes at the moon, and Kise shows warm kindness. The topic turns to “different kinds of kindness.”
While bird‑watching on deck, Okiya runs into Nina. From a conversation where neither can read the other’s true intent, Okiya seems to discover something…?
Fushimi is entrusted with a cocktail by Mikado. Seeing this, words from Kamiya about “the trust within Watchdog Dept.” make Fushimi reflect…
Ichikawa, seasick and unwell, is accompanied by Mikado. Though they seemed to have no connection, they bond over a conversation about Shinkai…?
Suoh, drunk before the sunset view, is cared for by Aizawa and Shinkai. Watching Suoh cheerfully praise the compatibility of Shinkai and Aizawa, Shinkai begins to ponder.
Aizawa proposes special training to Onda, whom he meets on board. When Onda shows reluctance, Aizawa suddenly challenges him with a “10‑times quiz”…?
Arima runs into Kanno, who is begging Tateshina for something. Arima slyly joins in, while also voicing his own evaluation of Tateshina…
Shido and Shinkai recall a day spent enjoying board games at Aporia. Each seems to have felt something about the other’s approach to strategy…
Tateshina steps in to help Ayato, who is being hit on at the bar. It seems Tateshina’s intervention in his junior’s troubles is an everyday occurrence…
Onda uses his athletic ability to collect decorations scattered on the deck. Watching this, Mikado senses Onda’s kindness…
Tateshina tries to express his gratitude to Nina, whom he rarely meets. But Nina is reluctant to accept it…?
Okiya watches from afar as Kamiya chats with Onda. Okiya seems interested in Kamiya’s impressions of Onda…?
Manami is reproached by Kosaka for his overbearing attitude. The source of his irritation seems to be Nina’s “unnecessary meddling”…?
