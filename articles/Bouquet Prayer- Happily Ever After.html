Overview

This time, Aporia has taken on a agency request to “find the real parents” of two people who are about to get married. However, a shocking truth comes to light... Aporia, on behalf of the public, offers their blessings. ── Kanno, Tateshina, and Onda are watching over the choices made for “happiness”.

Play Activity

Play a normal puzzle song to earn Pt Flower Shower and Flower Shower Medal. The medal will be used to enter the limited event puzzle to earn more Pt .

Pts' drop rate in normal puzzle using Stamina

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.
