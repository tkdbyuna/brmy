Characters June Bride 2025

Ai Kosaka 		File:Voice kosaka 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚は、相手に己の人生を賭ける覚悟ができた時にするものだろう
Marriage is something you do when you're ready to entrust your life to someone else.

Yuzuru Kise 		File:Voice kise 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 俺が幸せを祈る人達が、数多ある幸せになる手段から結婚を選んだなら、 目一杯祝福したいなって思っています。もう、呆れるくらいに
People I wish happiness for have many ways to be happy, and if they choose marriage as a way to that, then I'd want to congratulate them as much as I can. So much, they'd be annoyed.

Roka Suoh 		File:Voice suoh 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 みんなの嬉しそうな顔を見ていると、僕まで嬉しくなるよ
Looking at everyone's happy face makes me happy too.

Kou Ayato 		File:Voice ayato 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚って、ずっと貴方を愛し続けますっていう一種の契約だと思うけど、実際のところどうなんだろうね
I thought marriage is a form of a contract "to love you forever" though, I wonder how it actually works?

Mao Ukyo 		File:Voice ukyo 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚式って参列する側も結構お金かかるけど、大切な人の晴れ姿が見られるのは、あの場限りだって思うと別に惜しくないかな
Though guests also spend money to attend a wedding ceremony, thinking about how we can watch the people important to us being very happy limited to that special moment makes it quite worth it.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		File:Voice hinomiya 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 いろんなお客さんからプロポーズされてん。ちっちゃくちぎって明星くんのかけら～でみんな我慢してくれたらええんやけどね
I got proposed by various customers. It would've been nice if I could tear myself into tiny pieces, so everyone can hold off with a piece of Akehoshi-kun~

Yuragi Kanno 		File:Voice kanno 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 ジューンブライド‥‥‥もっと美味しそうな名前だったらいいのに
June Bride..... They should've named it with a more delicious-sounding name.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		File:Voice tsukimoto 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 この前、挙式後の新郎新婦を見かけたんですが、日差しが降り注ぐ中でのフラワーシャワーがドラマのようですごく綺麗でした
A while ago, I saw a bride and a groom after ceremony. The flower shower they did under the sunshine looked very beautiful, as if they were in a drama.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		File:Voice ichikawa 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 来世に期待。今世はゲームとおこげで満たされてます
Expect it in my next life. I already feel fulfilled with games and Okoge in this life.

Sei Okiya 		File:Voice okiya 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 女神の加護を得て幸せを誓おうという方々がみな、佳い日をお過ごしになられるとよいな、と
May everyone who wishes to receive blessing from the goddess and promise for happiness have a wonderful day

Shizuka Fushimi 		File:Voice fushimi 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 探してるうちは運命の相手に出逢えないって言うけど、元より望んでない場合も結局は出逢えないし。普通に齟齬だろ
They say you wont meet your soulmate while you're still searching, but you'll never meet someone if you don't want to anyway. That's just inconsistent.

Takeru Mikado 		File:Voice mikado 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 見た目も良くて親切な面子が揃ってるとは思うんだけど。『結婚』って言われたら正直、どう？　って人間が、うちには多くないかしら‥‥‥？
I do think we got many gentle faces with attractive appearances here, though. I guess there aren't many of them who think about "marriage"......?

Soyogu Shinkai 		File:Voice shinkai 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚がどうとか言うつもりはねえけど。幸せは伝播するから、そういう意味じゃ結婚式も悪くねえのかもな
I don't intend to say anything about marriage, but if it does spread happiness, then a wedding ceremony might not be that bad.

Shinobu Aizawa 		File:Voice aizawa 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚は楽しいことばかりじゃない、ってよく言うじゃないですか。そういうものに好きな人と挑んでいこうとするのは、かっこいい選択なのかも？
They often say marriage isn't always a happy thing, don't they? Choosing to challenge that with a person you like might be a cool choice to make?

Kiho Arima 		File:Voice arima 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 結婚について占うことも多いよ。それだけ普遍的なテーマで、容易に判断できるものでないと言えるんだろうね
I often do readings about marriage. I guess I'd say that it's a universal theme and not something that can be easily judged.

Kyoya Shido 		File:Voice shido 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 文化やその時の思想によって絵画の見方や価値が変わるように、結婚への考え方も環境や時代によって、 常に移り変わっていくものなのかもしれないね
Like how cultures and thoughts of that time can affect how someone see and value a painting, opinions about marriage might also be something that constantly change as they get affected by one's environment and era.

Riku Tateshina 		File:Voice tateshina 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 神社の前を通りがかったら結婚式してたみたいで、 綺麗だったよ
The shrine I passed by looked like it was having a wedding ceremony, it was beautiful.

Tomose Onda 		File:Voice onda 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 式の日に雨が降ると縁起が良い、という伝承があるらしい。もちろん鵜呑みにはしないが、それで前向きになれるなら悪くない話だ
There seems to be a legend that says rain on the wedding day means good luck. I surely won't take it at face value, but it's not that bad if it can make it more positive.

Yu Nina 		File:Voice nina 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 文化として理解できることと、自分事として受け入れるかはまた別の話だ。そこが重なる人間が、結婚という道を選ぶんだろう
Understanding it as a culture, and accepting it personally are two different things. People who have both of those overlap would choose marriage.

Kamiya 		File:Voice kamiya 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 赤の他人が家族になるって、なんかいいよね。それでいうと俺にとっての家族はAporiaって、思ってる！
Having a stranger become your family kinda sounds nice. Putting it like that, my family would be Aporia. Or that's what I think!

Urara Manami 		File:Voice manami 2-15 (vo home 96).mp3 この季節は雨ばっかだってのに、風習だかなんだか。結婚式が多いのも意味わかんねーな。大事な日なら尚更、晴れる可能性が高い日にすりゃいいだろ
It keeps raining in this season, but as a custom or whatever, there are still many wedding ceremonies, which I don't get why. If it's an important day, then they should hold it on a day where there's a high chance of it being sunny.
