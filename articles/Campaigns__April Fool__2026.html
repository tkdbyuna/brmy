Overview]

The hugely popular streamer demu’s official highlights channel has finally arrived!

To celebrate, a special campaign is being held in Break My Case, featuring limited-time stories (released at 18:00 JST!) and missions.

Don’t miss out — check them out alongside the highlight videos that will be updated throughout the day…

On the Home Screen, you can also enjoy exclusive April Fool Home Voices.
Try setting your favorite staff member on the Home Screen.

And more!

To make the most of today, turning ON push notifications from the app is highly recommended…
Please check your device settings regarding receiving push notifications from the app.
