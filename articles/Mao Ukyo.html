Description]

Systematic and cool-type brain
The brain of Simulation Dept. One of the highly educated in store and has a high IQ. Thinks systematically and sometimes gets surprised by the surroundings. Although it seems difficult to approach, he doesn't shy away from socializing. Aporia's valuable person with common sense. Often has fun talking about makeup with Mikado.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Simulation Dept.[2]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ & Mao Ukyo 🧩 & Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Yuragi Kanno 🍬

They have more opportunities to work together compared to other departments, and they socialize outside of work too, but the sense of unity and camaraderie within their department is weak. Plans for fun activities like "Let's do something with the four of us from the Simulation Dept." rarely materialize, but if the four of them do end up going out, they can still enjoy themselves.

  • Business Couple[3]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ and Mao Ukyo 🧩

To avoid trouble, these two have a contract to act as lovers when necessary. It began when Ayato, who has many problems with romantic entanglements, made a request of Ukyo, who accepted it for a fee. In reality, they are just friends: “partners” according to Ayato and “just putting up with each other” according to Ukyo.

The incident that led to this partnership is mentioned in Simulation Prequel.

  • Excursion Buddies[4]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Mao Ukyo 🧩 & Yuragi Kanno 🍬

Kise gets along equally well with everyone except Kosaka, but he is actually remarkably close to Ukyo and Kanno. On their days off, they go to buffets, go shopping, or spend time at the park. It's a peaceful relationship where they take turns fulfilling each other's wishes.

  • Cosmetic Dept.[5]: Mao Ukyo 🧩 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

The two share a keen interest in fashion and beauty, especially cosmetics. Due to the last train schedule, Ukyo, who can't make it home if he works until closing, usually stays at Mikado's place. Ayato sometimes joins them there.
