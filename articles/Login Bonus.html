※Notes: If you fail to log in for a consecutive day, the rewards aren't skipped over to the equivalent days, but are based on your login progress.

For example, you already logged in on day 1 and day 2, and collected the bonus of day 1 and day 2. However, on day 3, you can't log in, and you log in on the next day (day 4, counting from the campaign start date). Then, you will collect the day 3's bonus that hasn't been collected, and your bonus collecting progress is slower than the campaign period by one day.

Usually, even though the campaign ends, if the event, which the campaign belongs to, is still active, you can still collect the rest. In conclusion, you should be aware of the timeline and try to log in daily.

Login Story]

Login Story is available for certain campaigns for a limited time.

The story can only be accessed within the campaign; after that, it won't be republished.

Some of the events/campaigns have login stories:

  • Campaigns/April Fool/2025
  • 1st Anniversary -Knotting Point-
  • 2026 Happy New Year
