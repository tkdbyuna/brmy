Special Mission (スペシャルミッション, supesharu misshon?) is available for a limited time, usually during campaign periods and similar occasions. Some special occasions, events, and campaigns have special missions thus far.

Deparment Series]

Eve of the Event Campaign (前夜祭キャンペーン, Zen’yasai Kyanpēn?) is a fixed Special Campaign before the main department series' event begins.

This campaign happens for around 3 days and offers an increased item drop rate for all playlists.

The special mission comes along with this campaign and rewards 100 crystals and 10 new card enhancement items.

List of special missions is as follows, some songs are later used as the event-exclusive stages for the upcoming event.
