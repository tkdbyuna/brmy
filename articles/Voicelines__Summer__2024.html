Characters Summer 2024

Ai Kosaka 		誰に許可を取ってこんな気温にしてるんだ
Who gave permission to set the temperature like this?

Yuzuru Kise 		暑い日が続きますね‥‥‥空調などで気づく点があったら教えてください
The hot days continue.......Please tell me if you notice anything about the air conditioning.

Roka Suoh 		暑くて僕が溶けてしまっては世界の大損失だ‥‥‥ということで、こんな時は見る清涼・おシズの出番！　冷たい視線でヒンヤリさ！
Making it so hot that I'm melting is a huge loss for the world...... With that said, it's time for someone refreshing to appear—Oshizu! Let's feel cool using his cold gaze!

Kou Ayato 		あつー。ここ数年、夏か冬かしかなくない？
So hot. Don't you feel like it's either summer or winter these years?

Mao Ukyo 		涼しそうに見える？　いや、普通に暑いから。そうだ、前気になってるって言ってたかき氷でも食べに行く？
I look refreshed? No, it's hot for me too. Right, should we go eat the shaved ice you said you were curious to try?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		毎日、暑すぎて溶けそうやない？
Don't you feel like melting since every day is so hot?

Yuragi Kanno 		暑い、最悪。気温上げて良いなんて許可してない
Hot. It's the worst. I didn't give permission for the temperature to be raised.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		今月も電気代高いな‥‥‥。まあ、冷房に加えてＰＣ機材も多いから仕方ないか‥‥‥
The electricity bill for this month is expensive again...... Well, we have lots of PC equipment in addition to cooling so it can't be helped...

Haruhi Ichikawa 		あづい‥‥‥溶ける‥‥‥いやもう溶けてるといっても過言じゃない‥‥‥うー
Hot... I'm melting... Saying that I'm melting isn't an exaggeration anymore....... Ugh.

Sei Okiya 		なんといっても夏ですからね、少しくらい開放的になっても良いかもしれません
No matter what we say it's summer, being a little bit more open-minded would be nice.

Shizuka Fushimi 		新開のトレーニング、参加してるんでしょ？　水分はしっかり摂って。ただでさえ暑いんだし
You're participating in Shinkai's training, right? Make sure you stay hydrated since it's hot.

Takeru Mikado 		アッツ‥‥‥アンタ大丈夫？　この日差しは将来の自分の負債よ、お肌にしっかり現れるの。日陰行きましょ
So hot...... Are you okay? This sunlight will become a future liability by appearing on our skin. Let's go somewhere with a shade.

Soyogu Shinkai 		おい、へばんなら冷房つけた室内にしとけ。そのままでいんなら、問答無用で持ち上げんぞ
Oi, if you're unsure then go back to an air conditioned room. If you stay still then I'll lift you up with no questions asked.

Shinobu Aizawa 		夏と言ったらアツい、アツいと言ったらグッチの胸板！
If it's summer then it's hot, if it's hot then it's Gucchi's chest!

Kiho Arima 		暑いなら、何か涼しくなる話でもしようか
Why don't we talk about something refreshing since it's hot?

Kyoya Shido 		夏は暑くて敵わないね。極端に車を使う頻度が増えてしまうよ
Summer is too hot for me. I end up using my car more frequently.

Riku Tateshina 		暑いね～。あ、海へのお誘いなら、２週間くらいくれる？　仕上げてくるから
It's hot~ Ah, if you're inviting me to the beach then can you give me 2 weeks? I'll go finish up stuff.

Tomose Onda 		暑そうだな、俺のカゲで涼んでおけ
It seems hot, go cool down using my shadow.

Yu Nina 		ここの土地は湿気がひどい
The land here is extremely humid.

Kamiya 		あっづい‥‥‥。アイスでも買いにいこうかな‥‥‥。代理も何かいる？
Sooo hot....... I probably should go buy some ice cream....... Do you need some too, deputy?

Urara Manami 		熱帯夜の掃除とか地獄か
Cleaning up on a tropical night[Note 1] is hell!
