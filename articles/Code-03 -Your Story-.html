Code:03 -Your Story- is a playable puzzle song in BREAK MY CASE. It's Takeru Mikado's second personal character song sung by his voice actor, Yuki Ono, and composed by Akira Takeuchi. The song is featured on the BREAK MY CASE Personal Song Compilation Vol.5.


To play the second solos in-game, you need to finish reading Interlude I Episode 1 and have at least 10,000 NICEs for the respective character. To unlock the Jukebox, the puzzle score is at least SS on any side.
