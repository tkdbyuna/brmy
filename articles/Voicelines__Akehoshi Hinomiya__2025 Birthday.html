Characters Akehoshi Hinomiya's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		樋宮はいつも一言二言多いが‥‥‥今日は大目に見る、なんてことは特にない
Hinomiya always say extra things but...... I'll overlook it today, is clearly something I won't be saying.

Yuzuru Kise 		明星は‥‥‥人を巻き込んでいくのが上手くて、真似しようと思ってもできない勢いと雰囲気がありますよね
Akehoshi is....... skilled at involving people, even if I want to imitate him, there are these energy and vibes I can't do.

Roka Suoh 		あっけーは魅惑のボーイだね！　大人な色気も子どもの純粋さも兼ね備えている。今までもこれからも、楽しみだよッ！
Akke is a charming boy! He has both the sexiness of an adult and the purity of a child. I look forward to what will come from now on!

Kou Ayato 		明星の誕生日か。プレゼント、どうするかなあ‥‥‥あいつって何でも持ってそう、つか何でも“貰ってそう”で迷うんだよな
Akehoshi's birthday. What should I do for the present......? He seems to have everything or more like "be given anything", it's hard to choose.

Mao Ukyo 		明星の誕生日か‥‥‥プレゼントするならなんだろう、少しでも地に足つくようにオモシとか？
Akehoshi's birthday...... What kind of present should I give him? Some weights so he can be a bit down-to-earth?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		誕生日って、自分より他所の方が気合い入れてるとこあらへん？　嬉しいんやけど、壮大すぎると他人事に見えてくるんよなあ
Don't other people get more excited than us on our birthdays? I'm glad they do, but it seems to become someone else's when it gets too grandiose.

Yuragi Kanno 		明星なら、自分の誕生日のアピールは自分でするし、こっちが祝いに行かなくても自分からくるでしょ
Akehoshi would be promoting his birthday on his own, even if we don't go to celebrate him, he'll start on his own.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		なにか菓子でもやくか。揺がいうには、貰っても食べられるものが限られるっていうしな‥‥‥。想像するのはやめておくか
I should probably bake some sweets, since Yuragi told me what he can eat from what's given are limited...... I should stop imagining things.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		樋宮くんてアレでしょ、やばい人ホイホイっていう。類友っていうし、本人もある意味ぶっ飛んでる説
Hinomiya-kun is that, right? A thoughtlessly dangerous guy. They say birds of a feather flock together, and the person himself is strange in a way, anyway.

Sei Okiya 		明星くんは‥‥‥予想のつかない行動が面白い。人が想像する域をゆうに超える彼が、私は好きですよ
Akehoshi-kun's...... unexpected behavior is interesting. I like how he is beyond people's imagination.

Shizuka Fushimi 		強行部の俺から見ても危なっかしいやつって印象かな、寮でもよく環野とバタバタやってるし
As someone from Watchdog Dept., I guess he is someone reckless. I often see him being hectic at the dorm alongside Kanno.

Takeru Mikado 		お誕生日のお祝いも兼ねて、ミカ特製カクテルをひとつ伝授してあげるわ。教えるからには、マスターしてもらうわよ～？
To celebrate your birthday, I shall teach you how to make one of Mika's special cocktails. Since I'm teaching you, I'll make sure you master it, okay~?

Soyogu Shinkai 		もし今日樋宮の部屋が騒がしくても、大目に見てやるか。誕生日だもんな
I guess I should overlook how noisy Hinomiya's room might be for today. It's his birthday after all.

Shinobu Aizawa 		あっけーおめでと！　また寮で飲み会あったら誘ってよ、秒で駆けつけるし
Akke, congrats! Invite me whenever you hold a dorm drinking party, I'll be there in a second.

Kiho Arima 		人を狂わす才能を持っている人っていうのは、一定数いるよね。経験談だし、悪いことではないよ。使い所の問題かな
There's only a certain number of people who have the talent of driving people crazy. It's based from experience, so there's nothing bad about it. I guess the problem is where it's being used.

Kyoya Shido 		明星くんの誕生日だね。彼は欲しいものをしっかり表現できるタイプだし、今頃両手がいっぱいなんじゃないかな
It's Akehoshi-kun's birthday. He's the type to properly express what he wants, so I think his hands should be full by now.

Riku Tateshina 		明星の誕生日か。あいつ、プレゼントは君で、とか平気で言いそうで怖い。いやむしろもう言ってる説あるな
Akehoshi's birthday. It's scary how he would casually say that he wants you as his present. No, he might've already said that to you.

Tomose Onda 		樋宮はどんなときも気さくに話しかけてくる。そういうやつは珍しい
Hinomiya always talks to me in a friendly manner. That kind of guy is rare.

Yu Nina 		樋宮は手の抜きどころがうまい。‥‥‥褒められないこともあるが、社会で生きる人間には重要な要素だ
Hinomiya is good at cutting corners....... It might not be something praiseworthy, but that's something people who live in the society need.

Kamiya 		本日の主役、明星くん！　どっかご飯行こって誘おうと思ったけど、今日は忙しそうだから我慢する。その代わり、今度絶対ね
Today's main star, Akehoshi-kun! I was thinking of inviting you to eat somewhere, but you seem to be busy today so I'll hold back. In exchange, I'll absolutely do it next time.

Urara Manami 		あいつにとって誕生日って平和な日なのか？　怪しくね？
Is birthday a peaceful day for him? Isn't it suspicious?
