Characters Akehoshi Hinomiya's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		明星にお祝いしてほしい、って言われてて。‥‥‥もちろん そのつもりだったけど、改めて本人から言われるとちょっと張り切っちゃいますね
I'm impressed with the number of requests Hinomiya handles. ......Let me say this though, don't be complacent just because I praised you a bit.

Yuzuru Kise 		樋宮のこなす依頼量には感心している。‥‥‥言っておくが、少し褒めたからといって慢心はするな
Akehoshi told me that he wants me to celebrate him. Of course, I plan to do that, but hearing it from the person himself makes me a little more excited.

Roka Suoh 		あっけーのおうちの家電は大丈夫かな？せめて、今日くらいは何事もなく動いていることを願うよ
Are the home appliances at Akke's place fine? I hope they can work fine for today at least.

Kou Ayato 		はい、防犯プサー。お前も大概危ないんだから持ってな、8ヶ月越しで俺とお揃い探すの苦労したから大事にして
Here you go, a safety alarm. It's mostly dangerous for you, too, so bring it. I struggled finding the matching one with mine after 8 months, you'd better use it with care.

Mao Ukyo 		欲しいものドライヤーでいいの？明星にとっては消耗品だと思うんだけど
Do you really want a hair dryer? I think it'll be considered a consumable for you, though, Akehoshi.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		代理さんもお祝いありがとう～。プレゼントは物とかやなくて、人気者の代理さんとふたりきりでご飯とか行けたら嬉しいんやけど、どうかな？
Thank you for celebrating me too, Deputy-san~ I'd be happy if your gift for me isn't a thing, but a chance to go for a meal with the famous Deputy-san alone, how about it?

Yuragi Kanno 		これ以上、明星に電化製品壊されたら嫌だから、今日は俺がボタン押したらすごいお礼言われた。これくらい、いつでもやるのに
I don't want Akehoshi to break anymore electric appliances, so I usually press the button, yet he thanked me a lot today. Even though I always do this much.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		揺からよく樋宮くんのことを聞くんだが‥‥‥普段どういう生活をしているのか、絶妙に想像できないんだよな
I often hear about Hinomiya-kun from Yuragi, but.....I can't exactly imagine what kind of daily life he has.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		何かあげるとかそういうのなさすぎて。てか最近の陽キャは何が好きなわけ？
I really don't have any idea of what to give him. I mean, what do recent extroverts like again?

Sei Okiya 		明星くんの魅力は底知れませんね。今年もたくさんの贈り物と思いを受け取るのでしよう
Akehoshi-kun's charm is bottomless, I believe he'll receive a lot of gifts and thoughts this year too.

Shizuka Fushimi 		人の印象はそうそう変わらないって、樋宮を見るたびに思うよ。それがいいか悪いかって話じゃなくて
Every time I look at Hinomiya, I think about how our impression of someone doesn't really change. This is not about whether it's good or bad.

Takeru Mikado 		飲みたいものなんでも言いなさい。コークハイもカクテルも、とっても美味しく作ってあげる
Tell me whatever you want to drink. Coke highballs, cocktails, I'll make any of them nicely.

Soyogu Shinkai 		次の寮飲みの買い出しは、好きなもんカゴに入れろ。度が過きない範囲で奢る
Put anything you like into the cart at the next drinking party's shopping. I'll treat you to a reasonable amount.

Shinobu Aizawa 		あっけーおめ！プレゼント、先に選んじゃおうかなって思ったんだけど、やつば一緒に選びに行こ！そっちのが絶対楽しい！
Akke, 'grats! I was thinking of choosing your gift on my own, but let's go together instead! I'm sure it'll be more fun that way!

Kiho Arima 		明星は毎年誕生日になるとたくさんのプレゼントを抱えてるね。Aporiaのみんなに愛されてる証拠だ。なんだか俺まで嬉しいよ
Akehoshi carries a lot of presents when his birthday comes every year. It's the proof of how beloved he is by everyone in Aporia. It makes me glad too somehow.

Kyoya Shido 		明星くんは今ごろプレゼントに埋もれてるのかな。毎年たくさん祝われるって話が俺の耳まで届いてるから、そんな想像を勝手にしてるんだけど
I wonder if Akehoshi-kun is buried in presents right now? The story about how he gets celebrated a lot every year has reached my ears, so I imagined that kind of thing on my own.

Riku Tateshina 		もう俺があげなくてもいいでしょってくらいプレゼントの箱を抱えてたよ。それでも「ちょうだい」って言ってくるあたりが、明星らしいね
He was carrying a box filled with so many presents that I don't even need to give him anymore. Still, Akehoshi tells me to give him something, and that's very him.

Tomose Onda 		樋宮が随分とプレゼントを抱えていたから、一部運ふのを手伝った。人の気を引くというのも大変だな
Hinomiya was carrying a lot of presents, so I helped him by carrying some of them. It must be hard to be getting people's attention.

Yu Nina 		時間があるなら、この前聞いてきたレシピを教えようかと思ったが、今日は忙しそうだな。またタイミングを見て声をかける
I was thinking of teaching him the recipe he asked about when he has time, but he seems busy today. I should find another time to call him.

Kamiya 		明星くんといるといつも色んな刺激をもらえてる。本当にありがと！俺からも何か与えられてるといいんだけど
Being with Akehoshi-kun always stimulates me in many different ways. Thank you very much! I'd be glad if you've been getting something from me too.

Urara Manami 		お前の誕生日だからどうしたんだよ、それで飲み参加するわけがねえ。浮かれんなアホ
So what if it's your birthday? There's no way I'm participating in your drinking party. Don't get carried away, stupid.
