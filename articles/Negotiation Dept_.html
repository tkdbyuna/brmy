Negotiation Department (交渉部, koushou-bu), often shortened to Negotiation Dept., is the department in charge of negotiations. They act on behalf of the Simulation Department on cases they cannot undertake due to difficulty, danger, and aptitude. They are also in charge of Human Resources work, including how to handle problems that arise in Aporia’s business and recruitment. In addition, it is also their job to review new clients or agency services.
