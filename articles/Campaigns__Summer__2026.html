To welcome the summer with excitement, (うきうき夏到来! キャンペーン, Ukiuki Natsu Tōrai! Kyanpēn?, lit. Cheerful Summer Arrives! Campaign) is underway. This campaign features:

  • Special Login Bonus: log in within the campaign period to get Stamina Charge [10] x1
  • ONE FREE Gacha Pull every day (Total 3 pulls)
  • Special Mission: Completing all missions will reward you with 100 crystals and summery items useful for strengthening cards.

Also, new merch/goods for "Cheerful Marine Fair" are available at the same time.
