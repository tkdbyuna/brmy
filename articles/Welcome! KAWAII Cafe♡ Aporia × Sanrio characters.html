Play Activity]

In the cafe-bar event, players will make a drink using the materials collected in the puzzle and earn sales in 😊.

Drinks can be made from the MAKING button on the event's top screen.

When they are completed, they will be lined up at the counter, so tap or swipe to serve the drink.

When 😊 accumulates, players can release the event story and earn rewards such as event-limited rewards cards and Gacha tickets.

In addition, players can earn more materials than usual in event-limited playlists, as well as special materials related to this event. Players can also use the background play function in event-limited playlists.

During the event period, staff will take turns in charge of each shift. Achieve sales targets for each shift and total sales targets to earn a variety of rewards.

Note:

  • In the cafe-bar event, players can play event-limited playlists by using regular stamina instead of event medals.
  • There is no event Pt or event Pt exchange in this event. In addition, there is no total high score ranking or event Pt ranking.

SPECIAL TIME]

During the newly introduced SPECIAL TIME in this event, Sanrio Characters will assist in the making process. During SPECIAL TIME, the making time is halved! SPECIAL TIME occurs daily in the timeframe 12:00 - 13:00 JST and 20:00 - 21:00 JST.
