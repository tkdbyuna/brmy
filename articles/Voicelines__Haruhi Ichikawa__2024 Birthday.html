Characters Haruhi Ichikawa's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		壱川の姿はほとんど見ないな、深夜に麻波と遭遇する確率のほうが高いか‥‥‥？　仕事さえしてくれれば、それでいい
I rarely see Ichikawa; I guess encountering Manami late at night is more likely...... As long as he's working, that's fine.

Yuzuru Kise 		この前車で送って行ったら、いつの間にか後部座席で眠ってて。安心できる運転ができてたならよかったです
Before, when I drove him home, he suddenly fell asleep in the backseat. I'm glad I was able to drive reassuringly.

Roka Suoh 		るんるん、顔色がとてもいいね！　僕も君の健康を祈っているよ、何をしたら良いかな？　‥‥‥放っておいてほしい？　ぴえん
Runrun, your complexion looks great today! I also pray for your health—what should I do? ...You want me to leave you alone? Pien.

Kou Ayato 		本人とはあんま接点ないんだけど、クリエイティブなことができるってモテそうじゃない？　今時って感じだよね
I don't contact much with him, but won't he become popular since he's working in the creative area? It feels very of-the-times.

Mao Ukyo 		壱川くんの誕生日か。管理部って、あの仲間内で誕生日パーティとかあるのかな？　全く想像ついてないんだけど
Ichikawa-kun's birthday, huh. Does the Administrative Dept. hold birthday parties among themselves? I can't imagine it.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		春日さんの誕生日か‥‥‥ちょっとしたものでも高価なもん持っとるし、プレゼント悩まへん？
Haruhi-san's birthday...... Even small items of his are expensive, so isn't it hard to decide on a gift?

Yuragi Kanno 		春日、おめでとう。今日はおこげだけじゃなくて春日にも会いにきた。日頃のお礼、恋に持たされたやつだけど
Haruhi, congrats. I came to meet not only Okoge but Haruhi too today. As a daily gratitude, I brought the things Kou told me to bring.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		お前の怠惰な生活を許すのは今日だけだからな。明日からはまた朝、ちゃんと起こしにいく
I'm only forgiving your lazy lifestyle today. I'll properly wake you up again starting tomorrow.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		大河も誓も張り切りすぎてて、すでになんもしてない俺が疲れてる‥‥‥なんていうかすごい"家"って感じ。別にいいのに
Taiga and Sei are so excited that it makes me—who hasn't done anything—already feel tired...... It kind of feels like an amazing "home". It's fine, though.

Sei Okiya 		春日の誕生日だからと、大河が朝から張り切っています。ふふふ、微笑ましい‥‥‥と、いけませんね、私も準備しなくては
Taiga has been excited since the morning because it's Haruhi's birthday. Fufufu, how pleasant...... Oh, I must prepare things too.

Shizuka Fushimi 		新開って管理部のハウスに出入りしてるのに、よく壱川にトレーニングを仕掛けないな。いや、敢えてか
Shinkai often goes to the Administrative Dept.'s House, but he doesn't set up Ichikawa to train? No, does he dare to?

Takeru Mikado 		春日には、なかなか会えないのよねえ。誓が『酔うと可愛らしくなる』って言ってたけど、本当かしら？　見てみたいわ
I can't really meet Haruhi. Sei said that "he becomes cute when he's drunk," though—is it true? I want to see it.

Soyogu Shinkai 		ここまでくると腐れ縁だからな。変わったところも変わらないところも、壱川の良さだ
We're in an inseparable relationship at this point. Ichikawa's nice points are that he changes yet doesn't.

Shinobu Aizawa 		DTPデザインとか興味あるんで、壱川さんとそういう話してみたいんですよね
I'm interested in DTP[Note 1] designs, so I want to try talking to Ichikawa-san about that.

Kiho Arima 		本人は社会に出たくないだろうけど、ホストになったら売れるタイプなんじゃないかな
The person might not want to enter society, but I think he'd be a well-selling host.

Kyoya Shido 		生徒が話していたけど、demuの誕生日は今日じゃなくて来週なんだって？　身バレ対策、相変わらず徹底してるね。感心だな
I heard my students said that demu's[Note 2] birthday isn't today, but next week? His measures to avoid getting doxxed are thorough as ever. I'm impressed.

Riku Tateshina 		春日の誕生日ってことは、今日はハウスはパーティかな。いいねえ、あったかい
Haruhi's birthday means the house is having a party today. How nice, it feels warm.

Tomose Onda 		壱川にFPSはうまいのかと聞かれたが現実とゲームでは勝手が違うだろうからな‥‥‥
Ichikawa asked me whether I'm good at FPS, but reality and games are incomparable...

Yu Nina 		クリエイティブなやつだと聞いている
I heard he's a creative person.

Kamiya 		壱川さん超レアキャラだから、どう祝うかな‥‥‥いきなりハウスにプレゼント届けに行くのは、さすがに距離間バグりすぎか
Ichikawa-san is a super rare character so how should I celebrate...... Suddenly delivering a gift to the House would be very weird for me, who isn't that close to him.

Urara Manami 		インドアなのに派手だよな。矛盾してね？
He's an indoor type but flashy. Isn't that inconsistent?
