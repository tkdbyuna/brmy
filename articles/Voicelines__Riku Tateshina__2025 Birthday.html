Characters Riku Tateshina's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		誕生日おめでとうございます。気にかけてくださるのは嬉しいですが、俺は貴方の身も心配ですよ
Happy birthday. I'm glad that you look out for me, but I'm also worried about your body.

Yuzuru Kise 		吏来さんは俺が支えたい人を、俺とは違った思いで一緒に支えてくれる方で。本当にいつも頼もしく思っています。お誕生日、おめでとうございます
Riku-san is someone who supports the person I want to support together with me with a different thought. You're always very reliable. Happy birthday.

Roka Suoh 		特大の気持ちを込めて、誕生日おめでとう！　吏来の優しさをいつも受け取っている分、今日はいっぱいお返しするよ！
Putting in some extra large feelings of mine, and happy birthday! I shall return as much as the kindness l've received from Riku today!

Kou Ayato 		祝われる側なはずなのに、芦佳さんの勢いでお疲れ気味なのさすが吏来さんって感じ。祝われる側とは思えない気遣いよう
Riku-san is supposed to be the one who gets celebrated, but the way he becomes tired because of Roka-san's vigor is very like him. He's so considerate that you wouldn't think that it's actually his celebration.

Mao Ukyo 		それとなく吏来さんの欲しいものを探ろうとしたら、すぐに何かに気づいたみたいで。あれは敢えて気づいてないフリしてる顔だった
Riku-san seems to quickly noticed that I was trying to find out what he wanted, even though I was doing it subtly. I dare say that he was acting as if he didn't notice anything afterwards.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		吏来さんが貰ってたプレゼント、俺も欲しかったやつやから思わずええな〜って言うたら、「これはだめ」って。吏来さんがダメって言うの、珍しない？
A present Riku-san received was something I wanted, so I unconsciously said "how nice~," then he replied with "this one is a no-go." Isn't it rare for Riku-san to say no?

Yuragi Kanno 		吏来さんにはいつも差し入れもらってるから、今日は俺からお返し。恋と一緒に選んだから、間違いないはず
l'm giving some return for today since I often receive sweets from Riku. I picked them out with Kou, so they shouldn't be wrong.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		立科さんを見てると、ただ年を重ねるだけじゃ“ああ”はなれないよなって思います。俺みたいな人間には、なろうと思ってもなれそうにないです
Seeing Tateshina-san makes me think that simply aging won't make someone become "like that." Someone like me couldn't possibly become like him even if I want to.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		今年も絶好調に写真ブレてると聞いて。どうぞ、ジンバルです
I heard that the pictures you take are blurred at their finest too this year. Here, a gimbal.

Sei Okiya 		Aporiaでは長い付き合いになりますが、貴方が慕われている姿を見るたび、胸が温かくなります。私も、みなさんのチャッタスのお祝いに参加しておきましょう
We've been together in Aporia for a long time, but seeing you being adored always makes me feel warm. I shall join everyone's celebration in Chattask.

Shizuka Fushimi 		芦佳さんがチャッタスで祝ってたから便乗しておいた。他のメンツの反応見ても、立科さんの人柄がよくわかるよ
I just took advantage of Roka-san's celebration in Chattask and joined it. Looking at others' reactions makes me understand what kind of person Tateshina-san is.

Takeru Mikado 		吏来のことお祝いするのも、もう何回目？　来年もお祝いするつもりだから、元気で１年過ごしなさいよ
How many times live celebrated Riku already? I plan to celebrate you again next year, so spend this one year cheerfully, okay?

Soyogu Shinkai 		なんつーか、隙があるように見えてやっぱ大人だよなって思うことばっかな気がする。俺は吏来さんこそ、舐めてかかっちゃいけない人だと思いますけどね
How do I say it, I feel like I often think how he is a mature adult even though he seems to have weaknesses. I think Riku-san himself is someone who shouldn't be underestimated though.

Shinobu Aizawa 		お誕生日おめでとうございます。吏来さんといえば包容力！　かっこいい！　大人の男！　にゃはは、いつも褒めてもらってるんで、今日は俺から
Happy birthday. If I have to describe Riku-san, then tolerant! Awesome! A mature man! Nyahaha, you always praise me so I should be the one doing it today.

Kiho Arima 		吏来には本当に世話になっているからね、『なんでもするよ』って言葉すら薄いくらいだな。最善を尽くしてお祝いするね
Since I've really been in Riku's care, the phrase "l'll do anything" pretty much means nothing. I'll be doing my best to celebrate you.

Kyoya Shido 		吏来さんの誕生日ということは、飲みにいけるというわけですね。もちろん俺の奢りで。先約がある場合は諦めますが、どうですか？
Riku-san's birthday means we can go drinking together. Of course, I'll be the one paying. I'll step back if you have prior engagement though, how is it?

Riku Tateshina 		いつの間にかまた誕生日を迎えちゃって。１年って早いな。‥‥‥ってこれすごくジジくさくない？　危ない危ない
My birthday came again before I knew it. A year passes by so fast. ......Ah, did I just sound like an old man? That was dangerous.

Tomose Onda 		吏来、誕生日おめでとう。いい１年を。
‥‥‥俺を好きに？　そうか。なるな
Riku, happy birthday. May you have a nice year.
......You might like me? I see. Don't.

Yu Nina 		吏来は色々なやつに親しまれているようだな。先日、綾戸が吏来の写真をみて息切れするまで笑っていた
Riku seems to be familiar among many kinds of people. The other day, I saw Ayato laughing at Riku's pictures until he ran out of breath.

Kamiya 		吏来さんってセンスいいから、何をあげるか迷うな。でもきっとあの大人の余裕で何あげても喜んでくれる気がする！
I'm at loss on what to give to Riku-san since he has a nice sense, but I feel like he'll gladly accept anything with that adult composure of his!

Urara Manami 		いつまで浮ついてるつもりだよ。今だにそうなら、もう、死ぬまでか？
Until when are you going to be frivolous? If not now, is it until death?
