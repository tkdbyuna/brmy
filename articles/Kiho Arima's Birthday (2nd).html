Kiho-san's dignified appearance must've been earned from the experience that I don't have. I'm always grateful for your help, including things with Roka. Thank you very much.

Yuzuru Kise 		樹帆さんが淹れるココアには到底敵わないとわかってるんですけど、いつもたくさんお世話になっているので、俺にぜひ淹れさせてください
I know that mine is no match against the cocoa Kiho-san make, but live always been in your care, so please let me make some for you.

Roka Suoh 		樹帆と過ごしたこれまでが、僕にとってはかけがえのない時間だよ。同じように思ってもらえてたら、これ以上に嬉しいことはないね！
The time I've spent with Kiho up until now is something irreplaceable for me. There's nothing that can make me feel happier than if you think the same as me!

Kou Ayato 		樹帆さん相手のプレゼント選び、物凄く緊張するんだけど。聞きたい気持ちありつつ、聞いたら絶対何もいらないと言われるのもわかってるから聞けないよね
Choosing a gift for Kiho-san is very nerve-racking. Even if we want to ask him about it, we know that he'll surely say he doesn't need anything, so we can't actually ask him.

Fortune-telling requires not only spiritual power, but also the skill to see someone's true nature to be good in it. Putting it like that, I get why people get nervous when Kiho-san looks at them.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		樹帆さんにお酒だすのドキドキするんよねえ。でも色々コメントもらえて助かるから、今度また俺の作ったお酒飲んでほしいなあ
I always get nervous when I serve a drink for Kiho-san. But getting commented on it helps me, so I want him to have the drink I made again sometime.

Yuragi Kanno 		樹帆が今日は俺にいい事が起きる日って言ってた。もしあたりのお菓子が出たら、樹帆にも分けるつもり
Kiho said that something good will happen to me today. If I get a winning sweet, I plan to share it with Kiho.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		在間さんのような方に喜んでもらえるお祝いができるか‥‥‥？　いや、俺が勝手に気後れしてるだけなんだが‥‥‥
Can I do a celebration that pleases someone like Arima-san......? No, it's just me getting intimidated on my own......

Victor Hugo said, "There is one spectacle grander than the sea, that is the sky; there is one spectacle grander than the sky, that is the interior of the soul". May good luck come to you who lead something magnificent.

Shizuka Fushimi 		部長会の後はほっといてもバーで飲むことになるけど、今日の名目は在間さんのお祝いになりそうだな

Takeru Mikado 		占いで、『今日のラッキードリンク』とかわかったりしない？　それで乾杯しましょうよ
Can't you tell something like "today's lucky drink" through fortune-telling? Let's have a toast with that.

Soyogu Shinkai 		樹帆さんみたいな人になるのに、どう年を重ねたらいいのかマジで謎だよな‥‥‥。いや、俺がなりたいってわけじゃねえけど。もちろん、なりたくないってわけでもねえ
How someone get older to become like Kiho-san is genuinely a mystery.......No, it's not like I want to become like that but of course, it doesn't exactly mean that I don't want to.

Shinobu Aizawa 		樹帆さんにおめでとうって伝えてきたけど、ほんとに１歳増えた‥‥‥？　毎年同じこと思って、結果永遠に変わらないってなりそう
I went to congratulate Kiho-san though, did he really become a year older......? He might stay like that forever since I keep thinking about the same thing every year.

Kiho Arima 		昔はかなり派手に祝ってもらったけど、気の許せる人達と過ごすのも悪くないね。君っていう新しい仲間も増えたしね
I used to get celebrated in a grand manner a long time ago, but spending it with trustworthy people isn't that bad either. l've gained you as a new comrade after all.

Kyoya Shido 		おめでとうございます。今年は、ココアを飲むのに良さそうなマグにしました。樹帆さんが普段使いしているブランドを中心に数点手配したので、気に入ったものを使ってください
Congratulations. I picked some mugs that can be used to drink cocoa nicely for this year. I've arranged a few of them mainly from the brand Kiho-san usually use. Please use the ones you like.

Riku Tateshina 		誕生日おめでとう。今年はどんなお祝いがいい？　いつも頼もしい樹帆さんへの感謝をこめて、俺ができる限り叶えますよ
Happy birthday. What kind of celebration do you want this year? I'll do my best to make it happen to thank the always reliable Kiho-san.

Tomose Onda 		樹帆は年相応ではないな。‥‥‥ああ、もちろん芦佳とは異なる意味でだ
Kiho doesn't seem to be his age...... Yeah, of course, it's in a different meaning to Roka.

Yu Nina 		賄いを出した時、思ったよりよく食べると感じた。それで、自分が先入観を持っていたことに気付かされた
I felt that he enjoyed the staff meal I served more than I expected. It made me realize that I had a preconception.

Kamiya 		樹帆さん宛に、ココア味のドーナツを用意してみたんだけど、気に入ってもらえるかな。いっそ俺がココアを樹帆さんに淹れるのもアリか‥‥‥？
