ST Department (特務部, tokumu-bu), often shortened to ST Dept., is the department in charge of special affairs. They are the personal security department. They respond to clients who cannot be contracted by the Watchdog Department due to difficulty, danger, or aptitude; those who are not here for regular services. Like the Watchdog Department, they handle capture, recapture, and response.
Other than the agency, it operates mainly as a backside- usually cooking or cleaning.
