Characters Mao Ukyo's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		交際部の秩序は宇京次第だと聞く。いつも良くやってくれている
I heard the Simulation Dept.'s order depends on Ukyo. He's always maintaining it well.

Yuzuru Kise 		次のおでかけは真央の誕生日として、揺のおすすめスポットでお祝いしたいって。行き先は秘密らしいけど‥‥‥毛がついても大丈夫な服がいいかもね
Yuragi said our next hangout will be at his recommended spot to celebrate Mao's birthday, The destination seems to be a secret I should wear something I wouldnt mind getting fur on.

Roka Suoh 		まおち、誕生日おめでとう！　面白そうなボードゲームを見つけたから、スタッフルームに置いておくね！　また今度、激戦を交えようじゃないかっ！
Maochi, happy birthday! I found an interesting board game, so I'll leave it in the staff room! We shall have a fierce battle at another time!

Kou Ayato 		真央の誕生日になると本人じゃなくて俺が真央ってこれ持ってる？　って聞かれるのちょっと面白いんだよな。まあおかげさまで今年も、誰とも被らないもの買えたから期待していいよ
It's funny how people start asking me whether Mao has certain things or not instead of asking Mao himself for his birthday. Well, I managed to prepare something different from theirs thanks to that, so look forward to it.

Mao Ukyo 		お祝いありがとう。そこまで真剣に祝ってもらわなくても‥‥‥って思う時があるけど、みんなの誕生日にも同じことしてるから、人のこと言えないか
Thank you for the celebration. You guys don't need to celebrate it this much...... was something I ever thought about, but I guess I can't really tell them off since I usually do the same.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		ハッピーバースデーまおち！　まおちのために激辛メニューを用意したんよ、喜んでもらえたら嬉しいわあ
Happy birthday, Maochi! I've prepared a super spicy menu for Maochi, I'd be happy if you gladly accept it!

Yuragi Kanno 		この間出かけた時に一緒に見たハンドクリーム、いい匂いだったから買ってみた。俺は使わないから、真央が使って
I tried buying the handcream we saw when we went out together last time since it smelled nice. I don't use it, so Mao should use it.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		宇京くんがいると場がよくまとまると聞く。何かコツがあるのか、今度聞いてみるか
The place becomes more organized when Ukyo-kun is present. I should try asking whether he has a trick for that.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		ボドゲとおしゃれ好き‥‥‥完全揺の受け売りだけど。最近流行ったボドゲでも渡しておいてもらうか
Likes board games and stylish things....... It's a second-hand opinion from Yuragi, though. I should probably give him the recently popular board game.

Sei Okiya 		冷静に判断ができる人間がいるというのは心強いものです。真央くんが交際部にいてくれる安心感は‥‥‥ふふ、私よりもみなさんの方が強く実感していそうです
It's reassuring to have someone who can calmly make a decision. The peace of mind of having Mao-kun in the Simulation Dept...... Fufu, everyone there should be feeling it better than me.

Shizuka Fushimi 		宇京の誕生日か。あまり絡んでないけど、相変わらず交際部の手綱をうまく握ってるみたいだね
Ukyo's birthday. I don't really interact with him, but he seems to be holding on the Simulation Dept.'s reins skillfully as always.

Takeru Mikado 		真央に関しては好きそうなものも似合うものも知りすぎてて、困るわ。次の休みはいつ？　お誕生日ショッピング、行きましょ！
I know too much about what you would like or what would suit you, Mao, it's troubling me. When's your next day off? Let's go for a birthday shopping!

Soyogu Shinkai 		これ、よかったら。正直何かもよくわかってねえんすけど、姉御に聞いたんでハズレはないと思います
Please take this if you don't mind. I honestly don't really understand what to give, but it shouldn't be a miss since I asked Anego.

Shinobu Aizawa 		まおちさん、誕生日祝いの手合わせどうです？　どのゲームでも本気でいきますよ！　そのほうが喜んでもらえるだろうなって
Maochi-san, how about a game to celebrate your birthday? I'll give my all for any games! I was thinking that you'll be glad that way.

Kiho Arima 		誕生日おめでとう。分け隔てなく平等な視点は、欲しいと願っても易々と手に入れられるものではないから、真央は変わらず真央でいてね
Happy birthday An equal perspective without discrimination is something people cant easily attain even if they desire it, may you keep being you, Mao.

Kyoya Shido 		真央くんの誕生日だってね、おめでとう。機会があったらボードゲームで手合わせでもと思うけど、さすがに真央くんに分がありすぎるかな

Why don't we go to a department store together next time? I think it's better for you to choose on your own for that kind of stuff.

Tomose Onda 		近くキッチンに入る。宇京に世話をかけないよう努めるが、よろしく頼む
I'll be in the kitchen soon, I'll leave it to you to try to not cause any trouble for Ukyo.

Yuzuru said the hall becomes stable when Ukyo is there. It also helps the kitchen staff.
