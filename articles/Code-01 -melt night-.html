Code:01 -melt night- is a playable puzzle song in BREAK MY CASE. It's Kiho Arima's second personal character song sung by his voice actor, Takuya Sato, and composed by Sondai Nishijima. The song is featured on the BREAK MY CASE Personal Song Compilation Vol.4..


To play the second solos in-game, you need to finish reading Interlude I and have at least 10,000 NICEs for the respective character. To unlock the Jukebox, the puzzle score is at least SS on any side.
