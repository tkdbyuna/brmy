Overview

.....I don’t know what to draw for a present.

The second round of trips for the production of “Memories” is with the Simulation Department. The usual theme is, “Please go and experience painting on tea bowls to give to each other as gifts.” When the four of them gather, they should be able to enjoy the trip, but for some reason, Kanno is overly attached to Ayato......?

Play Activity

Using normal stamina, play puzzles to earn Event Pt Wagashi and Travel Coins (in short trv.coins). to exchange rewards. Event Pts can be earned in the playlists for targeted staff and targeted attributes (See more in Event Bonus) throughout the event, while trv.coins can only be earned in the second half of the event and from the event-exclusive stage.

During the first half of the event, only the new song stages are playable. The Another Story stages released during the second half can be unlocked by clearing the puzzle stages added to the event-exclusive playlist.

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina players use will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Travel coins.
