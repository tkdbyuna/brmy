Translated by Misora

Characters Yuragi Kanno's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		環野は人が物を食べる時に、凝視するのをなんとかしたほうがいい。目は口ほどに物を言うというが、本当だな
Kanno better fix his habit of staring at someone eating. The saying eyes speak louder than words is actually true.

Yuzuru Kise 		揺おめでとう。今日の賄いはゼリー付きだよ、しかもホイップものせてみた。揺だけだから、みんなにはナイショね
Yuragi, congrats. Today's staff meal includes a jelly, and I also put some whipped cream on it. It's only for Yuragi, so keep it a secret from the others.

Roka Suoh 		今日はユーランが好きそうなお菓子をたくさん用意してきたんだ！　今から一緒に‥‥‥え、１人がいい？　照れ屋さんだね！
Today I've prepared lots of sweets Yuran would like! Let's eat it together now...... Eh, you want to be alone? What a shy boy!

Kou Ayato 		揺が好きそうなもの持ち寄ってパフェ作ったんだけど、少しの振動にも耐えられなさそうなバランス‥‥‥どうするか
Each of us brought sweets that Yuragi would like and make a parfait out of them, but it seems like it'd fall with the slightest vibration...... What to do?

Mao Ukyo 		今日くらいは本人としても楽しく過ごしてほしいし、ご機嫌モード多めだといいけど
I want the person himself to at least have fun today, so I hope he's mostly in a good mood.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		ゆら、誕生日おめでとさん！　って大声で言ったらめっちゃ不機嫌にさせてもーたから、今日は１日部屋では静かにしてんねん
Yura, happy birthday! Ah, he'll be in a very bad mood if I say that out loud, so I should spend today quietly in my room.

Yuragi Kanno 		見て、このお菓子。全部みんながくれた。あんたが手に持ってるのも俺に？　‥‥‥嬉しい
Look at all of these sweets, everyone gave them to me. Is the one you bring also for me?...... I'm glad.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		いつもおこげと遊んでくれてありがとな。揺が好きそうなもの、たくさん作って管理部全員で待ってる
Thanks for always playing with Okoge. I made lots of food Yuragi would like so Administrative Dept. will be waiting for you today.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		揺ハピバ。おこげも待ってるから、また遊びに来な
Yuragi, hapiba. Okoge is also waiting, so come to play again.

Sei Okiya 		揺くんとおこげのじゃれあいを見かけると、時に猫が２匹いるような錯覚を覚えます。微笑ましい
When I see Yuragi-kun and Okoge playing around, I remember seeing the illusion of two cats instead sometimes. It's heartwarming.

Shizuka Fushimi 		なんていうか、猫を見つけるたびに環野が自然と紐づくんだよな。刷り込みって恐ろしい
How do I say it, Kanno always immediately gets linked to any cat he finds. Imprinting is scary.

Takeru Mikado 		この前ちょうどご機嫌だったみたいで、ゆらがね、ぺた～ってくっついてきて‥‥‥可愛すぎて持って帰りそうだったわ
Sometime ago Yura seemed to be in a good mood so he snuggled to me...... He was so cute that I almost brought him home.

Soyogu Shinkai 		環野もヒョロっこくて気になるっちゃーなるが、無理に運動させても良くないからな‥‥‥
It bothers me that Kanno is also skinny, but forcing him to exercise isn't good either.....

Shinobu Aizawa 		ゆらおめでと。じゃーん、コンビニ菓子品評会開催できるくらい買ってきたからさ、はいプレゼント～
Yura, congrats. Tadaa~ I brought so many sweets that you can even do a competitive show with them. They're your present~

Kiho Arima 		人の庇護欲に乗っかるのがうまい子だよね。自分を活かす生き方がわかっている子だ
He's someone who's skilled at taking advantage of people's desire to protect. He knows how to live life by making the most of himself.

Kyoya Shido 		誕生日って聞いてね、今日はお気に入りの洋菓子店でいくつか見繕ってみたんだ。好きなだけどうぞ
I heard it's your birthday so I went to my favorite Westem-style confectionery shop and tried choosing some sweets. Go have as much as you like.

Riku Tateshina 		甘い系のお菓子の情報ってあんま入ってこないからチョイス難しかったわ。ゆらが喜んでくれるといいけど
I don't really have any information on sweet confections so it was difficult for me to choose. I hope Yura will be happy getting these.

Tomose Onda 		たしかに機嫌に左右されるところが多いようだが、態度から敵意が感じられるものではない
He certainly seems to depend greatly on his mood, but I don't sense any hostility from his attitude.

Yu Nina 		環野は行動も愛嬌も動物的だが、そういう人間の方が対応に迷わなくて済む
Kanno's behavior and charm are animal-like, but that kind of person is actually easier to interact with.

Kamiya 		ベタすぎるけど、お菓子いっぱい買ってきたから貰って。甘いものに囲まれると、幸せになる。わかる
It's very cliché, but I bought lots of sweets, so have some. Surrounded by sweet stuff is happiness.

Urara Manami 		あいつってほんとに１人の人間か？　２人いるだろ
I understand that well. Is he really one person? There must be two of him.
