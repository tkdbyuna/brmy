I’m standing in for Owner Suoh regarding that concern (須王オーナーの代行します、その悩み, Suō ōnā no daikō shimasu, sono nayami) is an interactive mini-game questionaire featuring Roka Suoh and Yuzuru Kise, you can have a conversation with them to determine the staff to handle your trouble.

  • 👑 Ai Kosaka:
A prideful perfectionist. A high-spec human who will do what he said will do. Doesn't like or hate people and treat them straightforwardly though not a friendly person. Hates guts and wants logic. Takes care of management instead of the happy-go-lucky owner.[1]
  • 🍴 Yuzuru Kise:
A sincere good person. Kosaka's skillful assistant. Doesn't complain about whatever work is pushed onto him. Among the store full of problematic people, he is a "conscience" so no one hates Kise. Despite his delicate appearance, he is actually bold and has a lot of guts. Is weak against hunger.[2]
  • 🦎 Roka Suoh:
Simple-minded and honest. Brimming with curiosity, he wants to try doing everything, yet cannot do everything. People strangely don't hate him since he has no evil intentions. Widely connected to questionable people and has a lot of acquaintances. Although he shows a rare glimpse of his ability, no one knows the truth.[3]
  • 🎞️ Kou Ayato:
Popular since birth. Friendly and sociable but is my-paced. Hates "responsibility". He doesn't reject those who come or chase those who go in romantic relationships, has never had a special girlfriend. To avoid troublesome things, he asks Ukyo to act as a business couple together.[4]
  • 🧩 Mao Ukyo:
The brain of Simulation Dept. One of the highly educated in-store and has a high IQ. Thinks systematically and sometimes gets surprised by the surroundings. Although it seems difficult to approach, he doesn't shy away from socializing. Aporia's valuable person with common sense. Often has fun talking about makeup with Mikado.[5]
  • ⭐️ Akehoshi Hinomiya:
Friendly dog-type man. Has a caring nature and is good at being pampered. Doted by people older or younger than him. Has a simple and somewhat extreme way of thinking. Placed in the top ranks of "looks proper but is actually terrible" among the people of the store. Roommates with Kanno in the dormitory. Usually get yelled at for being too caring. [6]
  • 🍬 Yuragi Kanno:
An ultimate moody person. The way to swing it is 0 or 100, when in a good mood, he'll get along nicely with you, but when in a bad mood, he'll lash out at you when you try taking care of him. Likes cats and gets along well with Okoge, the cat kept in Administrative Dept.'s house, and also the cat community in the neighborhood.[7]
  • 🌙 Taiga Tsukimoto:
A proper person with common sense. Due to his sense of duty and caring personality, he becomes the mom role for Administrative Dept. while taking care of Ichikawa. Despite his appearance, he has a tearful side, often cries over anime and light novels.[8]
  • 🎧 Haruhi Ichikawa:
A super indoor type who has a weak constitution, which makes sunlight his natural enemy. He is also active as the streamer demu while working in Administrative Dept.. He eats small meals and is picky as much as Kanno. He is selfish towards Tsukimoto, but they share a deep trusting bond.[9]
  • 📘 Sei Okiya:
A gentle-spoken gentleman who uses formal speech with everyone. He always carries a smile on his face, but no one can read his true intentions, which makes him suspicious. He is full of warped compassion for pitiful people, so he loves both Tsukimoto and Ichikawa.[10]
  • 💻 Shizuka Fushimi:
A man who values order and loves silence. Doesn't like to interact with people and wants to be surrounded by plants instead. He cares about and is trusted by his comrades. He doesn't leave the shared space dirty, though his own territory is very dirty.[11]
  • 🌹 Takeru Mikado:
Beautifully the strongest. An overwhelming power type that dominates the field with his long limbs. Has a history of being Watchdog Dept.'s leader before Fushimi joined. Has a jealous side, but he is a passionate and kind human-like human. Beauty seeker with dry skin who never misses a face mask.[12]
  • ⚔️ Soyogu Shinkai:
A martial artist with a well-rounded physique and natural sense. Ultimately competitive. He also has an intelligent side and works as an assistant to Okiya's translation business. A secretly popular man who is polite and casually considerate. The only one who is not on the same page as Kosaka and is in a cat-and-dog relationship with him.[13]
  • 🐍 Shinobu Aizawa:
Friendly, innocent, and carefree person. He is a good-looking young man with no hidden motives, but he has a few screws missing and sometimes shows glimpses of his strangeness. Is particular about clothes and is different genre fashion friends with Ayato and Shido.[14]
  • 🔮 Kiho Arima:
A person with a graceful demeanor and fleeting aura. He also has the strength to take pride in it and live with it to the fullest. Enjoys doing fortune-telling as a side job, and his serious tarot skill is frighteningly accurate. As if "he can see everything".[15]
  • 🎨 Kyoya Shido:
Art teacher from a prominent historical family. He is a man of refined taste in his belongings, words, and actions, and is confident in everything he does, which sometimes makes him naturally feel superior. He has no malicious intent at all, but his words and actions often leave Arima and Tateshina stunned.[16]
  • 🥃 Riku Tateshina:
A freelance counselor with a psychologist certification. He is elusive, and it's unknown whether he has motivation or not. Cannot live without being in love and severely infatuated. He has a special concern for Kosaka.[17]
  • 🌏 Tomose Onda:
Never fight a losing battle and always seek "certainty". His blunt and straightforward manner tends to give a cold impression, but there is no malice beyond his words. Has a hidden airheaded and romantic side. He does not show his weakness to others, but he only allows himself to be taken in by Nina.[18]
  • 🚬 Yu Nina:
A man with cold, piercing eyes. Earnest, loyal, and never forgets a favor received. In the past, when he was leading a shabby life, he met Onda and was saved. His world is composed of "Tomose Onda" and "humans other than him", he also thinks of dying for Onda someday.[19]
  • 🍩 Kamiya:
Doesn't have any memories before coming to the store. Doesn't even know his own name or age. He is a cheerful, caring, and quick-tempered person. He has a sense of distance from Onda and Nina, and often spends time with Manami, but he is disliked by Manami himself.[20]
  • 🧹 Urara Manami:
Aggressive and rabid. He thinks everyone hates him, and he takes the stance that he should hate them, too. Being unconditionally kind to him will bug him. Although he has a bad mouth and attitude, he handles things with care. Is actually a lonely person who wants to be pampered. Annoyed when Kamiya comes to pamper him.[21]
  • Rare:
A talking-type medium sized bird. He's like a cockatoo but the details are unclear. Has a gentle, my-pace disposition. With food, he's a glutton who loves the person giving it to him. His name's origin is said to come from a place of "Because he's raw-meat colored."[22]
