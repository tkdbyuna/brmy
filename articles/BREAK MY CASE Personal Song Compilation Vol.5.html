INTRODUCTION

The fifth compilation album of personal songs that express the feelings of the staff who appear in "Break My Case".

In the fifth installment, you can enjoy songs by seven artists: Takeru Mikado, Sei Okiya, Yuzuru Kise, Shizuka Fushimi, Kamiya, Akehoshi Hinomiya, and Tomose Onda.

Please enjoy the vocal songs sung by the Aporia staff cast. Includes cast comments exclusive to the CD. [1]
