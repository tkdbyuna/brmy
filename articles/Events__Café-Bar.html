Café-Bar Event (カフェバーイベント, Kafe Bā Ibento?, lit. Café-Bar Event) is an event type that was first introduced in the event Invitation from the Underworld.

Play Activity]

In the cafe-bar event, you will make a drink using the ingredients collected in the puzzle and earn sales in "EN".

Drinks can be made from the MAKING button on the event's top screen.

When they are completed, they will be lined up at the counter, so tap or swipe to serve the drink.

When EN accumulates, you can release the event story and earn rewards such as event-limited rewards cards and Gacha tickets.

In addition, you can earn more ingredients than usual in event-limited playlists, as well as special ingredients related to this event. You can also use the background play function in event-limited playlists.

During the event period, staff will take turns in charge of each shift. Achieve sales targets and total sales targets for each shift and earn a variety of rewards.

Note:

  • In the café-bar event, you can play event-limited playlists by using regular stamina instead of event medals.
  • There is no event Pt or event Pt exchange in this event. In addition, there is no total high score ranking or event Pt ranking.

SPECIAL TIME

From Welcome! KAWAII Cafe♡ Aporia × Sanrio characters event (refer later as KAWAII Event in short), the SPECIAL TIME was introduced, when other characters will assist in the making process. During SPECIAL TIME, the making time is halved! SPECIAL TIME occurs daily in the timeframe 12:00 - 13:00 JST and 20:00 - 21:00 JST.

Ingredients]

Main article: Ingredients

In a specific event, a list of ingredients is used as the main ingredients shared between all the shifts. At the [STORAGE], you can read their descriptions and check their stocks.

※Tips: you can also check the storage in the following ways:

  • Hard press the ingredients on the [MAKING] screen
  • Hard press the ingredient drop on the Puzzle result screen

The making capacity is 100 drinks maximum, you can't make more drinks if the shift's going to end soon.
For example, it's 15:30 JST now, and you make drinks, which requires 2m45s to make. There are 30 minutes left to change shift, so you can only make the number of drinks in that time span, which is 10 drinks (10 * 2m45s = 27m30s).

Most ingredients are reused in other events except for limited ingredients.

Some recipes can be served by other staff in a different event, however, each shift within an event serves different recipes.
The last recipe of the shift is always unique to the staff.
