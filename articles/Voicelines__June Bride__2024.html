Characters June Bride 2024

Ai Kosaka 		耳の痛くなる話題なのはお前も同じだろ。はあ‥‥‥見合いだなんだと他人に心配されるような生き方はしてないんだがな
It should be sickening for you too. Hah...... They keep appointing omiai for me even though I don't live a life that worries others.

Yuzuru Kise 		この間ちょうど花嫁行列を見たんです。道を開ける通行人はきっと他人のはずなのにみんな眼差しがあたたかくて‥‥‥素敵な空間でした
I saw a bridal procession some time ago. The passerbies who opened up the way should be strangers, but their gazes were warm...... It was a wonderful space.

Roka Suoh 		大きな選択の先に、たくさんの幸せがありますように！
May there be lots of happiness beyond the big decision!

Kou Ayato 		ドレスも白無垢も似合いそうだけど‥‥‥お前が選ぶお相手さんって、ちょっと想像つかないわ。どんな感じ？
A dress or a white wedding kimono would suit you though... I can't really imagine the partner you'd settle with. What kind of person is it?

Mao Ukyo 		ジューンブライドね。海外なら乾季で気候的に良くても日本だと梅雨だし、そこはロマンってやつなのかな
June bride. It'd be dry season and mostly good weather abroad, but in Japan, it's the rainy season, I guess that's the romance in it.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		結婚かあ。今したら、ゆらも合わせて３人暮らしとかになるんかなあ
Marriage, huh. If it's now, then with Yura, the three of us will be living together.

Yuragi Kanno 		結婚？　俺にはあんまり関係ない。でもファーストバイトってやつだけやりたい
Marriage? Not related to me. I only want to do the thing called "first bite".

Taiga Tsukimoto 		人様の幸せな姿って感動しますね‥‥‥。この前なんて月１０で思わず‥‥‥泣きそうになりました
Looking at other people's happiness moves me. .. Even before I unintentionally... wanted to cry for a whole month.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		結婚とか、赤の他人と暮らすってことでしょ？　今の生活は‥‥‥また別の話
Marriage means living together with a stranger, right? My current lifestyle... is a different story.

Sei Okiya 		ジューンブライドですか‥‥‥そうですねえ。結婚よりも、その話を私に振るに至った貴方の思考の方がよほど興味深い
It's June Bride....... Yes. Though the thoughts that led you to bring up this topic to me are far more interesting than the marriage topic itself.

Shizuka Fushimi 		俺は１人が楽で好きなんだ。誰かと一緒にいたいって思う未来は‥‥‥今の所見えない
Being alone is easy and I like it. The future where I think of wanting to be together with someone......For now, I can't see it.

Takeru Mikado 		和装も洋装も捨てがたい！　着たいもの全部着て、したいこと全部叶えればいいわよね、アーン話してるだけで楽しくなっちゃう
Traditional outfit and western outfit are both hard to choose! It should be fine to wear everything you want to and have everything you wish for granted. Ahn~ Talking about it is already fun.

Soyogu Shinkai 		お前にそう言うやつができたら、俺より強いか試してやるから連れてこい。しょーもないやつだったら追い返す
If you have someone you'd like to marry, then let me test his strengths so bring him to me. I'll chase him out if it's someone good for nothing.

Shinobu Aizawa 		この前ちょうどウエディングフォト撮ってるとこに遭遇して、幸せド真ん中って顔してました。‥‥‥そっか結婚かぁ
Before I coincidentally encountered a wedding photo session and their expressions clearly show that they're in the middle of very big happiness. .....I see, marriage, huh.

Kiho Arima 		言葉というのは口にした瞬間から、責任を伴うからね。誓い方も気をつけないと
Words hold responsibility the moment they are spoken. We have to be careful about how we make an oath.

Kyoya Shido 		吏来さんに『恭耶のタキシード姿は結婚詐欺みたいだな』って言われたよ。お互い様だよね
Riku-san said, “Kyoya in a tuxedo looks like a marriage swindler," to me. I think that goes for both of us.

Riku Tateshina 		この時期特有のなになにブルーっていうのも多いよね。みーんな幸せ一色でありますように
Lots of stuff are in this month's characteristic color, blue something. May everyone be filled with happiness.

Tomose Onda 		道で甘い香りがした。クチナシだろうか、白が似合う季節だな
There was a sweet smell on the streets. Perhaps it's the gardenias, a season where white suits it.

Yu Nina 		誰かと共に人生を全うすることを誓うという意味では、頷ける文化ではある
It's an understandable tradition in the means of swearing an oath to live out one's life with somebody.

Kamiya 		誰かと共に人生をうちはタキシード似合いそうな人ばっかだな〜。こうも美形揃いだと感覚おかしくなりそ
We have lots of people here who'd look good in tuxedos~ Being together with many good-looking people can make your sense become strange.

Urara Manami 		オレには関係ない
This has nothing to do with me.
