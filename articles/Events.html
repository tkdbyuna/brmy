How to Play Event]

There are multiple event formats, each with different gameplay.

Event formats have been implemented:

  • Medal-Format Event
  • Travelogue series
  • Prequel series events
  • Café-Bar Event
  • OJT Event
  • Vignette Series
[Medal-Format Event]
Main article: Medal-Format Event

During the event period, you can earn Event pt by clearing puzzles and event missions. The Event pt you collect can be exchanged for luxurious rewards in the exchange shop, and reaching certain totals will unlock event‑exclusive story content.

You can obtain Event Medals each time you play a stage from the normal playlist. By spending collected Medals, you can play stages in the event‑exclusive playlist.

※Stages in the event‑exclusive playlist award more Event pt than the normal playlist.

Event‑exclusive playlists feature newly released songs and provide early access to event‑new card enhancement items.

※Event‑new card enhancement items will also become obtainable in stages added after the event ends.

If you include staff cards that are eligible for event bonuses, the amount of Event pt you earn increases. Details about event bonuses can be checked from the event top screen during the event period.

A Total High Score Ranking will also be held, where ranking is determined by the total high score in the event‑exclusive playlist.

[Travelogue Series Event]
Main article: Travelogue Series Event

Travelogue series event is also an event where you play puzzles to collect Event pt, but some aspects differ from a medal‑format event.

Key differences: The event period is split into a first half and a second half; an Event pt ranking is held during the first half.

The Event pt ranking determines ranks based on cumulative Event pt at the end of the first‑half period, and rewards are awarded according to rank.

※Event pt can continue to be collected until the entire event ends, and can be used in the exchange shop and to unlock event stories. ※The Total High Score Ranking runs until the entire event ends.

  • [Features of the Travelogue series event]

When the event enters the second half, additional puzzle stages and Another Story are released.

From the start of the second half, playing stages in the event‑exclusive playlist will also grant Travel Coins — items exclusive to the Travelogue series.

Travel Coins can be exchanged for items at the event exchange shop.

※Event pt can still be obtained from stages released in the second half.

  • [Features of the Travelogue series event story]

“Travelogue” is a limited‑time event series that individually features the six departments of Aporia: Main Office, Simulation Dept., Administrative Dept., Watchdog Dept., Negotiation Dept., and ST Dept.. Triggered by the discovery of Aporia’s hidden seventh department, the “Memories Department,” the series unfolds event stories themed around trips taken by each department.

[Café-Bar Event]
Main article: Café-Bar Event

In the Café-Bar event, you use materials earned by playing Puzzle to create drinks and collect Sales EN. Depending on the amount of EN you accumulate, you can unlock event stories and earn generous rewards, such as event-exclusive reward cards.

Café-Bar event operates on a shift system, and depending on the shift, the drinks you can create and the content of event-exclusive stages will vary. Additionally, there are two types of sales rewards you can earn by collecting Sales EN: shift-specific rewards and total sales rewards.

If you include staff cards eligible for event bonuses in your lineup, the amount of materials you obtain will increase, and the amount of EN you receive when collecting drinks will also increase. You can check the details of event bonuses from the event top screen during the event period. Event pt, the exchange shop, and high score rankings are not available in the Café-Bar event.

[OJT Event]
Main article: OJT Event

In this OJT event, you’ll take on challenge stages and aim to achieve a BREAK by surpassing the trainer’s (opponent’s) score. You can use Prize Medals earned each time you play a puzzle to play Prize Boxes, which grant random rewards.

The OJT Event operates on a shift system; the content of the challenge stages and the contents of the Prize Boxes will vary depending on the shift.

When you include eligible Staff Cards in your team, medals earned, and the cards’ total power increase during challenge stages. Detailed event bonus information can be viewed from the event top screen during the event period. The Prize Box is an item gacha where you can win random rewards.

※Items in stock and random items are available.
※As the “number of items” in stock decreases, the chance of winning the remaining items increases.

Event Mission]

Further information: Mission

These are missions that can only be attempted during the event period.

◆ Types of Event Missions]

[Daily Mission]

Progress is reset every day at 4:00 AM JST.

[Main Mission]

These appear based on your progress in the event.
※Unlike Daily Mission, these are not reset.

[Achievement]

These appear based on your progress in the event.
※Unlike Daily Mission, these are not reset.

[Extra Mission]

Unlocked after clearing all Event Main Missions.
※Depending on the event format, some missions — such as Extra Mission or Main Mission— may not be available.

◆ Mission Completion Rewards]

  • Tap the [Claim] button to receive your rewards.
  • The [Claim All] button allows you to claim all completed rewards for each mission type at once.
  • Any mission rewards not claimed by the end of the event period will be sent to your Gift Box when you log in the following day or later.

※Please note that items in the Gift Box have an expiration date.

  • Missions for which you have already claimed rewards will be moved to the bottom of the list.

Event-limited Playlist]

This is a playlist available only during the event period. Within the playlist, you can play new songs introduced for the event and earn new card enhancement items.

[Medal-Format Event]
Main article: Medal-Format Event

In the Medal-Format event, you need event medals to play the event-exclusive playlist. You can earn more event pt in the event-exclusive playlist than in other playlists.
You can earn Event Medals by clearing regular puzzle stages during the event period.

[Prequel Series Event] & [Travelogue Series Event]
Main articles: Prequel Series Event and Trave;logue Series Event

In the Prequel and Travelogue Series event, you can use regular Stamina to play the event-exclusive playlist. The event-exclusive playlist and the playlists of staff members featured in the event allow you to earn more Event pt than other playlists.

[Café-Bar Event]
Main article: Café-Bar Event

In the Café-Bar event, you can use regular Stamina to play the event-exclusive playlists. Event-exclusive playlists allow you to collect more ingredients than regular stages.

[OJT Event]
Main article: OJT Event

There are no “exclusive playlists” in the OJT event. You take on the challenge stages displayed on the event's top screen.

Event Ranking]

Various rankings will be held depending on the event format.

※There are no rankings for the Café-Bar event.
※There are no rewards based on ranking for the Continuous BREAK rankings held during the OJT event.

◆ Total High Score Ranking]

Held during the Medal-Format event, Prequel Series, and Travelogue series events. Rankings are determined based on the total high score achieved in the event-exclusive playlist.

The total high score is the sum of the high scores from all stages within the playlist.

Rankings will be announced on the event's top screen after the event ends. Rewards will be added to your present box based on your ranking position. You can check interim rankings and rewards by tapping the [Check Rankings] button on the event top screen.

<img>

※Rankings are updated every 15 minutes.
※From the end of the event until the rankings are announced, the status will show as “Tallying,” and you will not be able to view interim rankings.

◆ Event pt Ranking]

Held during Travelogue series events. Event pt rankings are determined based on the total number of event pt earned.

Rankings will be announced on the event's top screen after the event ends. Rewards will be distributed to your present box based on your ranking position.

You can check interim rankings and rewards by tapping the [Check Rankings] button on the event top screen.

<img>

※The rankings are updated every 15 minutes. ※From the end of the event until the rankings are announced, the status will be “Tallying,” and you will not be able to view interim rankings.

◆ Consecutive BREAK Ranking]

Held during the OJT event. Rankings are determined based on the number of consecutive BREAKs achieved in the Advance Phase during each shift period.

※There are no rewards based on ranking position for the Consecutive BREAK Ranking.
