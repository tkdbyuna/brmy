Raid (レイド, Reido) is a playable puzzle song in BREAK MY CASE. It's Urara Manami's first personal character song sung by his voice actor, Hiroyuki Yoshino, and composed by Amala. The song is featured on the BREAK MY CASE Personal Song Compilation Vol.2.


To play the first solos in-game, you need to finish reading all 8 episodes of Thread1. Chilling Green and have at least 1,000 NICEs for the respective character. To unlock the Jukebox, the puzzle score is at least SS on any side.

Main Mission - Jukebox
Japanese English Reward
スコアランクSSを達成しよう [レイド] Achieve Score Rank SS at playlist [Raid]

Note: So far, Personal Song doesn't have Side C yet, even if that song has Side B.
