Marvelous Challenge

Marvelous Challenge is a character's exclusive playlist, where players can challenge themselves with more difficult puzzles than the normal puzzle playlist. You can participate with ZERO stamina, however, Card Lv. and NICE pt won't be accumulated in this stage.

How to Play]

Season 4]

Season 4 (or Marvelous Challenge Elite) starts from 2026/03/01 to 2027/01/31:

  • For a limited time, the challenge is released. Take on puzzles that are more difficult than the regular stages. Aim for SS Rank with your carefully chosen team!
  • (NEW) As the difficulty increases, the stage content changes. Strive to achieve SS Rank at every difficulty level!
  • Clear the Challenge Stage to get Challenge coin! You can trade Challenge Coins for luxurious items at the Marvelous CHANGE Exchange.
  • Clear the ELITE‑difficulty Challenge Stage with SS Rank to earn a "Wappen" (badge) awarded only to truly great puzzlers!

Marvelous Challenge Elite]

From March 2026, Marvelous Challenge is renewed and named Marvelous Challenge Elite.

  1. A challenge stage appears for a specific staff member every month!
  2. Clear the challenge stage to earn Challenge Coins!
  3. New pins available exclusively through Challenge Coins!
  4. Clear the challenge stage at ELITE difficulty with an SS rank to get a "Wappen" (badge)!
