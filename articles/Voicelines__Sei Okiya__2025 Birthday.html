Characters Sei Okiya's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		言動見た目全てにおいて何を考えているかわからない部分があるからな。何を贈れば良いのか、相変わらず悩む相手だ。
I can't understand what he's thinking just by looking at his behavior and appearance. I always have a hard time choosing a gift for him.

Yuzuru Kise 		お誕生日おめでとうございます。誓さんの食リポを聞いていると、俺まで食べた気持ちになるのでとても幸せです。お腹は空いちゃうんですけど……
Happy birthday. Listening to Sei-san's food report makes me feel like I was actually eating it, and it makes me happy. It makes me hungry though

Roka Suoh 		誓が好きなものといえば‥‥‥レアくん！　るんるんにお願いしてオリジナルグッズを作るのも手かな？！　なんなら、僕も欲しい！
If it's what Sei likes then it's...... Rare-kun! Asking Runrun to make some original merch of him might be it?! Then I'd like to have some of my own too!

Kou Ayato 		揺だけじゃなくってレアもたまに行ってるって聞きました。隠岐谷さんって意外と動物に優しいんですね
I heard that not only Yuragi, but Rare also visit you sometime. Okiya-san is surprisingly kind to animals.

Mao Ukyo 		隠岐谷さんも結構映画を見るほうでしょ？　なら、デジタルチケットを送るのもありか。せっかくなら券面のデザインにもこだわっておこうかな
Okiya-san watches movies quite often too, right? Sending him a digital ticket might be good. Since it's like that, I should put some effort into the ticket design too.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		誓さんに今度Aporiaきてくれん、って言ってみよ。誓さんが気に入りそうなお酒、ミカ姉に頼んで特訓してもらったんよね
Let's try inviting Sei-san to come to Aporia. I had asked Mika-nee to specially train me about the drinks Sei-san likes.

Yuragi Kanno 		このお茶‥‥‥お菓子とあわせると美味しい。誓はそういうの見つけるの上手だよね
This tea...... It's good to drink it with the sweets. Sei is good at finding stuff like that.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		誓さんのおかげで管理部は成り立ってる。今日１日で伝え切れるものでもないけど、精一杯祝わせてくれ
The Administrative Dept. is established thanks to Sei-san. It's not something I can convey in just one day, but let me celebrate to the fullest.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		一緒に暮らしてても何が嬉しいのかとか、どういう生活してんのかとか謎だわ。まあこれも変わらず、大河が何か作ったりする方が喜ぶんだろうけど
Even if we live together, what makes him happy or how he spend his daily life are still a mystery to me. Well, I bet he'll be happy if Taiga just makes something for him like usual.

Sei Okiya 		誕生日のお祝いでしたら、どうかお気遣いなく。どうしてもとおっしゃるなら、この超入手困難な数量限定高級ハーブティーを‥‥‥。ふふ、冗談です。その気持ちだけで十分ですよ
Please don't worry about celebrating my birthday. If you insist, then please get this extremely hard-to-find, limited edition, luxurious herb tea...... Fufu, it's a joke. Your feeling is already enough.

Shizuka Fushimi 		新開が隠岐谷さんに何を贈るか悩んでた。読めない人だけど、言葉だけでも喜んでくれる人ではあるだろうから、尚更なんじゃないか
Shinkai was wondering what he should give to Okiya-san. He's unreadable, but he's someone who would be happy just by receiving some words, making it even harder.

Takeru Mikado 		短くはない付き合いになってきたけど、知らないことが沢山あるわ。お誕生日おめでとう、そしてこれからもよろしくね
It's not like we've only known each other for a short time, but there are still lots of things I don't know about you. Happy birthday, I'll be in your care from now on, too.

Soyogu Shinkai 		誓さん、舌も目もすげえ肥えてるからな‥‥‥。気持ちを喜んでくれる人ではあっても、祝うハードルは高いよな
Sei-san have a really good taste and eyes...... Even if the feelings themselves already make him happy, the bar to celebrate him is just too high.

Shinobu Aizawa 		誓さんへのプレゼント、絶対迷走してるってわかってるんだけど、いっそ『肩たたき券』みたいなのが一番喜んでくれたりとかない‥‥‥？
I know I'm definitely going to be lost at what to give Sei-san though, don't you think a "shoulder massage ticket" might make him happy......?

Kiho Arima 		誓の話は本当に面白いよ。今度プレゼントにお茶でも持って遊びに顔出すから、また話そう
Sei's stories are very interesting. I'll visit you and bring some tea as a gift, so let's talk again at that time.

Kyoya Shido 		誓さんはグルメだから、贈るならやっぱり美味しい食事かな？　ディナーチケット、大河くんと春日くんの分も用意しないとね
Sei-san is a gourmet, so if I'm giving him a gift, delicious food must be the answer, right? A dinner ticket, I should prepare it for Taiga-kun and Haruhi-kun too.

Riku Tateshina 		そこそこ長い付き合いだけど、誓もなかなかのグルメだから、贈り物するとき少しだけ緊張するんだよな
We've known each other for quite a long time, but Sei is quite a gourmet, so I'm a bit nervous to give him a gift.

Tomose Onda 		隠岐谷のありがとうには、他意があると思わせられる。不服なわけではなさそうだが
Okiya's "thank you" seems to have a hidden meaning. It doesn't seem like a dissatisfaction though.

Yu Nina 		隠岐谷がランチを食べに来た日は、夜に長文が届く。毎度参考になる。相変わらず何を考えているかはわからないが
Okiya would send me a long text at the night of the days when he comes to eat lunch. It always becomes a good reference, but as always, I don't know what he's thinking.

Kamiya 		今頃ハウスでお祝いしてもらってるんだろうな。邪魔しないように、お祝いメッセージは後で送っておこう
I guess they're celebrating in the House right now. I should send the celebratory message later to not disturb them.

Urara Manami 		さっき誕生祝いで出たケーキがどれくらい美味かったか延々語ってた。口、回りすぎだろ
He endlessly talked about how good the cake served for his birthday was earlier. He's too talkative.
