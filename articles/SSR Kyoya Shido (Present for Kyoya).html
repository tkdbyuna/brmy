Home ①
 出会った頃は、誰かの誕生日に3人で集まってお祝いするようになるとは想像もしなかったな。次はどんな予想外が起こるんだろう？今から楽しみだよ
When we first met, I never would have imagined that the three of us would end up getting together to celebrate someone’s birthday. I wonder what other surprises await us next. I’m already looking forward to it.
Home ②
 誕生日は、周囲に日頃の感謝を伝える機会でもあると思うんだ。樹帆さんと吏来さんには特別お世話になっているからね、今年は小旅行でも用意しようかな
I think birthdays are also a chance to express my gratitude to the people around me. Since Kiho-san and Riku-san have especially helped me, maybe I’ll arrange a short trip this year.

Roka-san celebrated with a birthday song—it was such an intriguing surprise. It might be fun to ask Riku-san for one too.
