Overview]

Aporia received a major request involving all staff members - A sabotage request. To commemorate the launch of a new luxury cruise ship, an immersive theater experience called "Cruise into the Story" has been planned on board. What the clients are seeking from Aporia, who will provide "temporary solutions," is...?

Play Activity]

Play normal puzzle song to earn Pt "Kusudama" (くす玉, kusudama, or "traditional Japanese paper balloon" or "festive paper orb"). Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts, but card level, coins, and drops are still 1 times.
