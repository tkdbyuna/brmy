Something on my back......? Hm, this fish pattern...... There can only be one culprit...... ROKA!

Ai-san may look like that, but his sleep talk is very cute. Which do you think it is, a truth or a lie?

I'm still trying to come up with a lie. I plan to surprise Tomo and Yuhan. The higher the hurdle, the better, right? Right!?

I'm going to the convenience store though, do you need anything? Akehoshi handed Yuragi an éclair squishy. It's what you think it is, I'm covering up for him.

April's Fool, huh. I'm actually going out with you...... if I said something like that, Kou would be troubled, so that's not a good lie.

Stare into my eyes for 7 seconds will make you like me. Alright, look closely...... Ah, Riku-san is glaring at me~ I'll stop.

I was the one who ate the sweets you left in the locker. ......It's a lie though, it's no use if you don't get angrier.

Haruhi actually went for a run yesterday...... Did you figure out that it was a lie? Haha, you know Haruhi well already.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		アイコン変えて嘘動画出して、って‥‥‥どう考えても１日で消費されるべきコンテンツじゃなくね？
Change my profile picture and upload a lie video...... No matter how I think about it, aren't they just one-day limited contents?

Even though lying isn't a privilege one can only have on this day. I suppose today is the day when honest people can have a taste of escaping reality.

I won't stop talking when I start, so I usually hold myself back. ...... Well, it's a lie, did you believe it?

Takeru Mikado 		この前エッグい額、使っちゃったのよね～気になる？　お耳貸してちょうだい‥‥‥エイプリルフールの嘘って？　じゃあそういうことで♡
I spent a huge amount of money last time~ Are you curious? Lend me your ear then...... April's Fool lie, you said? Then it is what it is♡

Wouldn't it be more beneficial to train your mind and body instead of wasting your time thinking of stupid lies? Oi, whoever said that it's boring, show me your face.

Shizuka-san's would only give a little reaction to my lie......I should tell it to Gucchi first, unveil it to Mika-nee, and finally, the last boss Shizuka-san. Alright, let's go!

Lying takes more effort than staying silent, so I'll stay silent today.
