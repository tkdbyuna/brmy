Characters Yuragi Kanno's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		仕事に支障がないなら、お前の猫じみた振る舞いについてどうこういう気はない。だが、許可なく俺の背後を取るな。足音を立てて近づいてこい。誕生日だから許すが、次はない
I don't intend to care much about your cat-like behavior as long as it doesn't affect your work. However, don't appear behind me without my consent. Approach me with your footsteps audible, I'm forgiving you because it's your birthday, but there won't be next time.

Yuzuru Kise 		揺のことをたくさん知ってるからこそ、あれもこれも喜ぶだろうなってすぐ想像できちゃって。プレゼント候補がいつばいになっちゃった
I can picture so many things that will make Yuragi feel happy to receive it since I know you very well. I got so many gift candidates.

Roka Suoh 		じやじゃーん、知り合いから猫カフェのチケットをもらったんだ。今度一緒に行こうネッ☆ ユーランがいたら、おやつがなくても猫たちが遊びにきてくれそうだ！
Tadaa, an acquaintance gave me some tickets to a cat café. Let's go there together next time☆The cats will surely come to play with us even without any treats when Yuran is with me!

Kou Ayato 		あれ食べたい、おこげ描いて、寝るから枕になれって、誕生日パワーで随分マイベースな‥‥‥と思ったけど、割といつものことか
Want to eat that, draw Okoge, be my pillow cause I want to sleep, you're doing things as you like thanks to the birthday power...... was what I thought, but it's actually nothing out of ordinary.

Mao Ukyo 		はい、猫カフェのチケット。この間代行で行ってきたんだけど、どの子も生き生きしててすごくよかった。僕も今度、本を読みにいく時にでも利用しようかな
Here, a cat café ticket I went there for an agency work before and every one of it were lively. I should probably go there again with a book to read.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		ゆらがハウスの炬燵が気持ちいいって言うてたし、うちでも導入するのはどうやろ？ 俺が壊さへんかは心配やけどなあ
Yura said that the kotatsu in the House felt nice, so how about we bring one to our place? I'm worried about me breaking it though.

Yuragi Kanno 		お誕生日の人は主役だから、お菓子たくさんもらっていいって言われた。いつも食べてるけど、みんなからもらったやつの方が美味しい気がする
I was told that its fine for the birthday person to get many sweets since theyre the main character. I always eat some, but I feel like the ones given by everyone taste better.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		来るかどうかはまだ聞いていないんですが、ケーキは用意してあるんです。今日じゃなければまた焼けばいいだけですし、他で祝われてるならそれも嬉しいので
I haven't asked whether he'd come visit or not but live already prepared a cake. I can just bake another one if he doesn't come today, knowing him being celebrated by others also makes me happy.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		揺おたおめ～。大河も張り切っていろんなもん準備してたし、おこげもおしやれして待ってるからおいで
Yura, HBD~ Taiga is being all enthusiastic and did lots of preparations, Okoge is also dressed up waiting for you, so come here.

Sei Okiya 		おこげもお祝いしたいと言っていました。本当ですよ、今日はお誕生日だと伝えたらみやんと鳴いていたので
Okoge said that it wants to celebrate too. It's the truth, when I told it that today is your birthday, it meowed as an answer.

Shizuka Fushimi 		さっき環野が急に路地裏から出てきてさすがに少し驚いた。祝うタイミング逃したから後で見かけたら言っとく
Kanno suddenly appeared from an alley and it surprised me a bit I missed my timing to congratulate him, so I'll tell him when I meet him again.

Takeru Mikado 		グルテンフリーって言わすに持ってたお菓子をあげたんだけど、思ってたのとちょっと違うって顔してたわ。悪いことしたわね
I gave him the snack I had without telling him that it was gluten-free, and he had this face saying like it was different than what he expected. I did something bad.

Soyogu Shinkai 		よく行くランニングコースに猫の溜まり場が‥‥‥つて最初にランニングの誘いじゃないって言わねえと、警戒されるかもな
I found a cats' gathering place along the running course I frequent go through. ...... Ah, I need to tell him beforehand that I'm not inviting him to run together, or he'd be wary of me.

Shinobu Aizawa 		じやじゃーん！ゆらへお菓子の家をプレゼントします。自分で組み立てるやつだけど‥‥‥そのまま食べても良しとした！
Tadaa! I'm giving Yura a gingerbread house as a gift. You need to build it on your own though...... You can also eat them as it is!

Kiho Arima 		お誕生日おめでとう。揺が好きそうなお菓子をいくつか買ってきたんだ。猫動画も見繕ってみたから見てみて
Happy birthday. I bought some sweets that Yuragi would like. I also picked out some cat videos, so try watching them too.

Kyoya Shido 		揺くんの誕生日プレゼントは迷わなくていいよね。今年も俺のお気に入りの洋菓子店で見繕ってきたから、喜んでくれると思うよ
It's nice that I dont need to think hard about Yuragi-kun's birthday gift. I picked them out from my favorite western confectionery shop again this year.

Riku Tateshina 		甘いお菓子ばかりだったから逆張りで塩つけのあるお菓子を選んでみたけど‥‥‥。好みじゃない可能性もあったか
It was all sweet snacks, so I chose a snack that is salty in contrast...... There's a chance he won't like it huh.

Tomose Onda 		環野が菓子で喜ぶというのであれば、確か貰い物があったはすだ。今度持ってこよう
If Kanno will be happy to receive sweets then, I certainly received some before. I should bring it next time.

Yu Nina 		誕生日の祝いにと甘い物をせがまれた。わかりやすくて助かる
He begged me to give him sweet things to celebrate his birthday. It's good that he is easy to understand.

Kamiya 		揺さんにハピドの超甘々なドーナツあげようかなって思ったけど、他のにした。今日はたくさんの人からお菓子をもらってるだろうから日持ちしないものはやめておこうかなって
I was thinking of giving [[|Yuragi-san]] the super duper sweet donut from Hapido, but I ended up going with something different. Since he'll be getting lots of sweets from everyone,

Urara Manami 		あいつ祝うのになんかやるつもりなら、ポロポロ落ちねえ菓子にしろ。掃除は一応するみてえだが、たまに甘え時あってダルい
I decided to not give him something that won't last long. If you plan to give him something to celebrate, make it a snack that doesn't crumble. He'd try to clean it, but there are times when he just becomes all sluggish.
