Characters Kyoya Shido's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		祠堂が名指しで特別なプレゼントをくれないかと言ってきた。当然、そんなものはない
Shido specially called me out and asked if I could give him a special gift. Of course, I won't be giving him anything.

Yuzuru Kise 		恭耶さんのお誕生日に最近美味しかったお菓子をお渡ししたんですが、早速お返しをもらってしまって‥‥‥。どんな時でも恭耶さんは恭耶さんで、尊敬します
I gave Kyoya-san some sweets I found nice recently, but he quickly gave me a return gift......I respect the way that Kyoya-san is always Kyoya-san.

Roka Suoh 		キョンキョンが喜ぶ美しいものかあ。僕にとってはAporiaの仲間との絆が何よりも美しいものだと思うんだけれど、キョンキョンもそう思ってくれるかな？
Something beautiful that will make Kyonkyon happy, huh. For me, the bond of Aporia is the most beautiful thing I could ever find though, will Kyonkyon think the same?

Kou Ayato 		恭耶くんと行きたいお店があるんだよね。誕生日のお祝い兼ねて、今度都合いい時一緒にきてくれない？
There's a store I want to visit with Kyoya-kun. Making it a birthday celebration as well, can you please come with me the next time it's convenient for you?

Mao Ukyo 		本当に欲しいものは、欲しいと思った瞬間に手に入れてそうだし、恭耶さんの周りの人ってプレゼント選び苦労しそうだよね
Kyoya-san seems to be capable of immediately getting anything he really wants, so the people around him must be having a difficult time to choose a present

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		恭耶さんへのプレゼントって、Aporiaでするお祝いの中でも最高クラスの難問やない？　だから一緒に正解探してくれへん～？
Isn't coming up with a present for Kyoya-san a top-class challenge among Aporia's celebrations? That's why, why don't we search for the answer together~?

Yuragi Kanno 		持ってた駄菓子恭耶にあげたら、誰よりも美味しく食べられる君が食べるべき、だって。そうかもって、返してもらった
When I gave Kyoya some cheap sweets I was carrying, he told me "you, the one who can eat them deliciously more than anyone, should be eating it." I said "well," and he returned them to me.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		祠堂さんって、“絵の中から出てきました”ってくらい人間離れした雰囲気あるよな。って、やっぱりこれも失礼か‥‥‥？
Shido-san has some inhuman vibes that make him look like "he came out of a painting". Ah, I guess this also sounds rude......?

Haruhi Ichikawa 		Aporiaで鉢合わせた時に俺のアナログイラストが見たいって言われて。一応描いたけど、これどうしたらいいのって今更気づいたわ。誓にパスしとくか
He told me that he wanted to see my analog drawing when I came across him in Aporia some time ago. I finished a drawing though, I just started wondering about what I should do with it. I should probably pass it to Sei.

Sei Okiya 		お眼鏡に叶うといいのですが‥‥‥とは言いつつ。今回紹介するお店は必ず気に入っていただけると思っています。私が行きつけている中でも、さらに“とっておき”ですから
I hope this is to your liking....... That aside, I'm sure that you'll like the store I'll be introducing you to. This is the best among all the places I frequent after all.

Shizuka Fushimi 		祠堂さんの誕生日だってね。会うことがあれば伝えるけど、まあ可能性は低いだろ
I heard it's Shido-san's birthday. I'll greet him when I meet him though, the chance should be low.

Takeru Mikado 		吏来からお気に入りって聞いたウイスキーを仕入れたの。まずはトワイスアップでいかがかしら
Riku told me that you like a certain whisky, and we happened to have purchased it. How about starting from a twice-up?

Soyogu Shinkai 		祠堂さんって、年取ってもこれ以上姿変わんなそうじゃねえか？　理屈ではそんなわけねえとはわかってても、そう思わせられるっつーか
Don't you think Shido-san's appearance won't change anymore even if he gets older? Even if I know that's logically impossible. He makes me think so, do you get it?

Shinobu Aizawa 		恭耶さんおめでとうございます。お祝いにとびっきりの１杯作るんで、よかったらカフェ寄ってってください！
Kyoya-san, congratulations. I'll make a glass of the best drink to celebrate, so please come visit the café!

Kiho Arima 		今日は特別、恭耶くんのみてほしいことなんでもみてみよう。‥‥‥というのは口実で一緒に食事でもどうかな？
Special for today, let's look into all the things Kyoya-kun wants to see...... That was actually an excuse, how about we have a meal together?

Kyoya Shido 		俺が祝福してほしい人達にお祝いされる、今年も贅沢な誕生日を迎えることができて嬉しいよ
I'm happy to be able to celebrate another extravagant birthday with the people I want to be celebrated by this year.

Riku Tateshina 		お誕生日おめでとう。去年散々好きなものを強請られたからね。欲しいものを直接聞くのは無難で間違いないけど、俺からの気持ちも遅らせてよ
Happy birthday. I tried forcing you to tell me what you like last year, I know it's difficult to directly ask what you want, but let me send my feelings along that.

Tomose Onda 		いつにも増して祠堂の存在感が強いが、誕生日なのか。言われると、そうだろうなという感じがある
Shido's presence seems to be stronger than usual, then I found out that it's his birthday. Now that you mention it, it does feel like that.

Yu Nina 		よくわからないことを言うやつだが、わからないままで困ることもない
He's someone who says things I don't understand, but not understanding it doesn't give me any problem.

Kamiya 		恭耶さんセンサーに引っ掛からなかった時のために、消え物のプレゼントを探してきた。受け取ってはくれると思うんだよね
I'm looking for a perishable gift, in case it doesn't get detected by Kyoya-san sensor. I think he'll accept it though.

Urara Manami 		誕生日とかしらねー。こいつ自我強すぎて、微妙に話通じねーんだよ。早くあっちいけ
I don't care about his birthday. His too-big ego makes me unable to communicate with him. Just hurry up and go away.
