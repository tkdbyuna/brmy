In missions (ミッション, misshon?), you can earn rewards according to your gameplay progress.

※Note: Stages that are cleared in the limited-playlist of characters won't be taken into account in the mission progress.

Mission Type]

◆ Beginner Mission]

Main article: Beginner Mission

Beginner Mission (ビギナーミッション, biginaa misshon?) is a mission for beginners that you can challenge after finishing the tutorial. By progressing through them, you’ll learn how to play BREAK MY CASE.

◆ Daily Mission]

Main article: Daily Mission

Daily Mission (ミッション・ディリー, dirī misshon?) reset progress daily at 4:00 AM JST.

Also, before clearing the Daily Mission, you will get login bonus upon login.

◆ Main Mission]

Main article: Main Mission

Main Mission (メインミッション, mein misshon?) appears based on your gameplay progress and doesn't reset daily like Daily Mission.

◆ Achievement]

Main article: Achievement
Further information: Titles/List and Pins/List

Achievement (実績, jisseki?) recognizes a player’s completion of a specific challenge, milestone, or optional task. They also appear based on your gameplay progress and don't reset daily like Daily Mission.

◆ Special Mission]

Main article: Special Mission

Special Mission (スペシャルミッション, supesharu misshon?) is available for a limited time, usually during campaign periods and similar occasions.

◆ Bremile Mission]

Main article: Bremile Mission

Bremile Mission (ブレマイルミッション, Buremairu misshon?) resets progress monthly at 4:00 AM JST on the first day of the month. Bremile is a point that accumulates according to the stamina consumed in Puzzles or Spins.

※ You earn 1 Bremile for every 1 stamina consumed.

Additional Premium Missions that grant extra rewards will be unlocked. Furthermore, if you purchase a Bremile Pass in the shop, you can receive luxurious rewards.

You can claim rewards in the [Bremile] section of Missions based on the amount of Bremile you’ve accumulated.

◆ Event Mission]

Main article: Event Mission

(イベントミッション, ibento misshon?) is a mission belong to Events In Event Mission, they're also divided into Daily, Main, and Achievement. Besides that, they can also have an Extra Mission where the Main Mission has been cleared.

Depending on the occasions and type of events, the event mission will vary. Please check the main page of a specific event to know the details.

Mission Completion Rewards]

  • Press the [Receive] (受け取る, uketoru?, lit. to claim) button to claim your reward.
  • The [Receive All] (一括受け取り, ikkatsu uketori?, lit. bulk claim) button allows you to collect all completed rewards for each type of mission at once.
  • Mission rewards not claimed before the mission period ends will be sent to your Present Box the next time you log in.

※ Please note that rewards in the Present Box have an expiration date.

  • Missions whose rewards have already been claimed will move to the bottom of the list.
