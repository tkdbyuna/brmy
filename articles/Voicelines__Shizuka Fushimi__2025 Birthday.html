Characters Shizuka Fushimi's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		強行部の奴らが妙に盛り上がっていると思ったら、節見の誕生日か。芦佳と張る騒がしさだな
I thought the Watchdog Dept. was strangely getting all excited, turns out it's because of Fushimi's birthday. Their noisiness is on par with Roka.

Yuzuru Kise 		静さんが置いてくれたグリーンは、なんだか活き活きして見えるんですよね。優しくて丁寧な愛情をしっかり受け取って育ってるのかな
The plants left by Shizuka-san are looking somewhat lovely. I wonder if it's because they were raised with kind, gentle affection poured into them?

Roka Suoh 		いつもたくさんお世話してくれてありがとう、って植物たちも言っているよ！　モチロン、僕と強行部のみんなも☆
The plants are also telling you thank you for always taking care of them! Of course, that includes me and everyone from the Watchdog Dept.

Kou Ayato 		真央お墨付きの激辛スナック、こんなもんで申し訳ないんですけど、誕生日ってことであげます。俺には辛すぎたんだけど、静さんならいけそうだなって
This is the super spicy snack approved by Mao. I'm sorry that it's something like this for your birthday. It was too spicy for me, but it should be fine for Shizuka-san.

Mao Ukyo 		静さんの好きなものって、動物と植物だっけ。‥‥‥いや、疲れてるのかもと思って。リラックスアイテムでも用意しようかな
Shizuka-san likes animals and plants, was it? .....No, I was thinking that you might be tired. I should prepare something relaxing.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		今度静さんちで寮飲みとかどうやろ？　なんならその前にサービスで部屋のお片付け‥‥‥は篠くんがやるかあ
How about we hold a dorm drinking party at Shizuka-san's room next time? If so, before that happens, I'm offering to tidy up your room...... or not. Shino-kun will be the one doing it huh.

Yuragi Kanno 		あっちの公園、花が咲いて綺麗だった。気分転換に良さそうだから、静に教えてあげる
The park over there has flowers blooming and it was beautiful. It seems nice for a change of pace, so I'll tell Shizuka about it.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		パソコンがフリーズして困った時も、神業のような速さで直していただいて。節見さんがいてくれるお陰で、安心して仕事ができています
He always fixes my frozen computer at a godly speed. I can work with peace of mind thanks to Fushimi-san being there.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		大河やら誓のＰＣ見にくる時にたまに会うんだけど、ちゃんと詳しいんだよな。下手に関連するもの渡すより、今度いるもんないか聞いてみるか
I meet him when he comes to check Taiga or Sei's PC sometimes. Rather than badly giving something related to it, I should try asking what he needs next time.

Sei Okiya 		いつも植物のお世話をありがとうございます。節見くんが礎を築いてくれたお陰で、今日も綺麗に咲き誇っていますよ
Thank you for always taking care of the plants. They are blooming beautifully thanks to the foundation laid by Fushimi-kun.

Shizuka Fushimi 		こういうのって祝われてるうちが華っていうけど、１人で静かに過ごせるならそっちの方が良くないか
They say a person should be happy being celebrated, but isn't it better to be able to quietly spend your time alone?

Takeru Mikado 		静の指示にはいつも助けられてる。私たちみんな感謝してて‥‥‥って、誕生日っていうより父の日とか、母の日みたいになっちゃうのよね
Shizuka's instructions are always very helpful. We are truly grateful...... Ah, that kinda sounded more like Father's Day or Mother's Day rather than a birthday.

Soyogu Shinkai 		篠信も姉御も盛り上がってんな。まあ、俺もこの日はただの普通の日とは思えねえしな
Shinobu and Anego are both getting excited. Well, I can't think of today as merely an ordinary day either.

Shinobu Aizawa 		静さん、おめでとうございます！　誕生日特別価格の掃除とケーキに加えて、今年はクラッカーもつけちゃいますけど、どうですか？
Shizuka-san, congratulations! I'm planning to use some crackers in addition to the birthday special price cleaning and the cake this year, what do you think?

Kiho Arima 		辛いものが好きなんだってね。ありったけの辛いものを用意してみたんだけど足りそうかな‥‥‥？
He's said to like spicy things. I tried to prepare as many spicy foods as I could though, will it be enough......?

Kyoya Shido 		節見くん、誕生日おめでとう。毎年スパイスじゃ芸がないと思って辛いソースにしたんだけど、もう誰かからもらってるかな。でもきっと、いくらあってもいいよね
Fushimi-kun, happy birthday. Giving you spice every year isn't exactly my style, so I'm giving you spicy sauce instead, I wonder if anyone has given you that? But I'm sure you're fine with having many of them.

Riku Tateshina 		いつも強行部っていう賑やかな部署をまとめてくれてるから、お祝いしたいと思ったけど、あの篠信たちのはしゃぎようを見たら‥‥‥。今日はそっとしておくか
I was thinking of celebrating you since you always manage the lively Watchdog Dept. well, but looking at how excited Shinobu and the others are...... I should leave you be today.

Tomose Onda 		今日は節見の誕生日か。知っているというよりは、相沢が浮かれていたからわかる
So today is Fushimi's birthday, Rather than knowing it, I could tell from seeing Aizawa being all excited.

Yu Nina 		節見のオペレーションは完璧とよく相沢から聞く。危険な依頼をこなす以上不可欠な存在なんだろう
I often hear from Aizawa about how perfect Fushimi's operations are. He must be an indispensable existence in completing dangerous requests.

Kamiya 		これ、結構話題になってたお片付けボックス。よかったら使ってみてください。‥‥‥あ、でも節見さんレベルだと意味ないのかな？　物増やしただけかも、すみません
This is the quite famous organizing box. Please try using it if you like...... Ah, but it might be meaningless for someone on Fushimi-san's level? It might only add more to the mess, I'm sorry.

Urara Manami 		なんでお前があいつらより落ち着いてんだよ。とにかく、騒がしいのはお前のせいだからな。責任持って黙らせろ
Why are you calmer than all of them? Anyway, they're being noisy because of you. Be responsible and shut them up.
