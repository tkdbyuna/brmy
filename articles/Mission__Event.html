Event Mission (イベントミッション, ibento misshon?) is a Events' mission, which can only be attempted during the event period. In Event Mission, they're also divided into Daily, Main, and Achievement. Besides that, they can also have an Extra Mission where the Main Mission has been cleared.

Types of Event Mission]

[Daily Mission] (ミッション・ディリー, Misshon/Dirī?)
Progress resets every day at 4:00 AM.
[Main Mission] (メインミッション, mein misshon?)
Appears according to your event gameplay progress.
※ Unlike Daily Missions, these do not reset.
[Achievement] (実績, jisseki?)
Appears according to your event gameplay progress.
※ Unlike Daily Missions, these do not reset.
[Extra Mission] (エクストラミッション, ekusutora misshon?)
Unlocked once you clear all Event Main Missions.
※ Depending on the format of the event, some missions, such as Extra or Main Missions, may not be available.

Mission Completion Rewards]

  • Press the [Receive] (受け取る, uketoru?, lit. to claim) button to claim your reward.
  • The [Receive All] (一括受け取り, ikkatsu uketori?, lit. bulk claim) button allows you to collect all completed rewards for each type of mission at once.
  • Mission rewards not claimed before the mission period ends will be sent to your Present Box the next time you log in.
    ※ Please note that rewards in the Present Box have an expiration date.
  • Missions whose rewards have already been claimed will move to the bottom of the list.
