Item Conversion]

Item Conversion (アイテム変換, Aitemu Henkan) is where you can convert one kind of item into another.

  • Supply
  • Essence
  • Break Limit Chip

Each attribute:

  • 1 Large Supply is exchanged for 3 Medium Supplies of equivalent attribute, and vice versa.
  • 1 Medium Supply is exchanged for 3 Small Supplies of equivalent attribute, and vice versa.
