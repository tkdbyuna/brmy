Sweets Land (スイーツランド, Suītsu Rando?) is a super rare spot featuring three or four characters interacting with each other.

There is only one fixed interacting action per department/trio/quartet; however, it yields an extra three snaps (SSR, SR, and R) when all characters appear in the snap.

Hence, it would be 12 snaps for a three-person department and 15 snaps for a four-person department.

From version 1.5.0, new trios and quartets are added to games; see also List of Sweets Land Snaps (2nd) for more details.

Requirements: the lineup card team should have the whole department.
