Characters Riku Tateshina's Birthday (1st)

Please be careful not to get into trouble involving women. I don't want to lose someone important to Aporia.

Happy birthday my bestie! Alright, Akke, give this guest the superb drink of ours. Of course, put it on my tab!

It's Riku-san's birthday and it certainly seems like he'd be really happy to get a message from you, but I think it's better to text carefully. He might misunderstand after all.

Tateshina-san's birthday, huh. Someone like him seems like a lonely person, so I should probably contact him through Chattask.
