Medal-Format Event (メダル形式イベント, medaru keishiki ibento?, lit. Medal‑Format Event) is the first type of Events, which is introduced in Florist: Garden of Words Soaked in Flowers event.

How to Play]

Play a normal puzzle song to earn Event Pt and Event Medal. The medal will be used to enter the limited event puzzle to earn more Event Pt.

Pts' drop rate in normal puzzle using Stamina

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.

Event Limited Playlist]

The event period is usually 12 days[Verification needed]:

  • Day 1: Side A and B of the event song, Side A of 2 previous event songs are released.
  • Day 5: Side C of the event song, Side A of 2 previous event songs are released.
  • Day 9: Side D of the event song, Side A of 2 previous event songs are released.

Hence, a total of 7 event-limited playlists are available to play since the 9th day.
