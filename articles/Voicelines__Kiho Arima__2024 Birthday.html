Characters Kiho Arima's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		たまに部屋の一点を見つめて黙っている時があるが‥‥‥ただ瞑想をしているのか、何か見えてるのか、未だに謎だ
There are times when he just stays silent and stares at a spot in the room...... It's still a mystery whether he's simply meditating or he's seeing something.

Yuzuru Kise 		誰にも分け隔てない芦佳さんですが、樹帆さんといるときは一層気を許しているような気がします
Roka-san is considerate towards everyone, but I feel like he lets his guard down even more when he's with Kiho-san.

Roka Suoh 		樹帆を祝うのも何年目かな。そしてこの先、あと何回祝えるだろう。未来はわからないのが醍醐味だ、ワクワクしちゃうね！
For how many years have I celebrated Kiho? And how many times will I be able to celebrate him after this? Not knowing the future is a real thrill—it makes me excited!

Kou Ayato 		樹帆さんって、こういう日何するんだろ。ケーキのろうそく吹き消すところとか、ガチで想像できない
I wonder what Kiho-san does on a day like this. I seriously can't imagine him blowing out candles on a cake.

Mao Ukyo 		樹帆さんが店に来ると、その一角だけ雰囲気の濃度がガラッと変わるんだよね。これ伝わる？
When Kiho-san comes, the atmosphere usually changes drastically in that one corner. Does this describe it well?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		樹帆さんの占いってなんやすごいんやろ？　他人のこと見るのって疲れるやろし、誕生日はゆっくりすごせるとええなあ
Kiho-san's fortune telling is somehow amazing, isn't it? I guess it's tiring to look at other people, so I hope he can spend his birthday leisurely.

Yuragi Kanno 		樹帆はなんか、一緒にいるといい匂いする。あれは動物に好かれる匂い‥‥‥
Kiho somehow has a nice smell when we're together. That's the smell of someone liked by animals ......

Taiga Tsukimoto 		在間さんにはいつも世話になってるからな、次の部長会でなにか贈るか‥‥‥。で、何にするかだが、悩ましいな‥‥‥
We've always been in Arima-san's care, so I should give something to him at the next dept. leader meeting...... Though I said that, it's difficult to decide......

Haruhi Ichikawa 		樹帆さんって怒るとかあるの？　想像できな
Has Kiho-san ever been angry? I can't imagine it.

Sei Okiya 		樹帆さん、誕生日おめでとうございます。あと何回だって、この言葉を貴方に贈りたいものですねえ
Kiho-san, happy birthday. I want to say these words to you again and again.

Shizuka Fushimi 		論理的ではないこと言うけど、在間さんは歳を取らないって言われても、妙に納得感あるよな
I'm saying something illogical, but if someone told me that Arima-san doesn't age, I'd strangely be convinced.

Takeru Mikado 		樹帆の目を信じてるから、こうやって付いてきてるのよ。‥‥‥今日はスペシャルなカクテルを作ってあげる♡
I believe in Kiho's eyes—that's why I keep following him like this...... I'll make him a special cocktail for today ♡

Soyogu Shinkai 		樹帆さんは近寄り難さ半分、不思議な引力があるよな‥‥‥
While Kiho-san is partially difficult to approach, he strangely has an attractive force......

Shinobu Aizawa 		お誕生日おめでとうございます！　樹帆さんって永遠にあの見た目って言われても納得しちゃう感じ、わかります？
Happy birthday! I'd be convinced if someone told me that Kiho-san will look like that forever, do you get that?

Kiho Arima 		誕生日‥‥‥ああ、確かに今日だね。今の仕事に就いてから、そんなに意味を見出してないだけで、祝ってもらえるともちろん嬉しいよ
Birthday...... Ahh, it certainly is today. It doesn't have that much meaning now that I'm working in this field, but of course, I'm happy getting celebrated.

Kyoya Shido 		樹帆さん誕生日おめでとうございます。安らぎの時間を過ごしてほしいという願いを込めて、お香とミルクパンを贈ります
Kiho-san, happy birthday. I'm giving you incense and milk bread, including my wish for you to spend a peaceful time.

Riku Tateshina 		樹帆の幼少期ってどんな感じ？　生まれた瞬間から落ち着いてそうだよね、いや交渉部全員、口から生まれてきたまであるな
How was Kiho during childhood? He seems to be calm since he was born, no each of the Negotiation Dept. can be said to be born with a big mouth.

Tomose Onda 		占いというのは文化的に馴染みがある。‥‥‥が、俺自身は強い興味はない
He is culturally familiar with fortune telling...... Though I don't have a strong interest in that.

Yu Nina 		樹帆の誕生日か。あの独特の雰囲気‥‥‥飲まれる奴は多いだろうな
Kiho's birthday. That unique atmosphere of his...... I guess it can engulf lots of people.

Kamiya 		樹帆さんには感謝してもしきれない。芦佳さんと樹帆さんは今の俺にとって父と母って感じ。どっちがどっちかは秘密！
I can't thank Kiho-san enough. Roka-san and Kiho-san are like a father and a mother to the current me. Who is which is a secret!

Urara Manami 		はあ‥‥‥まじで苦手なんだよ、あの目‥‥‥
Hahh... I'm seriously bad with those eyes......
