Description]

An ex-staff member who used to work in Negotiation Dept., but quickly resigned after a certain event. Liking humans makes him act friendly, causing people to often get fooled on the first sight. He is a dangerous person who is currently "banned" from Aporia.[1]

Etymology]

  • 天羽 Amou consists of 天 ten and 羽 hane
    • 天 ten means heaven
    • 羽 hane means wings
  • 嵐 Ran (commonly read as arashi) means a storm

Trivia]

  • He was first mentioned in (Watchdogs) Prequel -Embracing Tenacious Passion- where Riku mentioned that he was quitting.
  • His last name was revealed when Ai mentioned him again in Prequel: Dinner on the Night of Near-Full Moon.
  • Ai described Ran as a troublemaker as he kept making Riku took care of him, though he admits that Ran has an affinity for negotiation.
  • Ai also mentioned how drastic Ran and Soyogu's physiques are.
  • His back is featured in the Thread2. Piece of CAKE Key Visual, which was released on March 22nd, 2025.
  • His first official appearance is Thread2. Piece of CAKE Episode 8.
