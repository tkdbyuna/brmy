Description]

Rational leader of watchdogs
A man who values order and loves silence. Doesn't like to interact with people and wants to be surrounded by plants instead. He cares about and is trusted by his comrades. He doesn't leave the shared space dirty, though his own territory is very dirty.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Watchdog Dept.[2] Shizuka Fushimi 💻 & Takeru Mikado 🌹 & Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

While they have individual friendships, they primarily build relationships as a group of four. When someone suggests going out for drinks with two others, it usually becomes a group invitation for the other two as well. They have immense trust in Fushimi as our leader who makes sound judgments and gives clear direction, yet they maintain a healthy dynamic where everyone feels comfortable voicing their opinions.

  • New and Former Department Head[3]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

The former and current heads of the Watchdog Dept.. They share the responsibility of protecting the other members. Though their preferences and lifestyles are completely different, so they don't spend their days off together, they have many opportunities to talk and share various information. They both find each other dependable.

  • Watchdog Department’s Intellectual Pair[4]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

In the same department and living in the same dorm, these two are fellow intellectuals and neighbours. If one were to say what work they are in charge of, it would be “stillness” and “motion” respectively. Despite being total opposites, they have a mutual respect for one another. Even from those within Aporia, Fushimi is someone Shinkai holds in particularly high regard.

  • Lifeline[5]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

Though he seems indifferent, Fushimi somehow always looks out for his comrades, and loyal dog Aizawa adores him for it. As the department head, Fushimi protects Aizawa's safety daily, while Aizawa regularly supports Fushimi's life by cleaning his room and doing his laundry for a fee.

  • Mama and the Owner[6]: Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙 and Shizuka Fushimi 💻

Fushimi regularly comes to check on the plants and flower beds that are growing in the House. Watering is on a rotation system, but since Tsukimoto is in charge of managing the plants, they frequently communicate for questions regarding seasonal repotting and maintenance. They also rely on each other when there are issues around the PC, so they unexpectedly have quite a bit of interaction.
