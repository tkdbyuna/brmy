Christmas 2024

  • Release Period: December 10, 2024 00:00 JST - December 25, 2024 23:59 JST

Translated by Misora

Characters Christmas 2024

Ai Kosaka 		歩くモミの木に出会った事はあるか？　俺はある。‥‥‥変な顔をするな、最後まで聞け。それがどでかいモミの木を担いで事務所に向かう芦佳で、もちろん締め出した
Have you ever seen a walking fir tree? I have...... Don't make a weird face, listen until the end. It was when Roka tried to carry a very big fir tree to the agency, and of course, I locked him out.

Yuzuru Kise 		ミカさんがお菓子のアドベントカレンダーをくれたんです。毎日１つずつ開けるのが楽しくて、毎朝はしゃいでるんです
Mika-san gave me a sweets advent calendar. Opening one by one every day is so fun that I get excited every morning.

Roka Suoh 		あと何回寝たらクリスマスかな！？　待つ時間も楽しいなんて、最高のイベントだね！
How many times do I have to sleep to get to Christmas!? To think that the waiting itself is fun, what an amazing event it is!

Kou Ayato 		この時期はイルミネーション綺麗でいいよな。バイク乗ってるとき特に思うわ
The illuminations during this time are beautiful. I especially feel so when I'm on my bike.

Mao Ukyo 		継ぎ足し完了っと。揺がさ、アドベントカレンダー初日に全部開けちゃって。なのにもう無いって拗ねるから、補充してんの
Refill complete. You know, Yuragi opened the whole advent calendar on the first day and is now sulking because there's no more of it, that's why I'm refilling them.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		今夜、君が１人で占領してくれてもええんやで。特別に９０％OFF‥‥‥って言ったら陛下にやられるやろな
You can occupy me for the night, you know? I'll especially give you 90% OFF...... but if I said that His Majesty would come and get me.

Yuragi Kanno 		このお菓子のアドベントカレンダー。全部開けたはずなのに、今見たらまた詰まってた。魔法の箱かも
I should've had opened the whole advent calendar but now that I see it again, it still has sweets. It might be a magic box.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		今日はやたらと誓さんからの視線を感じるな‥‥‥食べたいクリスマス料理があるとか‥‥‥？　なんなんだ一体
Somehow I've been feeling the stare from Sei-san today...... Does he have any Christmas menu he wants to eat...... ? What exactly is his deal?

Haruhi Ichikawa 		リア充視聴者はいなくなるから同接減りそうだけど‥‥‥ロンリーなやつらのために配信するか
The riajuu viewers will be gone so my view count would surely go down but....... may be I should do a stream for the lonely ones.

Sei Okiya 		２人の枕元にプレゼントを置こうと思っているんです。こういうのは夢が大切ですからね、隙をうかがって完璧に遂行して見せますよ‥‥‥
I'm thinking of placing a present on their bedside. It's important to keep their dreams for this, I shall wait for an opportunity and make sure it's executed perfectly.

Shizuka Fushimi 		芦佳さんから「良い子に」ってプレゼントもらったんだけど‥‥‥“良い”も“子”も怪しくないか？
I got a present from Roka-san saying that "it's for a good kid"... Isn’t "good" and "kid" a bit suspicious?

Takeru Mikado 		こっちが静でこれが戦と篠信‥‥‥貰うのも嬉しいけど贈るのも楽しいわ。何色のリボンにしましょうか
This one is for Shizuka, and these are for Soyogu and Shinobu... I'm happy to receive but giving is also fun. What color of ribbons should I use?

Soyogu Shinkai 		どこもかしこもイルミネーションがピカピカ浮かれてっけど。ま、暗い夜道よりは安心じゃねえか？
The illuminations are shining bright everywhere...... Well, I guess it's safer than dark night streets?

Shinobu Aizawa 		クリスマスってイベントとしては準備が本番ってか、正直当日にはもう締め作業って感じですよね～
Christmas as an event feels like the preparation being the actual event and the actual day being the closing, right~

Kiho Arima 		メリークリスマス。こんな所にいていいのかな。君ならたくさんのお誘いがあったんじゃないかと思って
Merry Christmas. Is it okay for you to stay here? I was thinking that you must've received many invitations.

Kyoya Shido 		イルミネーションで街が煌めいているね、気分も明るくなるよ。この高揚感を君と分かち合いたいな
Seeing the illuminations sparkling in the streets brighten up my mood. I want to share this excitement with you.

Riku Tateshina 		メリークリスマス。まだ誰の誘いも受けてないっていうことなら、その奇跡に縋らせてもらっていい？
Merry Christmas. If you haven't accepted anyone's invitation, can I hold on to that miracle?

Tomose Onda 		プレゼントが欲しいとは思わないが、そうだな。幸福を願う者のもとに、それがあるといい
I don't think I want any presents but right. I hope it comes to those who wish for happiness.

Yu Nina 		クリスマスだな。客が浮き立ってるが、なにかあるのか？
It's Christmas. The customers are excited, is there something?

Kamiya 		やっぱり今年も大忙しになりそうな予感‥‥‥！　準備運動してからホールに出ないとな‥‥‥
I knew it would be super busy again this year......! I have to go to the hall after warming up......

Urara Manami 		どこもかしこも浮かれやがって。祭りがしたいだけだろうが
Everyone's getting excited just cause they want to do festivals.
