Play Activity

Using normal stamina, play puzzles to earn Event Pt Kesaran pasaran to exchange rewards.
In the Prequel series, puzzles in event-exclusive playlists that earn a lot of Event Pts can also be played with normal stamina.
Event Pts can be earned in the playlists for targeted staff and targeted attributes (See more in Event Bonus) throughout the event.

During the first half of the event, only the new event song stages are playable.
In the second half, new playlists are added to the event-exclusive playlist. Rating SS will enable adding the song to Juke Box.

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina players use will multiply the drop rate of Pts, but not Card Level, Nice points, and Items drop.
