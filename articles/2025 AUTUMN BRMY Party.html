2025 AUTUMN BRMY Party

2025 AUTUMN BRMY Party (2025 AUTUMN ブレマイパーティ, 2025 AUTUMN Buremai Pāti?, lit. 2025 AUTUMN Bremai Party) is a Gacha Event happens alongside with the BMC RMX FES Vol.2 Event.

The cards illustrated by Utako Yukihiro, the game's character designer, for Taiga Tsukimoto and Yu Nina are introduced.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.

Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): September 10, 2025 18:00 JST - September 16, 2025 15:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "2025 AUTUMN BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 draws for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 draws for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.3)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.

※Note: For STEP 3 & 4, you also need to draw 10 gacha to earn the rewards.
