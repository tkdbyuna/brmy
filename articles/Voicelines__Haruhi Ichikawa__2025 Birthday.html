Characters Haruhi Ichikawa's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		壱川のデザインは評判がいい。良いクリエイターを迎え入れるきっかけが、新開ということだけが癪だな
Ichikawa's designs are well-received. It's annoying that Shinkai was the one who recruited him in.

Yuzuru Kise 		お誕生日おめでとう。壱川くんのセンスにいつもAporiaは助けられています。どんなものが喜んでもらえるか悩んで‥‥‥健康に過ごしてほしいからホットアイマスク、よかったら使って
Happy birthday. Aporia has always been helped by Ichikawa-kun's sense of design. I tried thinking about what will make you happy‥‥‥ and I want you to be healthy, so please use these hot eye masks.

Roka Suoh 		るんるんは中々外に出られないと聞くからね！　プレゼントはおうち時間を楽しめるようなものがいいはず！　室内用滑り台とかどう！？
I heard Runrun doesn't really go outside! Something that can make you enjoy yourself at home should be good! How about an indoor slide!?

Kou Ayato 		いつも揺がお世話になってます。壱川を労わることも考えたんだけど、きっと揺に吸われまくってるおこげの方にも感謝しないとなと思ったから。これ、いい感じそうな猫のおやつ
Yuragi has always been in your care. I do think of you, Ichikawa, but I thought I must also be thanking Okoge, who has always been huffed by Yuragi. Here are some nice cat treats.

Mao Ukyo 		僕の周りでもdemuが好きって話を聞くことあるよ。あんまり触れると何か口走るのが怖いから、軽く流してるけど、知り合いがここまで有名なのってすごいことだよね
l've heard people around me liking demu. I'm scared I might slip it out if I talk about it, so I usually brush it off, but having a famous acquaintance is sure amazing.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		俺もハウスにお祝いにって言うたら、ゆらに怒られそうやしおめでとうは会えた時に伝えることにするわ
Yuragi might get mad if I say I'm joining the celebration at the House, so I'll just congratulate him when I meet him.

Yuragi Kanno 		いい猫カフェ見つけたから、今度春日と行きたい。人もそんな多くないし、気に入ると思う
I found a nice cat café, and I want to visit it with Haruhi next time. I think he will like it since there aren't too many people.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		春日は今日を思う存分謳歌してますよ。自分の怠惰が容認される日としてというのが９割方な気もしますが、本人が楽しめてるならいいか、と
Haruhi is enjoying today to its fullest. I feel like 90% of it is because this is the day when his laziness is tolerated, but I guess it's fine as long as he is happy.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		歳取ろうが減ろうが興味ないよね。本人がこのスタンスなのに、本人以上に盛り上がってるふたりは本当元気すぎるわ
I don't care about getting older or younger. I'm like this, but those two who are getting more worked up than me myself are truly energetic.

Sei Okiya 		一緒に住んでいる特権は、直接祝うことが容易なところですね。今年もそばで、君を見守らせていただければと思います
The privilege of living together is how easy it is to celebrate in person. I hope to be watching over you by your side this year as well.

Shizuka Fushimi 		壱川の誕生日か。たまにハウスに行く程度だけど、どんな感じで祝われてるのかは大体想像できるな
Ichikawa's birthday. I only visit the House sometimes, but I can already imagine how he's getting celebrated.

Takeru Mikado 		戦は『体力をつけるための体力をつけるメニュー』だって組んでくれると思うから、必要かもって思った時はぜひ声をかけてみて
I think Soyogu can make a "physical strength building for physical strength building program" for you, so feel free to call him whenever you think you might need it.

Soyogu Shinkai 		まさか誕生日を祝う仲になるとは、面白い縁だな。これからも仲間として、よろしく
I didn't think we'd end up in a relationship where we celebrate each other's birthday, I'll be in your care as a comrade from now on too.

Shinobu Aizawa 		壱川さん基本ハウスから出てこないから、直接おめでとうは言えなそうだよなー。驚かれてもいいからチャッタス送っとこっと！
Ichikawa-san doesn't really leave the House, so it doesn't seem like I can congratulate him directly~ It might surprise him but I should just congratulate him through Chattask!

Kiho Arima 		いつも素敵なクリエイティブをありがとう。壱川くんの作品あってこそのAporiaだよ。気が向いたときはぜひカフェにもきてね
Thank you for your wonderful creativity Ichikawa-kun's creations also make up Aporia. Come visit the café whenever you feel like it.

Kyoya Shido 		今日はdemuじゃなくて春日くんの誕生日だったよね。デザインの参考になりそうな企画展があるんだけど、興味持ってくれるかな
Today is not demu's but Haruhi-kun's birthday, yes? There's a special exhibition that might give him design inspirations, I wonder if he'll be interested?

Riku Tateshina 		春日がAporiaに来た時に本当たまに写真見てもらうことがあってね。いつもズバズバ意見をいただけてありがたいですよ
When Haruhi first came to Aporia, I had him look at my pictures sometimes. I'm grateful that he always gave me his honest opinions.

Tomose Onda 		『誕生日補正で神引き』の意味は何一つわからないが、めでたいことならなによりだ
I don't know what "godly birthday buffed pull" means, but good for you.

Yu Nina 		以前のドレスアップイベントで壱川のデザインを気に入っている客がいた
A customer from a previous dress-up event liked Ichikawa's design.

Kamiya 		壱川さんのデザイン、すごいよなあ。恭耶さんほど美術に詳しくなくても、魅入っちゃうぐらい！
Ichikawa-san's designs are amazing. It's so captivating even for me, who's not as knowledgeable in art as Kyoya-san!

Urara Manami 		無駄に他人と顔合わせずにすむのは悪くねえな。けど家こもってジッとしてるだけとか逆に身体壊れそうだ
Being able to avoid unnecessary encounters with other people doesn't sound bad. But staying inside the house without doing anything might ruin the body instead.
