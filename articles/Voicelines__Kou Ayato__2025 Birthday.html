Characters Kou Ayato's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		誕生日おめでとう。恋を祝う人は多いだろうから俺の言葉は不要とも思ったが。そういうものではないと、前に言われたからな
Happy birthday. I know there are lots of people celebrating you, Kou. Though you shouldn't need my greeting, I'll say it since you told me it's nothing like that at all.

Yuzuru Kise 		恋くんの誕生日に一緒にいたら、お知り合いらしい方が『誕生日を一緒に過ごせたとしても別に本命いるからやめときな』って。その‥‥‥優しい方ですよね
When I was together with Kou-kun on his birthday, a person who seemed to be his acquaintance came up, and they told me, "even if you celebrate his birthday together, he already has someone he's serious about so it's better to give up." That's...... very kind of them.

Roka Suoh 		こーたすには映画のチケットを贈ろうかと思ったけど、観たいものはもう観てるだろうし‥‥‥これは、オリジナル映画を撮影するしか！？
I was thinking of giving Koutasu some movie tickets, but I bet he already watched those he wanted to watch...... Is this my cue to make an original movie!?

Kou Ayato 		誕生日プレゼント？　ありがとう、今見てもいい？　‥‥‥防犯ブザー？　これ絶対明星の入れ知恵でしょ。はは、まあいいや、大事に付けさせてもらうから見るたび後悔してよ
A birthday present? Thanks, can I open it now? ......A safety alarm? You must've gotten the idea from Akehoshi. Haha, well, I'll use it with care so you feel regret every time you see it.

Mao Ukyo 		せっかくだし今日ご飯でも行く？　いや、カモフラージュっていうか普通に友人‥‥‥同僚として？
Since there's an occasion, why don't we go eat together today? No, not as a camouflage, but as friends...... or more like colleagues?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		今月、平和に乗り切れそうって言うたら恋くん喜ぶかな。あと１週間油断しないようにせえへんと
Will Kou-kun be glad if I told him this month might end peacefully? I have to make sure I don't let my guard down for another week.

Yuragi Kanno 		恋のスマホ、すごい連絡きてそうだった。でも誰にも会わないんだって。誕生日に会いたいって思わせるようなことしたのは、多分恋なのに
I saw lots of notifications coming to Kou's phone, but he said he won't be meeting anyone. Even though the one who made those people want to meet him on his birthday was probably himself.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		話に聞く感じ、綾戸くん自身は少女漫画のヒーローっぽいのに、モテ方が行きすぎてて最早ラブコメっぽいというか、ギャグっぽいというか‥‥‥
From what live heard, Ayato-kun's way of getting his popularity is so similar to the male leads in shoujo manga, making it look like he's in a romcom or sort of a gag......

Haruhi Ichikawa 		揺から聞く限り、交際部ってほんとすごいとこだよね。それを綾戸さんがまとめてると思うと、尊敬しかない
The Simulation Dept. I've heard from Yuragi is very amazing. I can only feel respect towards Ayato-san as he's the one putting everything together.

Sei Okiya 		映画が好きな恋くんに、僭越ながら私のおすすめを‥‥‥とも思ったのですが。祝う気があるのかと思わせてしまいそうなので、別の方法でお祝いしますね
Although it might be presumptous of me, I'd like to give my recommendation to Kou-kun who likes movies..... was what I was thinking. I'm afraid it might make him think that I wanted to celebrate much, so let's do it another way.

Shizuka Fushimi 		綾戸ならプレゼントのリクエストし放題だと思うけど、本当に欲しいものからは外して伝えるタイプだろあれ
I think Ayato gets to request any presents he wants to get though, he must be the type to tell anything except what he exactly wants.

Takeru Mikado 		無事に今年も誕生日を迎えられたようでよかったわ。モテるのは仕方ないけど、ある程度でセーブしておきなさいよ
I'm glad you're able to celebrate your birthday again this year. It can't be helped that you're popular, but do make sure you keep it safe to a certain extent.

Soyogu Shinkai 		洒落てない飯と酒でもよかったら、祝い飲みしましょうよ。そういうのも恋さん楽しんでくれるって知ってるんで
Let's go drink together to celebrate if you're okay with not so stylish food and alcohol. I know Kou-san also enjoys that kind of stuff.

Shinobu Aizawa 		一緒に遊んでもらってる時、俺でもやっぱ恋さんかっこいいなーってなるから、みんなメロメロになるの、仕方ないです。刺されないようにだけ気をつけてください！
Since I always become more sure of how cool Kou-san is every time we play together, I understand why people can't help but become infatuated with you. Just be careful not to get stabbed!

Kiho Arima 		恋は人気者だからね。こういう時はいろんな人に囲まれてるんだ。タイミング見てお祝いしよう
Kou is popular, so he gets to be surrounded by a lot of people during this kind of time. I should find the perfect timing to celebrate.

Kyoya Shido 		お誕生日おめでとう。樹帆さんや吏来さんとはまた違う付き合いをさせてもらえていつも楽しいよ。またお祝いに、食事に誘わせてもらおうかな
Happy birthday. Hanging out with you has a different feeling compared to when I hang out with Kiho-san and Riku-san, and I always have fun when we do. May I invite you to a meal for another celebration?

Riku Tateshina 		恋みたいなタイプって何が欲しいかわからないし、人からのプレゼントで埋もれてそうだから悩むな。無難に映画の電子チケットか‥‥‥
It's hard to know what people like Kou wants, yet he'd also get buried by a lot of presents, making it even harder. The safest choice would be an e-ticket for a movie......

Tomose Onda 		お祝いに何か映画を薦めてほしい、か。綾戸にとって目新しいものが提案できるかはわからないが、検討してみよう
He wanted me to recommend him some movies for his birthday. I don't know if the things I recommend would be new for Ayato, but let's see.

Yu Nina 		以前宇京に説教されている姿を見た。交際部は良いバランスを保っているんだな
I saw him getting lectured by Ukyo before. The Simulation Dept. does maintain a good balance.

Kamiya 		恋さんの誕生日か～。なんでもありがとってスマートに受け取ってくれそうだけど、たまには嬉し驚きみたいな反応見たいよね？　ドンピシャ欲しいもの当てたいな～
Kou-san's birthday, huh~ He would gratefully accept anything though, it makes me want to see him getting nicely surprised sometimes, you know? I'd like to guess correctly what he exactly wants~

Urara Manami 		映画の電子ギフト券を送っといた。‥‥‥チッ。貰いっぱなしは性に合わねえだけだ
I sent a gift e-voucher for movies. ......Tch, I don't like being a person who only receives.
