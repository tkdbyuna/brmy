Play Activity

Play a normal puzzle song to earn Pt Aporia bouquet and Bouquet Medal. The medal will be used to enter the limited event puzzle to earn more Pt .

Pts' drop rate in normal puzzle using Stamina

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.

The amount of Medals will multiply the drop rate of Pts, but card level, coins, and drops are still 1 times.
