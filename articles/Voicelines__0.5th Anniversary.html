Characters 0.5th Anniversary

Ai Kosaka 		記念日を大切にしろだなんだ言われてるのを思い出した‥‥‥はあ。まあなんだ、食事でも行くか。お前との写真を送るように言われてる、例のアレだ
I remembered getting told to cherish anniversaries‥‥‥ Hahh. Well, should we go have a meal? I was told to send a picture of you and me, you know that certain case.

Yuzuru Kise 		貴方が来てもう半年なんですね。いえいえ、お世話になったのは俺のセリフですよ。これからもどうぞ、よろしくお願いします
It's already half a year since you came. No, no, I'm the one who has been helped a lot by you. I'll be in your care from now on too.

Roka Suoh 		君にとってもう半年？　まだ半年？　どっちであってもおめでたい日に違いない！　皆の者、パーティの準備を！
Does it feel already half a year or still half a year for you? Whichever it is, there's no doubt that it's an auspicious day! Everyone, prepare for a party!

Kou Ayato 		慣れない仕事と癖のあるメンツでって結構心配してたんだけど、案外逞しいのな。これからも頼りにするし、頼っていいから
I was quite worried about you doing work you're not used to together with unique people but you're surprisingly tough. From now on, I'll keep relying on you, and you can rely on me.

Mao Ukyo 		君も大分業務に慣れてきたと思ったら、そんなに経つんだ。これからもよろしく
I thought that you've pretty much gotten used to the job, and it turns out that such time has passed. Keep it up from now on too.

Anniversaries are something that girls like— was what I thought but as expected, I'm also happy for today. Isn't this a big growth?

Half a year..... Eh, are you going to give me something for that‥‥‥? Ah, the opposite huh, I should be the one who give something?

So it's been half a year since then, time really went by in a blink of an eye. Please feel free to visit Administrative Dept. anytime, we will be in our care from now on too.

I thought you endured it well for half a year, but being able to get by among these oddballs means that you're pretty much the same.

Sei Okiya 		実にめでたいことです。大河だけでなく春日までああも歩み寄る、そんな貴方をもっと知りたい‥‥‥。いえ、独り言です
It's truly a joyous occasion. I want to know more about you who can make Taiga, even Haruhi to compromise that much...... No, I'm just talking to myself.

It's not something to get sentimental about. Half a year is just a blink of an eye, it's fine to celebrate but let's make our next fun plan.
