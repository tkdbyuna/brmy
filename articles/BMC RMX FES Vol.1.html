Play Activity]

During the event period, stages featuring 3 REMIX tracks will be added daily to the event-exclusive playlist.

Playing these stages will earn you large quantities of event items 'Notes', usable at the Exchange Shop.

※ For BMC RMX FES Vol.1, you can play the event-exclusive playlist using regular stamina instead of event medals.
