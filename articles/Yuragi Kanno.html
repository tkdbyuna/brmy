Description]

0 or 100 ultimate moody person
An ultimate moody person. The way to swing it is 0 or 100, when in a good mood, he'll get along nicely with you, but when in a bad mood, he'll lash out at you when you try taking care of him. Likes cats and gets along well with Okoge, the cat kept in Administrative Dept.'s house, and also the cat community in the neighborhood.[1]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Simulation Dept.[2]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ & Mao Ukyo 🧩 & Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Yuragi Kanno 🍬

They have more opportunities to work together compared to other departments, and they socialize outside of work too, but the sense of unity and camaraderie within their department is weak. Plans for fun activities like "Let's do something with the four of us from the Simulation Dept." rarely materialize, but if the four of them do end up going out, they can still enjoy themselves.

  • Cat and Dog Duo[3]: Yuragi Kanno 🍬 and Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️

Due to aligning and timing circumstances, both share a room in the dorm. Although the dog-like Hinomiya and cat-like Kanno aren’t hostile to one another, they’re not particularly close either, and maintain a distance such that they don’t interfere too deeply in each other’s affairs. According to Hinomiya, “It’s kinda like I have a one-sided crush".

  • Excursion Buddies[4]: Yuzuru Kise🍴 & Mao Ukyo🧩 & Yuragi Kanno🍬

Kise, who gets along equally well with everyone except Kosaka, is in fact remarkably close to Ukyo and Kanno. On their days off, they go to buffets, go shopping, or spend time at the park. It's a peaceful relationship where they take turns fulfilling each other's wishes.

  • Cat Friend[5]: Yuragi Kanno 🍬 and Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧

The day after hearing that "a cat (and a new staff member living with it) had arrived at the House," Kanno made an unannounced visit. This sparked an instant connection between the two cat lovers. Even now, Kanno frequently visits the house to see Ichikawa's pet cat, Okoge.

Strawberry Shake / ストロベリーシェイク
Japanese イチゴと牛乳を混ぜた可愛らしいシェイク。優しくまろやかな口当たりのソフトドリンクとなっており、お酒が飲めない方にもおすすめ。要望があれば、ホイップやチョコスプレーの量を追加するカスタマイズも可能
English A lovely shake made from the mix of strawberry and milk. A soft drink with a gentle and mellow taste, recommended for those who can't drink alcohol. If you wish, you can add more whipped cream or chocolate spray.
Cream Soda* / クリームソーダ
Japanese 前、明星が得意げに『大人のクリームソーダ』を出してきたから、何かと思ったけど、アルコールが入ってるのをそう呼ぶらしいよ。‥‥‥俺は、アルコールよりアイスの方が嬉しい
English A while ago, Akehoshi proudly served an "Adult's Cream Soda". I wondered what it was, but it turns out that it's the name for cream soda with alcohol added in. ……For me, adding ice would be much better than adding alcohol.
Chocolate Holiday* / チョコレートホリデー
Japanese アメリカのチョコって、カラフルなのが多い。この前芦佳からもらったお土産を食べてたら、舌が変な色になった。喋る度に真央と明星に笑われたけど、甘くて美味しかったから、また食べたい
English America's chocolates are mostly colorful. My tongue turned a weird color when I ate a souvenir I received from Roka a while ago. Mao and Akehoshi kept laughing at me every time I talked, but it was sweet and good, so I want to eat it again.
