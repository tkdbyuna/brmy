  • 1 Limited Ingredients

Ingredients are materials collected from playing puzzles in Café-Bar event. These materials will then be used to create recipe, which are drinks, cocktails, etc. Then these recipes are served to be collected EN.

For cocktails, the core ingredient is base liquor (spirit), and other support ingredients such as: mixers, sweeteners, citrus & acids, bitters & aromatics, garnishes, etc. This provides the alcohol content and dominant flavor profile, while other ingredients like mixers, syrups, citrus, and bitters are added to balance and enhance the drink.
