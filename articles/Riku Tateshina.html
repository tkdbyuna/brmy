Description]

Instable counselor with love predisposition
A freelance counselor with a psychologist certification. He is elusive, and it's unknown whether he has motivation or not. Cannot live without being in love and severely infatuated. He has a special concern for Kosaka.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Negotiation Dept.[2]: Kiho Arima 🔮 & Kyoya Shido 🎨 & Riku Tateshina 🥃

Though they appear to be a collection of individualists, they actually get along quite well. They maintain a comfortable distance, meeting regularly for drinks as a department activity, allowing them to interact without awkwardness. They enjoy talking and drinking together, occasionally going on trips as well.

  • New and Old Emperors[3]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Riku Tateshina 🥃

Kosaka, who always acts as the top authority, asks for advice from the respected older Tateshina. He trusts Tateshina’s humanity and way of working, and Tateshina is also fond of Kosaka. Although they are not blood-related, they are like siblings.

  • Friendship Between Adults[4]: Riku Tateshina 🥃 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

Besides being starting members, they are alike in that they are both indebted to Suoh. Because they are different types of people, they can view each other clearly and support each other appropriately. Despite the age difference, it is an equal friendship between adults.
