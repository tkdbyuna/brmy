Characters 1st Anniversary

Ai Kosaka 		大事なのはこの１年で何を成し遂げたかだ。‥‥‥その成果については評価してる
What you accomplished in this one year is what's important..... And I appreciate your results.

Yuzuru Kise 		出会って１年、貴方にとっても俺たちにとってもいろんなことがありましたね。なんて、思い出を語るには少し早いでしょうか
A year since our meeting, a lot has happened to both you and us. However, I guess it's still a bit soon to get nostalgic.

Roka Suoh 		一緒に君と過ごした１年を振り返りたいんだが、立ち話もなんだろう‥‥‥どこかカフェでも入らない？　僕のおすすめは、やっぱりAporia！
I'd like to trace back the one year I've spent with you, but it'll take a while...... Why don't we enter a café? My recommendation is, of course, Aporia!

Kou Ayato 		もう１年経つんだ。真央や明星とも仲良さげだし、何より揺が割と懐いてるじゃん。マジですごいことだよ？　‥‥‥俺は、さあ、どうかな～
So a year has passed. You've got along well with Mao and Akehoshi. In addition, Yuragi is quite attached to you. It's really an amazing thing, you know? ......For me, well, I wonder~

Mao Ukyo 		君がここに来た時、色んな意味で不安だった。仕事内容も人も変わった場所だし、慣れるのかなって。でも、今はそんなこと思ってないから
I was uneasy in so many ways when you first came here. It's a place with a strange job description and people, so I was worried about whether you can get used to it or not. But I don't think of that anymore now.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		君が来てからの１年、すっごい楽しいこと多かってん。来年は何したいとかある？　俺がもっと楽しませるから任せてな
A lot of very fun things have happened since you came here last year. Do you have anything you want to do next year? I'll entertain you even more, so leave it to me.

Yuragi Kanno 		あんたが来て１年か。たくさんお世話になりました。これは本当に思ってるんだけど
A year since you came. I've been in your care a lot. That's what I really think.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		貴方がいると、この家が賑わいますね。春日も誓さんも楽しみにしてるんで‥‥‥あの、もちろん俺も。だから、また来てください
This House becomes lively when you're here. Haruhi and Sei-san is looking forward to your visit...... And, of course, me too. That's why, please come again.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		もう１年か‥‥‥。日付の感覚ないのは置いといて、あんまそんな感じしない。馴染んでるってことなんじゃない？
It's already one year...... Me having no sense of date aside, I don't really feel that. Doesn't that mean you've become quite familiar to?

Sei Okiya 		この１年、貴方が来てくれたおかげで随分と楽しいものになりました。これからも、よろしくお願いしますよ
Thanks to you coming here, this one year has been a lot of fun. I'll be in your care from now on too.

Shizuka Fushimi 		１年か、これからも心身の健康第一でよろしく。そういうのってちゃんと意識しないと大切にできないもんだから
A year, huh. Please put both your mental and physical health first from now on, too. Those are something you'd neglect without realising after all.

Takeru Mikado 		この１年、アンタは自信をつけてどんどん綺麗になった。２年目ももっと美しくなるんでしょうね。側で見守らせてちょうだい
You've got more confident and gradually became beautiful in this one year. I believe you'll become even more beautiful in the second year. Let me watch over you from your side.

Soyogu Shinkai 		いい面構えになったな。ここで止まんなよ、せっかく両手があるんだ、ペンも剣もどっちも握れ
You've got a good look now. You'd better not stop here, since you've got two hands, use them to hold both a pen and a sword.

Shinobu Aizawa 		祝！　１年！　貴方が来てくれて、Aporiaも色々動き出したって感じですね！　俺的にはすっごく助かってます
Happy! One year! It feels like Aporia has started to move in a lot of ways since you joined! For me, you've been really helpful.

Kiho Arima 		ここで１年を過ごしてくれてありがとう。Aporiaにとってはある意味、転機になったんじゃないかな
Thank you for spending a year here. I guess you've become a turning point for Aporia in a way.

Kyoya Shido 		君と出会えてよかった。日々が賑やかになって、確実に時間の質が底上げされてる。もっと、と思ってしまうよ
I'm glad to have met you. The days turned lively, and the quality of my time has definitely improved. It makes me want more of it.

Riku Tateshina 		君と出会って１年あっという間だったけど、ここ最近で一番胸が弾んだ期間だったんじゃないかな。ありがとう
A year since I met you flew in a blink of eye though, I guess this was the most exciting time I've had in a while. Thank you.

Tomose Onda 		お前の仕事ぶりはよく耳に入ってくる。いい評判が出回るというのは、この１年の働きが真に素晴らしいということだろう
I often hear about your work performance. The fact that all of them are good ones means that your work for this one year has been truly amazing.

Yu Nina 		１年‥‥‥あんたの賄いの好みは粗方、把握したつもりだ
One year...... I've mostly understood your staff meal preferences.

Kamiya 		もう１年なんだ！　まだ１年だし、そのうち「もうそんな経つのか」って思うくらい一緒にいたいって思ってる
It's already one year! It's still one year, so it makes me think that I want you to stay with us together until we'd wonder that "so it has been that long."

Urara Manami 		弥代は‥‥‥はあ？　お前が名乗ったんだから名前くらい知ってる。舐めてんのか
Yashiro is...... Hah? I at least know your name since you mentioned it. Are you underestimating me?
