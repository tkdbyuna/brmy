Change Skill is the SP Skill related to changing the Attribute (Sun, Moon, Star), enabling coin-collectibility of the unmatch pieces on the board.
For a special case, Make Special Piece, which changes an unmatch piece to a special piece, also belongs to this category.

There are 15 skills under Change Skill:
