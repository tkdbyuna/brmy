Main article: Labor Thanksgiving 2024

  • Release Period: November 23, 2024 00:00 JST - November 23, 2024 23:59 JST

Translated by Misora

Characters Labor Thanksgiving 2024

Ai Kosaka 		お前の働きぶりは把握している。今後も励めよ
I know how well you've been working. Keep up the good work.

Yuzuru Kise 		ダメですよ、こんな日まで仕事したら。‥‥‥なんて俺もしちゃってたんですけど。ふふ、今日は思い切って休みましょう
You shouldn't work on this kind of day....... Just kidding, I actually worked a little bit too though. Fufu, let's fully take a break today.

Roka Suoh 		いつもお仕事お疲れ様。いつもみんなのためにがんばってくれてありがとう。だから今日は、僕が代表して君のために一肌脱ごうじゃないかッ
Thank you for all of your work, thank you for always doing your best for everyone. And so, I shall represent you to pitch in and help for today.

Kou Ayato 		仕事はほどほどに、いつもお疲れ様。一応部長ですから、それらしいこと言っておかないと
Work moderately, thanks for always. I'm a department leader, so I should at least tell you that.

Mao Ukyo 		今日って勤労感謝の日か。せっかくだし、お互いにご褒美でも買いに行く？
So today is Labor Thanksgiving Day. Since we're at it, should we go buy a reward for each other?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		代理さん、お疲れ様。いつも頑張ってるの見とるよ。今日くらいはゆっくりしてて？
Deputy-san, good work. I've always watched how you do your best. Take it easy for today, okay?

Yuragi Kanno 		代理がいつも仕事してくれてること、みんな知ってるから。今日くらいは、あんたも休んだら？
Everyone knows how much deputy always does for her work. How about you at least rest for today?

Taiga Tsukimoto 		いつも一生懸命な貴方にお菓子を焼いてきたんです。誓さんのお墨付きなんで出来は安心してください
I've baked some sweets for you who have always put your utmost effort in everything. They're approved by Sei-san, so please rest assured about the results.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		そもそも祝日１日しかないとか、感謝度低くない？
Having this kind of national holiday only once a year shows how low our level of gratitude is, doesn't it?

Sei Okiya 		勤労に感謝というなら、私はまず大河と春日に感謝すべきですね。今日くらいはゆっくりできるよう働きかけましょうか
If it's being grateful towards labor then I should thank Taiga and Haruhi first. I should get to work to make them able to take it easy for today.

Shizuka Fushimi 		休める時は休む、そこ軽視しないこと。今日なんて大手振って休める日だろ
Rest when you can rest is something you shouldn't take lightly of. Today is a day when you can rest without hesitation.

Takeru Mikado 		いいこと、今日はガッツリお休みしなさい。いつも仕事頑張ってるんだから今日くらいハメ外さなくっちゃ
The good thing is, please take a good rest today. You've always been doing your best at work, so you should let loose today.

Soyogu Shinkai 		いつもお疲れさん。勤労感謝の日ってことで、今日くらいゆっくりしようぜ。緊急の呼び出しがないこと祈りながら
Thanks for your hard work. Since it's Labor Thanksgiving Day, let's take it easy while hoping there won't be any emergency calls for today.

Shinobu Aizawa 		いつもお疲れ様です！　よかったら一緒にどっか出かけませんか？　あっでものんびりしたかったら言ってください
Thank you for your hard work! If you'd like, why don't we go somewhere together? Ah, but if you want to relax for today then please tell me so.

Kiho Arima 		日頃のご褒美に、美味しい日本酒でも開けようか。お互いの毎日に乾杯
I shall open up a bottle of nice Japanese sake as a daily reward for you. Cheers to each other's daily deeds.

Kyoya Shido 		勤労に感謝、美しい響きだと思うよ。ああ、今日はトクさんにも休んでもらってるんだ
I do think that gratitude for labor is a beautiful term. Ah, of course, I have Toku-san take a day off too today.

Riku Tateshina 		仕事してるのはどこのお嬢だー？　逢といい由鶴といい‥‥‥ここは真面目なやつも多いな。今日くらいみんな休みなさいって
Who's the lady that's working today—? Ai and Yuzuru aside...... This place has lots of earnest people. I keep telling everyone to at least rest for today.

Tomose Onda 		世の中には感謝されない仕事もある。だが‥‥‥こういう日があるのは良いことだと俺は思う
Jobs that no one feel grateful for also exist in this world. But...... I think having this kind of day is a nice thing.

Yu Nina 		いつもお疲れ様。ん？　そういう日なんだと聞いたが、何だその顔は
Thanks for your hard work. Hm? I heard it was that kind of day though, what's with that face?

Kamiya 		よかったらドーナツどうぞ！　いつも頑張ってる代理に感謝を込めて。今後も何か困ることあれば気軽に言って
Have some donuts if you like! It includes my gratitude for deputy, who always does her best. Feel free to tell me whenever you feel troubled from now on.

Urara Manami 		お疲れ様。よく頑張ったな、エライエライ。なんてオレが言うと思ったか？
Good work. You did your best, how remarkable. You think I'd say those?
