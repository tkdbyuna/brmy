Introduction]

A CD album contains the puzzle background music used in "Break My Case", including 2 discs. This album compiles background music previously published from BREAK MY CASE Original Soundtrack Vol.7 to Vol.8, and adds new songs.

There are two versions: the regular and the first press limited edition.
