Erase Skill is the SP Skill related to erase things: pieces (attribute, direction, color, range); coin, gimmicks (glass, flywood, etc.)
If gimmicks are all cleared (glass, flywood, etc.) on the board, Combo +3 will be added instead when SP skill is activated.

There are 21 skills under Erase Skill:
