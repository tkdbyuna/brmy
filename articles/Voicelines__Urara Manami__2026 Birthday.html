Characters Urara Manami's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		なんだかんだ、お前の誕生日は騒がしい気がするな。今後も口の聞き方に気をつけて、業務に励むように
I feel like it gets noisy when it's your birthday. Make sure to be careful with how you speak and keep working hard from now on.

Yuzuru Kise 		ちょっとしたプレゼント、レアのおもちゃとかなら一緒に遊べるし受け取ってくれるかな。‥‥‥レアへのプレゼントになっちゃうか
I wonder if he'll accept a small gift like a toy for Rare so that he can use it to play with him? ......l guess that'd make it Rare's gift instead.

Roka Suoh 		麗、今年もお誕生日おめでとう！　耳を塞がれたって何度でも伝えるさ。麗がいてくれて嬉しいって、心から思ってるからね
Urara, happy birthday this year too! I'll congratulate you countlessly even if you cover your ears. I'm genuinely glad that Urara is here with us from the bottom of my heart.

Kou Ayato 		今年は選べる範囲を増やしてネットショッピングのギフト券とかにするか。何かと使える機会もあると思うし
I should give him a gift voucher for online shopping to give him freedom of choice this year. There should be an opportunity for him to use it.

Mao Ukyo 		麻波に掃除道具を‥‥‥と思ったけど、あれは仕事用か。にしても、いつも大事に使ってくれてるよね。すごいと思う
Cleaning tools for Manami...... Was what I planned, but that would be something for his job. Still, he always uses them with care. I think that's amazing.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		今年はもー少し距離を詰めてみよかなと思って、チャッタスでおたおめスタンプ送ってみたんやけど‥‥‥ちゃんと既読ついてた
I thought of getting a little bit closer with him this year, so I tried sending him a happy birthday stamp through Chattask...... He properly read it.

Yuragi Kanno 		スタッフルームに置いてた猫の写真を、麗が真剣に見てた。あげたら喜ぶかな
Urara was staring at the cat picture left in the staff room. Will he be happy if I give it to him?

Taiga Tsukimoto 		業務用の道具が使えるのもあるだろうけど、麻波くんの掃除スキルは目を見張るものがあるな‥‥‥
He might be using commercial tools, but Manami-kun's cleaning skills are truly impressive.......

Haruhi Ichikawa 		大河がレアの形のクッキーどう作るか悩んでて、揺と誓がアドバイスしてたんだけど。成人男性の誕生日に動物クッキー焼くことに誰も疑問感じてないのおかしくない？　俺がおかしいの？
Taiga was at loss whether he should bake Rare-shaped cookies, then Yuragi and Sei advised him on it. Isn't it weird that no one is questioning the fact that he's baking some animal-shaped cookies for an adult man's birthday? Am I the weird one?

Sei Okiya 		麗くんの誕生日ですか。なかなか接点がない上に、本人が望まないでしょうから、お祝いの言葉は心の中でそっと添えることにしましょう
It's Urara-kun's birthday. I rarely have contact with him, and the person in question doesn't wish for it either, so I should gently keep these celebratory words inside my heart.

Shizuka Fushimi 		めずらしく麻波と顔合わせたからおめでとうって伝えたけど、予想通りの反応だったよ
I surprisingly was able to meet Manami, so I told him congrats, and he gave me the reaction I expected.

Takeru Mikado 		今日くらいは笑顔で、っていうのも良くないわね。麗が麗らしくいて沢山お祝いしてもらえる、そんな日にしてほしいわ
Telling him to at least spend today with a smile isn't exactly nice. I wish today would be the day where Urara gets celebrated a lot as he is.

Soyogu Shinkai 		反発されんのは目に見えちゃいるが、知ってて無視ってわけにいはいかねえよ。同じチームで動いてた仲間だからな
It's obvious that I'll get a backlash from him, but I can't simply ignore it when I know about it. We used to work in the same team after all.

Shinobu Aizawa 		麗へのプレゼント、実用的なものだったら許されたりする‥‥‥？　うーん、激ムズすぎ！
Will Urara let it slide if I give him something practical as a gift.....? Hmm, it's too super duper hard!

Kiho Arima 		俺から何かお祝いするより、いつも通りの中にそれを含ませるなら‥‥‥麗が美味しそうに食べてた賄い、内緒で有たちにリクエストしちゃおう
Rather than me doing something to celebrate him, if we include keeping it as usual as a way to do it then...... I should secretly request the staff meal Urara eat deliciously to Yu and the others.

Kyoya Shido 		欲しいものを聞いて教えてくれる子じゃないし、いらないものを押し付けてもね。特務の彼らが今年も祝うようだから、少し出資して、お祝いに代えさせてもらおうかな
He's not someone who will tell me what he wants when I ask about it, and forcing something he doesnt need would be too much. Since it seems like the ST Dept. is celebrating him this year, I should invest a bit to financially support the celebration.

Riku Tateshina 		何をもらったら嬉しいんだか、と思ったけど、変にものを渡そうとするよりも半ば押し付けるようなものがいいよな。何か消え物でいいものは、と‥‥‥
I was thinking about what he will be happy to receive. But half forcing him to take something should be better than giving him something weird. Something disposable......

Tomose Onda 		祝われたくないとも違うんだろうが、取り立てて何かされるのが嫌だと言うのも本当だろう。簡単な言葉を贈るにとどめておく
It's different to him not wanting to be celebrated, but him mentioning that he hate having something done to him is the truth. I'll keep it as a simple message.

Yu Nina 		神家が今年は直接祝えたと喜んでいた。うまくやっているなら問題ない
Kamiya was happy because he was able to celebrate it directly this year. There's no problem if they're doing well.

Kamiya 		麗には嫌がられるだろうけど、やっぱりちゃんと祝いたいから。お誕生日おめでとうって面と向かって言わせてほしい！
I know Urara would dislike it, but as I thought, I want to properly celebrate him. I want him to let me say happy birthday face-to-face!

Urara Manami 		祝ってくる奴らの誕生日は、さぞいい思い出ばっかりなんだろうな。一応で声かけてこられても、うざったいだけだって知らねーのか
Those guys who are celebrating must have only good nemories about their own birthdays. Can't they tell that I find them annoying even if they just called me out?
