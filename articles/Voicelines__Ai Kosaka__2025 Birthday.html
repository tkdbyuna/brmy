Characters Ai Kosaka's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		今日、やけに周りからだる絡みされるわけだ‥‥‥。俺の誕生日だからと媚び諂う必要はないからな
No wonder everyone is trying to mess around with me...... There's no need to flatter me just because it's my birthday.

Yuzuru Kise 		逢さんお誕生日おめでとうございます。ご用があればなんでも‥‥‥ってこれだと特別感がないですかね
Ai-san, happy birthday. Please tell me if you have anything.......Ah, this doesn't actually sound that exclusive.

Roka Suoh 		アイアイ'sバースデー！　事務所のカレンダーに書き込もうとしたら怒られたけれどねッ、僕はしっかり覚えていたよ！
Ai-ai's birthday! He scolded me when I tried to mark it on the office's calendar, but I properly remembered it!

Kou Ayato 		逢さんの誕生日にバースデープレートをみんなで作ったんだけどさ、明星が花火立てようって。‥‥‥いけると思う？
I made a birthday platter with everyone to celebrate Ai-san's birthday though, Akehoshi suggested lighting up fireworks. ......Do you think it's a good idea?

Mao Ukyo 		陛下、誕生日おめでとう。してほしいことあったら遠慮なく何でも言ったらいいよ。恋に
Your Majesty, happy birthday. Feel free to tell whatever you want Kou to do.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		祝・逢さん！　陛下って人生三周目くらいの貫禄あらへん？　って言ったら、なんや睨まれたことあるから気つけてな
Congratulations, Ai-san! Doesn't it feel like His Majesty has the dignity of a person who's on his third life? He somehow glared at me when I said that, so you'd better be careful.

Yuragi Kanno 		逢おめでとう。美味しそうなのもらったら、俺に教えて
Ai, congrats. Tell me if you get anything delicious.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		皇坂さん、よかったらこれ作業のお供にどうぞ。お口に合うといいんですが‥‥‥
Kosaka-san, please have this to accompany you on your work. I hope it suits your taste......

Haruhi Ichikawa 		逢さんの誕生日、へー。あの人に赤ちゃん時代あったんだ
Ai-san's birthday, heh. Even that person was a baby.

Sei Okiya 		逢くんの誕生日ですか。彼が生まれた日ともなれば、何か素敵な催しが必要ですね。貴方の協力を求めたいのですが、ご都合いかがです？
Ai-kun's birthday. A wonderful event must be held to celebrate the day he was born. I'd like to ask for your cooperation in this, is that fine with you?

Shizuka Fushimi 		おめでとう、皇坂に欲しいものってある？　ないかもだけど一応リクエストして
Congrats, do you have anything you want, Kosaka? You might have nothing but just request whatever.

Takeru Mikado 		逢ちゃんってああ見えて可愛いところがあってね。今度一緒に飲んでくれないかしら
Ai-chan has a cute part even if he looks like that. I wonder if he will drink with me next time.

Soyogu Shinkai 		へえ、あいつの誕生日‥‥‥。祝う気ないやつに祝われても嬉しくねえだろ。だから俺はパス
Heh, so it's that person's birthday...... Celebrating someone who doesn't want to be celebrated won't make him happy at all. I'm skipping this one.

Shinobu Aizawa 		逢さんって「天上天下唯我独尊！ばん！」って生まれてても俺全然驚かない、むしろ納得
I won't be surprised if Ai-san was born as "the only one in the world! Boom!" It'll actually be convincing for me.

Kiho Arima 		おめでとう。苦労をしているせいか年々険しい顔が様になってしまっているけど、君はその立場が似合っているし、ふさわしい
Congrats. Your face might grow grimmer with every passing year since you work hard, but that position itself suits you. Very befitting of you.

Kyoya Shido 		誕生日おめでとうございます。逢さんにはなにかと融通きいてもらってるので、俺も今日は何かしたいんですよね
Happy birthday. I want to do something for Ai-san since he has been very accommodating with me.

Riku Tateshina 		逢、誕生日おめでとう。プレゼントは何がいいの？　お兄さんが買ってやろう‥‥‥“お兄さん”って歳かって？　お前それ２年後ブーメランだからな
Ai, happy birthday. What present do you want? This big brother shall buy it for you...... Am I still in the age of a "big brother"? That'll be a boomerang for you in 2 years.

Tomose Onda 		今日は何かを持って事務所に行くやつが多い。皇坂が慕われている証拠だろう
There are a lot of people who brought something to the office today. That must be the proof of Kosaka being adored.

Yu Nina 		皇坂は、良いことも悪いことも自分の意思を隠さない。素直な人間だ
Good or bad, Kosaka doesn't hide his intentions. He's an honest person.

Kamiya 		逢さん誕生日おめでとうございます、これからもキビキビ働くのでそれがプレゼントってことで！
Ai-san, happy birthday. I'll keep working briskly from now on too, and that's my present for you!
