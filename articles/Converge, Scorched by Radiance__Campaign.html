Original Design Sake featuring Roka Suoh, Kou Ayato, and Kiho Arima in collaboration with KURAN[1]:

■ Original Liqueur - ROKA SUOH -
The beloved incompetent troublemaker, Roka Suoh. In celebration of the in-game event "Converge, Scorched by Radiance," we've prepared a bottle with an original design featuring Roka Suoh. Inside is a plum wine infused with the refreshing aroma of lemongrass. You can enjoy the sweet-tart plum and the vibrant elegance of lemongrass all at once.[2]

■ Original Liqueur - KOU AYATO -
The genius of temptation who scatters irresponsible love, Kou Ayato. In celebration of the in-game event "Converge, Scorched by Radiance," we've prepared a bottle with an original design featuring Kou Ayato. Inside is a sweetly tart pomegranate liqueur. Savor its juicy, refreshing acidity and the fresh, vibrant aftertaste.[3]

■ Original Liqueur - KIHO ARIMA -
Self-aware and devilishly alluring, the fortune-teller Kiho Arima. In celebration of the in-game event "Converge, Scorched by Radiance," we've prepared a bottle with an original design featuring Kiho Arima. Inside is an elegant, floral liquor of rose and cherry. The rose's refined fragrance and the cherry's sweetly tart bite create a one-of-a-kind, exquisite harmony.[4]

Each purchase comes with the following bonuses:

  • 【Novelty Illustration Card】and
