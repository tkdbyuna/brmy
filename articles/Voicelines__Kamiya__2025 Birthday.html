Characters Kamiya's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		先程会ったら、多くのプレゼントを抱えていた。神家はそういう人間だったな
I saw him carrying a lot of presents when I met him earlier. Kamiya is that kind of person.

Yuzuru Kise 		神家がいつも好きなものを教えてくれるから、俺たちはそれを贈ることができるんだよ。今日は好きなものに沢山囲まれる１日にしてね
Kamiya always tells us what he likes, so we can give it to him. Make today the day you can get surrounded by a lot of your favorite things.

Roka Suoh 		かみやんの誕生日プレゼントにドーナツ‥‥‥は、みんなも贈ってるだろうから。僕はこれにしよう！　ドーナツ型の充電器！
Donuts for Kamiyan's birthday present....... Hah, everyone must already be giving it to him. I should give this instead! A donut-shaped charger!

Kou Ayato 		神家お誕生日おめでとう。どうせハピドに埋もれてるんだろうなと思って、俺からはコーヒー豆。特別に淹れるところまでやろうか？
Kamiya, happy birthday. I was thinking that you'll be buried with Hapido anyway, so I'm giving you some coffee beans. Should I specially brew it for you?

Mao Ukyo 		簡単な差し入れでも神家は全力で喜んでくれるから、もっといいものを贈りたくなるんだよね。誕生日、おめでとう
Giving Kamiya some simple snacks already makes him happy with all his might, so it makes me want to give something even better. Happy birthday.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		かみやんお誕生日おめでとう～！　かみやんに似合いそうな服見つけたから、今度一緒に行かへん？　もし気に入ったらプレゼントさせて
Kamiyan, happy birthday~! I found some clothing that'd suit Kamiyan, so why don't we go check it out together next time? Let me give it to you as a present if you like it.

Yuragi Kanno 		いつも美味しいドーナツのお裾分けありがと。代わりに俺おすすめの穴の空いたお菓子あげる。食べて
Thanks for always sharing your delicious donuts with me. In exchange, I'm giving you my recommended sweets that have holes. Eat it.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		神家くんとはたまにしか会えないけど‥‥‥会えた時は気さくに声をかけてくれるから、会う頻度以上に距離感が近い気がする。神家くんの良さだな
Only meet with Kamiya-kun sometimes...... Since he always calls me out sincerely when we meet, I feel like our closeness has grown more than the times we meet. It ust be Kamiya-kun's good point.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		ドーナツが好きって聞いたから用意した。大河が
Since you like donuts, I got some prepared. By Taiga.

Sei Okiya 		ドーナツがお好きと伺ったのでお持ちしました。私が揚げたのではないのですが‥‥‥手作りの味も、かえって新鮮かと思いまして
I heard that you like donuts, so I brought some. I wasn't the one who made it though...... I believe handmade taste would be rather fresh for you.

Shizuka Fushimi 		誕生日祝いにたくさんドーナツもらったから、自分で買ったドーナツはお裾分けしますって神家に笑顔で言われたんだけど‥‥‥。普通に理解しがたい状況だよな
Since he got a lot of donuts for his birthday, Kamiya told me that he'll share the donuts I bought with me with a smile....... It's a situation I simply find difficult to understand.

Takeru Mikado 		正直ね、このドーナツ買うのにものすごく並んだのよ。でもその甲斐がある味って話だから、お楽しみに！
To be honest, the queue to buy these donuts was amazing. But, I'm saying it in a way that it was worth it to get, so look forward to it!

Soyogu Shinkai 		いい加減ハピド飽きねえのかって神家に聞いたけど、飽きねえっつーから、今からプレゼントに買いに行ってくるわ
I asked Kamiya, "don't you get sick of eating Hapido," and since he told me he's not, l'm going to buy them as a present for him now.

Shinobu Aizawa 		かみやん誕生日おめでとー！　お祝いにハピド食べ放題行こ！　全部俺の奢りって意味で！
Kamiyan, happy birthday! Let's have an all-you-can-eat Hapido to celebrate! It'll be my treat!

Kiho Arima 		今日はみんなからたくさんハピドを貰ってるかなと思ってね、俺からは合いそうな紅茶を贈らせて。‥‥‥はは、恋と同じ発想だったみたいだね
I think everyone will be giving you a lot of Hapido today, so let me give you tea that will go well with them. ......Haha, seems like I had the same idea as Kou.

Kyoya Shido 		ハピド‥‥‥という所じゃなくて悪いけど、行きつけの洋菓子店の焼きドーナツを用意してみたんだ。神家くんの口に合うと良いな
Hapido...... My bad that these weren't from there, but I tried preparing some donuts from the pastry shop I frequent. I hope it suits Kamiya-kun's taste.

Riku Tateshina 		絶対自分で買ってるってわかってたんだけど、つい買ってきちゃったよハピド。新作だって言ってたからせめてまだ食べたことない味だと良いんだけど
I know that you'd surely buy them on your own, but I ended up buying Hapido too. They told me it's a new flavor, so I hope that at least it's a flavor you haven't tried yet.

Tomose Onda 		神家、誕生日おめでとう。ここは自分の祝い事以上に他者の祝いに力を入れるやつが多い。たくさん祝われたなら、よかった
Kamiya, happy birthday. There are more people who put more effort in celebrating others than celebrating themselves here. I'm glad if you got celebrated a lot.

Yu Nina 		神家は、変わらずシフトに入っているのか？　一般的に誕生日は休みたくなると聞くが
Is Kamiya on shift as usual? I heard people generally want to take a day off on their birthdays.

Kamiya 		誕生日ってなんかむず痒くない？　人に無条件で「おめでとう」って言われることって、そんなにないからかな
Doesn't a birthday make you feel weirdly itchy? I wonder if it's because you don't really get unconditionally congratulated by people?

Urara Manami 		無視したら都合がいいことばっか妄想するからな。はっきり「祝わねえ」って言ったら‥‥‥それでも覚えてただの言ってきそうでうぜえな
It's because he'll delude himself with something nice if I ignore him. If I straight up tell him that "I won't celebrate you"...... He'll just reply with something like "but you still remember", and it's annoying.
