INTRODUCTION]

The first compilation album of personal songs that express the feelings of the staff who appear in "Break My Case".

In the first release, you can enjoy songs by seven artists: Ai Kosaka, Mao Ukyo, Sei Okiya, Soyogu Shinkai, Riku Tateshina, Kamiya, and Kou Ayato.

Please enjoy the vocal songs sung by the Aporia staff cast.

Includes cast comments exclusive to the CD.[1]
