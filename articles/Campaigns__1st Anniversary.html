1st Anniversary -Knotting Point- Campaigns]

Campaign Period (JP): May 03, 2025 20:00 JST - May 22, 2025 12:59 JST

Break My Case will celebrate its 1st anniversary on May 9.

To express our deep gratitude to everyone who has been playing BRMY, we are holding a 1st Anniversary Campaign!

Campaign highlights:

  • Celebrate with the staff in their 1st anniversary style on the special campaign page!

  • Up to 100 free gacha pulls!
