Characters Yu Nina's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		Aporia内の二足のわらじとして、非常に貢献度が高い奴だ。今日をゆっくり過ごせているといい
He has made a significant contribution in Aporia as a double role worker. He should take his time today.

Yuzuru Kise 		よかったら今日は俺が賄い作ってもいい？　お祝いっていうにはささやかだけど、何かしたくて
If you'd like, can I be the one who makes the staff meals today? It might be small to call it a celebration, but I want to do something for you.

Roka Suoh 		ハピバ有はん！　有はんはオシャレさんだからね。このシャツは、我ながら渾身のセレクト！　喜んでくれるかな？
Hapiba, Yuhan! Yuhan is a stylish person, so I'm giving you this t-shirt, which I picked out with all my heart! Will you be glad to accept it?

Kou Ayato 		有さんの賄いうまーって気持ちでいつも食べてるからさ、同じ気持ちを味わってほしいと思って有さんの賄い担当に立候補してみた。結果はご想像にお任せします
I always eat Yu-san's staff meal with "so good~' in mind, that's why I thought of making him feel the same way, and decided to register myself as a candidate to make Yu-san's staff meal. I'm leaving the results to your imagination.

Mao Ukyo 		有って、本当にいい意味で無駄がないよね。だからこそ、お祝い方法に困るんだけど
Yu, in a good meaning really isn't wasteful. That's exactly why I'm troubled with how to celebrate him.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		有さんに教えてもらった簡単レシピ試してみたんやけどほんまに簡単で美味しかったんよね。今度は俺からオーブンの簡単ボタンについて教えてあげようかなあ
I tried making the easy recipe Yu-san told me and it was truly delicious. I should probably teach him about the oven's easy button next time.

Yuragi Kanno 		誕生日だから、有の賄いのプレートの端にクッキーのせておいた。気づいてたから、食べてくれると思う
I put a cookie on the edge of Yu's staff meal because it's his birthday today. He noticed it, so I think he'll eat it.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		新名さんは、誕生日に恩田さんとパーティとかするんだろうか。‥‥‥って、余計なお世話でしかないな
I wonder if Nina-san will have a party with Onda-san? .....Ah, it shouldn't be my concern.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		揺が新名さんの賄いは美味しいって。‥‥‥この言葉がプレゼントってことにしてほしい
Yuragi said that Nina-san's staff meals are good to use these words as a gift to him.

Sei Okiya 		あれほど強固な信頼関係は、見ていて美しさを感じずにはいられません。恩田くんの誠実さと、新名くんの揺らがない忠実さのもと成り立っているのでしょうね
It's hard not to feel a sense of beauty when you see such a strong relationship of trust. It must be based on Onda-kun's sincerity and Nina-kun's unwavering loyalty.

Shizuka Fushimi 		新名さんの誕生日だっていうから、カフェ行くついでに伝えるか。でもキッチンにいるなら、シェフを呼んでくれって言う羽目になるのか‥‥‥？
Since it's Nina-san's birthday, I should congratulate him when I go to the café. But if he's in the kitchen, I might end up doing that kind of thing where someone asks for the chef......?

Takeru Mikado 		有をしっかりお祝いしたかったら、まず灯世ちゃんを誘って‥‥‥っていうのが難関なのよね
I want to properly celebrate Yu, so I need to invite Tomose-chan first...... Which is actually difficult to do.

Soyogu Shinkai 		フィジカルで押しきれない分を判断と技術で完全に補ってるのがすげえ。祝いついでに、話きけたりしねえかな
It's amazing how he makes up for what he can't do physically with hisjudgment and technique. I wonder if we can talk about it while were celebrating.

Shinobu Aizawa 		有さんってオシャレですよね！　誕生日祝うついでにいつも何意識して選んでるのかとか聞いたら教えてくれるかなー
Yu-san is so stylish! Will he answer me if I ask about what he usually has in mind when he picks out clothes while we're celebrating?

Kiho Arima 		みんなから有の賄いは評判がいいんだ。食事は活力につながるからね、そのお礼に何かできたらいいんだけど‥‥‥
Yu's staff meals are well received by everyone. Food leads to vigor, so it'd be nice if I could do something as a gratitude......

Kyoya Shido 		新名くんはキッチンにいたから、もうおめでとうを伝えてきたよ。反応‥‥‥という反応はなかったけど、祝われて悪い気がする人もいないんじゃないかな
I already congratulated Nina-kun since he was in the kitchen. His reaction..... wasn't exactly a reaction though, I believe no one feels bad about being celebrated.

Riku Tateshina 		本日の主役に賄い作ってもらっちゃったよ。何かお返ししないとな。にしても、有へのプレゼント選びのハードルは高いな。
I had my staff meal made by today's main character. I have to return it with something. Still, choosing a gift for Yu is quite hard.

Tomose Onda 		有が望まないことをするつもりはないが、日頃使えそうなものを贈るくらいは、問題ないだろう
I don't want to do anything that Yu doesn't wish for, but giving him something that he can use daily shouldn't be a problem.

Yu Nina 		神家の目線をあれだけ感じれば、今日が誕生日であることを思い出すのも容易だな
It's simply easy to remember that today is my birthday when Kamiya stares at me like that.

Kamiya 		灯世さんの隣に有さんがいてよかったっていつも思ってる。俺からできることは少ないと思うけど、何かあったらいつでも言って！
I always feel grateful that Tomose-san has Yu-san beside him. I might not be able to do much, but tell me if there's anything!

Urara Manami 		どうせ恩田の言うことしか聞いちゃねえ。祝うだけ無駄だからほっとけよ
He'll only listen to what Onda says anyway. Celebrating him is useless, just let him be.
