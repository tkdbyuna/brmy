GIMMICK (ギミック, gimikku?) is special mechanics or twists added to puzzle map to keep gameplay fresh, increase challenge, and create memorable “Aha!” moments.

How to check the gimmick
The explanation of gimmicks displayed when attempting a stage for the first time can be reviewed again from the "Gimmick Details" (ギミック詳細, gimikku shōsai?, lit. gimmick detail) option on the pause screen during the puzzle.

※Notes: The Main Story's puzzles are excluded in this article.

Seek Bar]

The Seek Bar is a bar moving across from one end to the other end of the puzzle map. Matched units, combo pieces can ONLY be erased after it touches them, except for stage applying no seek bar rule.

One stage has several rounds of seek bar moving; the number of combos scored during one section is tracked and graded: SAD, COOL, GREAT, STYLISH, and MARVELOUS. The staff will say the voicelines depend on the previous grading at the beginning of the new cycle. The highest-scoring combos are finalized at the final result screen.

It can move only vertically or horizontally. Pacing varies depending on the stages.

Besides, it can carry status effects that impact the pieces once it touches them.

◆ Shock Seek Bar]

Shock Seek Bar (カミナリシークバー, kaminari shīkubā?, lit. thunder seek bar)
Pieces that touch the Shock Seek Bar may be frozen.

See more Freeze Piece

◆ Sleep Seek Bar]

Sleep Seek Bar (ねむりシークバー, nemuri shīkubā?)
Pieces that touch the Sleep Seek Bar may fall asleep.

See more Sleep Piece

◆ Score Up Seek Bar]

Score Up Seek Bar (スコアUPシークバー, sukoa UP shīkubā?)
This is a user-friendly feature.

It appears to yield approximately 1.45 times the score of a standard seek bar.

◆ No Seek Bar]

No Seek Bar (ノーシークバー, nō shīkubā?)
On stages without a seek bar, units disappear immediately after a piece match, and new pieces appear.

See more No Seek Bar Rule

◆ Combo Seek Bar]

Combo Seek Bar (ノルマシークバー, noruma shīkubā?, lit. quota seek bar)

While the seek bar goes from one end to the other, erase the units to the designated number of times.

If you fail to erase the designated number of units, your life decreases by 1.

Rule]

When a rule is applied, certain gimmicks are activated on puzzle stages.

◆ NO SEEK BAR]

Instruction

In this stage, the seek bar does not appear. When you match pieces, the units are erased immediately.

◆ SPECIAL PIECE]

Instruction
1. In this stage, you can create special pieces.
When you erase a 4-piece unit, a Hirameki Piece appears.
When you erase a 5-piece unit, a Bomb Piece appears!
2. When you use a Hirameki Piece to create a unit, its effect activates when the unit is erased!
It clears an entire vertical or horizontal line of pieces at once.
3. When you use a Bomb Piece to create a unit, its effect activates when the unit is erased!
It explodes, taking along the surrounding pieces!
4. When you erase a unit of 6 or more pieces… a Rainbow Piece is created!!
Swap it with an adjacent piece, and all pieces of the same color will be erased.
5. Furthermore, when you swap two Special Pieces…

※In this rule, combo piece single combo piece, double combo piece, tripple combo piece won't happens. See more #Combo Piece

◆ GRAVITY+]

Instruction

In this stage, strong gravity occurs.
When you erase pieces, the pieces above fall down all at once.

◆ MOVE LIMIT]

Instruction

A MOVE LIMIT stage is a stage where you can swap pieces only a set number of times.

Status Effect Piece]

Pieces can carry a status effect.

◆ Abnormal Condition Piece]

◈ Freeze Piece]

Freeze pieces (まひピース, hima pĩsu?, lit. frozen piece) are unmatchable and immovable.

Freeze pieces are unable to move for a set period of time.

When a piece erasing occurs within four adjacent areas to freeze pieces, you can recover them to their normal status.

Attribute Sun Moon Star
Freeze Piece

◈ Sleep Piece]

Sleep pieces (ねむりピース, nemuri pĩsu?) are unmatchable, but movable.

When a piece erasing occurs within four adjacent areas to sleep pieces, you can wake them.

Attribute Sun Moon Star
Sleep Piece

◈ Moyamoya Piece]

Moyamoya Piece (もやもやピース, moyamoya pĩsu?, lit. hazy piece) are matchable and immovable.

Moyamoya Piece cannot be moved.

You can dispel the haze by forming a unit.

Attribute Sun Moon Star
Moyamoya Piece

◆ Special Piece]

Special piece (スペシャルピース, supesharu pīsu?) is introduced from version.

If a SPECIAL PIECE rule is applied, this gimmick will occur.

When you match the pieces to create a number-of-piece unit, the special piece is created at the matching spot.

There are three kinds of special pieces: Hirameki Piece, Bomb Piece, and Rainbow Piece.

◈ Hirameki Piece]

Hirameki Piece (or Inspiration Piece) (ひらめきピース, hirameki pīsu?, lit. inspiration piece)
Erasing a 4-piece unit will create a hirameki piece:

  • Vertical 4-piece unit creates a horizontal hirameki piece.
  • Horizontal 4-piece unit creates a vertical hirameki piece.

Units using a hirameki piece will erase all pieces in a single vertical or horizontal column at once.

Attribute Sun Moon Star
Hirameki Piece

◈ Bomb Piece]

Bomb Piece (ボムピース, bomu pīsu?, lit. bomb piece)
Erasing a 5-piece unit will create a bomb piece.

A unit that uses a bomb piece will explode, taking surrounding pieces with it.

Attribute Sun Moon Star
Bomb Piece

◈ Rainbow Piece]

Rainbow Piece (レインボーピース, reinbō pīsu?)
Erasing a 6-or-more-piece unit will create a rainbow piece.

Swapping it with an adjacent piece will clear all pieces of the same color.

◈ Special Piece x Special Piece]

Furthermore, swapping special pieces with each other allows you to clear pieces more effectively.

Special Piece
Effect Description
Hirameki P. × Hirameki P. Erase one vertical column and one horizontal row starting from the combined pieces.
Bomb P. × Bomb P. Explodes, clearing surrounding pieces in a wider area.
Hirameki P. × Bomb P. Clears 3 vertical columns and 3 horizontal rows starting from the connected point.
Rainbow P. × Hirameki P. Randomly spawns multiple Hirameki pieces and activates them.
Rainbow P. × Bomb P. Randomly spawns multiple Bomb pieces and detonates them.
Rainbow P. × Rainbow P. Clears all pieces.

Unlike match units and combo pieces, which need to touch the seek bar to be cleared, swapping special pieces activates the erasure immediately.

Miscellaneous Gimmicks]

Butterfly

Butterfly (蝶, chou?, lit. butterfly) gimmick is movable by players, swapable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Make the butterfly flap its wings with a hit! When the erasing of the piece occurs in the four adjacent areas of the butterfly, the butterfly flies.

You need to hit it up to three times.

Glass

Glass (ガラス板, gurasu ita?, lit. glass sheet or glass plate) gimmick is immovable, with swapable pieces on top of it, and removable from the board.

You can break the glass plate by erasing the pieces on the glass plate.

You need to repeat it up to three times.

Bomb

Bomb (ボム, bomu?, lit. bomb) gimmick is movable by players, swapable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Tap to detonate, its effect erases the pieces within 8 adjacent squares surrounding the bomb.

On stages with a "Gimmick Details" section explaining the bomb, if the bomb doesn't display initially, clearing pieces may trigger the bomb to appear.
The way to detonate is different than Bomb Piece: one needs to be tapped on itself, while the other needs to be matched in a unit.

For cards having SP Skill of Bomb Cracker, bombs appear and are denoted on their own instantly.

Inspiration

Inspiration (ひらめき, hirameki?, lit. inspiration) gimmick is movable by players, swapable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Tap to activate it, its effect clears all pieces in the direction of the arrow (vertical or horizontal) at once.

On stages with a "Gimmick Details" section explaining the inspiration, if the hirameki doesn't display initially, clearing pieces may trigger the inspiration to appear.
The way to activate is different than Hirameki Piece: one needs to be tapped on itself, while the other needs to be matched in a unit.

For cards having SP Skill of Hirameki Cracker, inspirations appear and are activated on their own instantly.

Switch

Switch (スイッチ, suicchi?) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

When the erasing of the piece occurs at the four adjacent areas of the switch, you can press the switch on.

The next switch will light up by pressing the switch on.

Bush

Bush (藪, yabu?) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

When the erasing of the piece occurs at the four adjacent areas of the bush, you can reap the bushes.

Aruko

Aruga mama koara no aruko (あるがままコアラのあるこ, Aruga mama koara no aruko?, lit. There is a koala Aruko as it is; in short: Aruko) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

It is hidden in the bushes, and you can find it by reaping the bushes. See more Bush

※Hint: if the bush is rustling, it's currently hiding in it. You can catch them at the time if you clear pieces; otherwise, it will move to another bush.

Counter

Counter (カウンター, kauntaa?) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

The number of pieces you eliminate will be counted (fifty in this case).

For every 50 achieved, all pieces of the same color as the counter will be cleared.

Attribute Sun Moon Star

Dressing Room

Dressing Room (フィッティングルーム, Fittingu Rūmu?, lit. fitting room or dressing room) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Eliminate the same clothes as in the fitting room with a hit.

Clothes will appear from behind the curtain.
Every new seek bar moving round, the next clothes appear behind the curtain, follow the order, and repeat: white shirt, blue shirt, then brown blazer.

Dressing room and clothes gimmick always go together.

Clothes

Clothes (服, fuku?, lit. clothes) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

Erase them with a hit.

There are three types: white shirt, blue shirt, and brown blazer.

Dressing room and clothes gimmick always go together.

Donut

Donut (ドーナッ, Dōnattsu?, lit. Donut) gimmick is movable by players, swapable, and removable from the board.

Hit it to recover a life; however, if your life is maximum, it won't add anymore.

Irritated Kosaka

Irritated Kosaka (いらいら皇坂, Iraira Kousaka?, lit. Irritated Kosaka) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Every few seconds, Kosaka’s stress spreads onto the surrounding pieces.

Eliminate the target moyamoya pieces to relieve Kosaka!
Fun fact: There are six statuses of Kosaka. Once the stage is finished, he will say something.

Kosaka's expression
Water

Water (水, Mizu?, lit. Water) is a self-movable gimmick, unswappable, and removable from the board.

They increase in time with the music, eliminate them with a hit!
Water will spread on the board and change the piece to water. ※Tips: Special pieces that have been changed to water then recover to normal won't keep their effect anymore. However, units, combo piece, and rainbow piece won't be changed by water gimmick.

Depending on the stages, the spreading speed of the water gimmick may vary.
Wall

Wall (壁, Kabe?, lit. Wall) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

You cannot swap pieces across walls.

However, you can create units across walls.

Some gimmicks (e.g. water; munching goat) that do not cross walls.

Bell

Bell (鐘, kane?, lit. bell) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Let’s ring the Bell of Happiness! When you erase pieces in the four adjacent areas to the bell, the bell will sway.

After three hits, the sound of the Bell of Happiness will resonate far and wide and reset the counting.

The Queen of the Heart's Rose

The Queen of the Heart's Rose (in short Red Rose) (ハートの女王の薔薇, Hāto no Joō no Bara?, lit. The Queen of Hearts’ Roses) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Let’s paint the roses red!

If you erase Sun‑attribute pieces (Sun (red) or Sun (pink) piece) in the four adjacent areas to the roses, you can paint the roses red.

Plywood Plate

Plywood Plate (べニヤ板, Beniya Ita?, lit. Plywood Board) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

When you erase a glass sheet, it will appear.

You can clear it by erasing the pieces sitting on top of it.

Metal Plate

Metal Plate (メタル板, Metaru Ita?, lit. Metal Plate) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

When you erase a plywood plate, it will appear.

You can clear it by erasing the pieces sitting on top of it.

Fireworks

Fireworks (打ち上げ花火, Uchiage Hanabi?, lit. launch fireworks) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

With hitting by erasing pieces in the four adjacent areas to it, it ignites and launches into the sky. You need to deal damage several times before it ignites.

When erasing, it clears the pieces around a few squares above.

Dust Cloth

Dust Cloth (ぞうきん, Zōkin?, lit. Cleaning Rag) gimmick is movable by players, unswappable, and removable from the board.

Hit the Dust Cloth to push the Dust Cloth to move across the board and erase pieces.

Bucket

Bucket (バケッ, Baketsu?, lit. Bucket) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

The bucket splashes water on the board.

Question

Question (問題, mondai?, lit. Question) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Every new round of seek bar, a True/False Question appears. Hitting the true (◎) or false (x) button to answer it. If your answer is correct, you get a good mark. If your answer is wrong, you will face consequences, which will depend on the stage.

Cherry Blossom

Cherry Blossom (桜の花, Sakura no Hana?, lit. Cherry Blossoms) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

Collect 5 petals to create a full-blossomed cherry blossom, then hit it to collect it by clearing the units in the 4 adjacent areas to it.

Hit one petal first, then the two-petal flower will automatically form. Hit the 3rd to the 5th petals, and they will grow on the two-petal flower first.

Once a full-blossomed cherry blossom is formed, a whole cycle is reset.

Broom

Broom (ほうき, Hōki?, lit. Broom) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

It restores the moyamoya pieces back into normal pieces.

Sometimes, however, it increases the number of moyamoya pieces.

Pancake

Pancake (パンケーキ, Pankēki?, lit. Pancake) gimmick is movable by players, swapable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Gather the scattered pancakes onto the plate with a hit! When you stack the target number of pancakes, the Pancake Tower will be complete.

Once the Pancake Tower is finished, you can collect it with a hit.

Santa Claus

Santa Claus (サンタクロース, Santakurōsu?, lit. Santa Claus) is a self-movable gimmick, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Hit the Santa Claus by clearing the unit at the 4 adjacent areas to the Santa Claus, and the present flies toward the stocking.

Sometimes the present lands inside the stocking, and sometimes it misses.

Otomedashi

Otomedashi (おとめだし, otomedashi?, lit. lucky money) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

Inside the envelope, there is either one or three bills.

Hit the envelope to collect lots of bills!

Cube

Cube (or Block) (ブロック, Burokku?, lit. Block) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

Normal piece erasing cannot break the block, only inspiration, bomb, hirameki piece, and bomb piece can.

No damage occurs on the opposite side across a block.

Butterfly Cube

Butterfly Cube (or Butterfly Block) (蝶ブロック, Chō Burokku?, lit. Butterfly Block) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

A combination of block and butterfly gimmick. Break the block first by the effect of inspiration, bomb, hirameki piece, and bomb piece.

Then, hitting the butterfly 3 times releases the butterfly.

Billiard

Billiard (ビリャード, Biryādo?, lit. Billiards) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

Need to clear in sequence to clear all of them: 1-> 2 -> 3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6 -> 7 -> 8 -> 9

Beach Ball

Beach Ball (ビーチボール, Bīchibōru?, lit. Beach Ball) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, but removable from the board.

The number on the beach ball is the number of hits to 'kick' it out of the puzzle board, initially is 3 hits. After hitting, it will fly to any place on the board and is immovable.

You cannot control where the beach ball will land, and it can't 'fly'(move) only by hitting.

Condominium

Condomium (マンション, manshon?, lit. condominium) includes the condominium gimmick itself and materials to build a condominium: concrete and reinforcing bar. The condominium gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the stage. On the other hand, concrete and reinforcing bar gimmicks are immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

Hitting the materials (either concrete or reinforcing bar) and collecting them to build a condominium.

Lock

Lock (錠前, Jōmae?, lit. Lock) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board. Key gimmick is movable by players, swapable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Move the key to the 4 adjacent areas to the Lock, so that you can swatch them to unlock.

Key and Lock appear together on a stage, but there are times when the number of keys is more than the number of locks.

Glass Heart

Glass Heart (ガラスのハート, Garasu no Hāto?, lit. Glass Heart) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

When hit, one life is reduced.

※ Tips: In most of the stages, donut gimmick appears alongside to recover lives.
Time Bomb

Time Bomb (時限爆弾, Jigen Bakudan?, lit. time bomb) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

The number on the time bomb is the number of seek bar moving rounds left until it explodes. When it explodes, one life is reduced.

Note: Don't denote bomb or bomb piece near them; it also denote the time bomb and lose life. However, using gimmicks like hirameki or hirameki piece still can.

TV

TV gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

The color of the unit that was touched and erased is reflected on the TV.

Match according to the target color!
On the board, if there are N number of TVs, you need to make all of the TVs display the same color. When the last TV displays the same color, all TV displays won't change anymore.

Auction

Auction (オークション, Ōkushon?, lit. auction) includes an auction gimmick and a bill gimmick. The Auction gimmick gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board. The Bill gimmick is movable by players, swappable, and removable from the board.

Collect bundles of bills and bid for the painting!

There are two types of bills: bill a and bill b, where bill b is 5 times more than bill a. Bill a is a stack of 10.000 EN bills, which may contain 100 bills and is equivalent to 1 million EN.

Popcorn

Popcorn (ポップコーン, Poppukōn?, lit. Popcorn) gimmick is movable by players, swappable with pieces, and removable from the board.

Cat Search

Cat Search (猫さがし, Neko Sagashi?, lit. Cat Search) includes two gimmicks: footprints and cats. Both are immovable gimmicks, unswappable, but removable from the board.

When you hit a footprint, another footprint will appear elsewhere. Follow the footprints to catch the cat.
Fun fact: there are six cats available, each has the same footprints, but the directions of them aren't similar.

Cat Variants
Colored Glass

Colored Glass (カラーガラス, Karā Garasu?, lit. Colored Glass) gimmick is immovable, with swappable pieces on top of it, and removable from the board.

An upgraded version of glass plates, it's first introduced in the (Special Task) Travelogue: The Path Emerging in the Iridescence's stages.
On the glass plates, when the corresponding attribute pieces are cleared, it breaks.

There are glass plates that do not break with just one hit.

Perfect Ginger Cookie

Perfect Gingercookie (パーフェクトジンジャークッキー, Pāfekuto Jinjā Kukkī?, lit. Perfect Ginger Cookie) includes two gimmicks: perfect ginger cookie and oven. A perfect ginger cookie gimmick is a self-movable gimmick, movable by players, swappable with pieces, and removable from the board. Oven gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and unremovable from the board.

Guide the lost ginger cookie to the oven. When hit, it is pushed out in the opposite direction.

After putting the ginger cookie into the oven, ignite it with the effects of Bomb or Hirameki! Bake the Perfect Ginger Cookie.
Depending on the stages, the moving speed of the perfect ginger cookie gimmick may vary.

Wooden Box

Wooden Box (木箱, Kibako?, lit. Wooden Box) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

Break it with a hit.

Colored Wooden Box

Colored Wooden Box (カラー木箱, Karā Kibako?, lit. Colored Wooden Box) gimmick is immovable, unswappable, and removable from the board.

Break it by hitting with the pieces of the attribute written on the box.

Love Letter

Love Letter (ラブレター, Rabu Retā?, lit. Love Letter) gimmick is movable by players, swappable, and removable from the board.

Collect them by hitting.

Munching Goat

Munching Goat (ムシャムシャヤギ, Mushamusha Yagi?, lit. Munching Goat) is an self-movable gimmick, unswappable, and removable from the board.

The Munching Goat goes to eat the nearest love letter or bush.

If the love letter is eaten, it will not be counted toward the target number. Furthermore, after eating, it burps (or farts(?)) and moyamoya pieces will also be created.

※Tips: It moves either horizontally or vertically. If its path is blocked, it gets confused and stops moving. When either love letters or bushes don't appear anymore on the stage, it stops moving and becomes immovable till the end of the stage.

Hydrangea

Hydrangea (紫陽花, Ajisai?, lit. Hydrangea) gimmick is movable by players, swappable, and removable from the board.

“Let’s grow hydrangeas of various colors.” When you clear a unit next to them, the hydrangea bush is dyed in the color of that unit.

Hit the dyed hydrangea again with units of the same color, and harvest the grown hydrangea.
