From version 1.5.0, Sweets Land (スイーツランド, Suītsu Rando?) has new trios and quartets added to games; see also List of Sweets Land Snaps for more details on the previous release.

Requirements:

  • The lineup card team should have all of them.
  • They are in default mini styles, or mini styles from the 2nd Anniversary onwards.
