Overview]

As part of Suou's plan, Aporia decided to deep-clean the areas they normally use. They split up into their assigned sections, chatting away as they cleaned, but one by one, they found mysterious cards...

Event Gacha]

These cards are limited and aren't added to permanent pool after the event.

Play Activity]

Play puzzles to earn Event Pt Party Hat and receive rewards. Players can earn Event Pts in the regular playlists. Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina players use will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.

Event Bonus]

  • Bonus applied to Pts gains, up to a maximum of 200%.
  • Limit Break Card doesn't add bonuses.
  • All 9 Cards illustrated by Yukihiro Utako, which have been released up to date, are 50% UP!

Event Mission]

Daily Mission]

Refresh every morning at 4 AM JST:

  1. Open the Event Top page (Reward Stamina Charge Item [1] ×1)
  2. Use 10 Stamina (Reward 20pt)
  3. Clear puzzles 1 times in the event playlist (Reward Urgent file request ×1)
  4. Complete 3 daily tasks (Reward Crystal ×5)

Achievement Mission]

Clear puzzles 500 times Reward Title
