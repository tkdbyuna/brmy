Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationship]

  • House[2]: Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙 & Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 & Sei Okiya 📘

Three people are living under one roof, named House. House is a 5LDK share house belongs to Sei Okiya and was inherited from his grandmother.[3] Their relationship is balanced between Tsukimoto, who takes good care of the others, Ichikawa, who is selfish only with Tsukimoto, and Okiya, who warmly watches over them. They all get along well and normally gather in the living room to play games and so on.

  • Friends on the Veranda[4]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 and Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙

Two earnest individuals who hold each other in high regard for their character and work reliability. They frequently work together as a member of the Main Office and the chief accountant respectively, and they enjoy talking about their shared passion for cooking. Ichikawa has referred to their friendly dynamic as "elders sitting on the veranda."

  • Mama and the Owner[5]: Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙 and Shizuka Fushimi 💻

Fushimi regularly comes to check on the plants and flower beds that are growing in the House. Watering is on a rotation system, but since Tsukimoto is in charge of managing the plants, they frequently communicate for questions regarding seasonal repotting and maintenance. They also rely on each other when there are issues around the PC, so they unexpectedly have quite a bit of interaction.
