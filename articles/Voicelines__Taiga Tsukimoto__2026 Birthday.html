Characters Taiga Tsukimoto's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		苦労が絶えない中、よくやっている。経理というのは会社の柱だからな。これからも期待している。
He's doing well despite the constant hardships. Accounting is the backbone of the company after all; I'll keep expecting him to do well.

Yuzuru Kise 		また、お茶してください。今日は特別な日だと思いますので、別の機会に。この前は壱川くんに老人会と言われてしまいましたけど、そんな時間が俺は好きなので。
Let's have another tea together. Today should be a special day, so let's do it another time. Ichikawa-kun called it an elderly association event, but I enjoy that kind of time.

Roka Suoh 		これは‥‥‥前にタイガーが好きだって言ってたアニメのグッズ！なになに、ランダム５種十シークレット１種？全部買っていこう！
These are...... The goods from that anime Tiger said he likes! Let's see, 5 random + one secret? Let's buy them all!

Kou Ayato 		槻本さん、お誕生日おめでとうございます。今年もゆらがお祝いに行くと思うんで、よろしくお願いします。
Tsukimoto-san, happy birthday. I think Yuragi will be visiting to celebrate you again this year, please take care of him.

Mao Ukyo 		お菓子作りって、何気に材料費がバカにならないですよね。ゆらがいつもお世話になってるし、必要なものがあったら出させてください。
The cost of ingredients to make sweets is quite high. Yura has always been in your care, so please let me provide what you need to make sweets sometimes.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		大河さんお手製スイーツの話をする時のゆら、いつも機嫌いいんよ。俺もお願いしたら貰えるかな‥‥‥って、誕生日にねだるのは良くないかあ。
Yura is always in a good mood when he talks about Taiga-san's handmade sweets. Maybe I should ask for some too.... Ah, it's not good to ask for something when it's your birthday.

Yuragi Kanno 		大河のお祝いしにハウスに行ってくる。大きいケーキがあるのは確認済み。
I'm going to the House to celebrate Taiga. I'm sure there will be a big cake.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		今日は、春日と誓さんが食事を用意してくれたみたいで‥‥‥。つい、涙腺にきました。
It seems Haruhi and Sei-san will be preparing a meal for me today...... It just hits my tear glands.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		あーその通販の箱大河の誕生日プレゼント。この前話してたラノベ入ってる。誕生日おめ~。
Ahh, that mail order box is Taiga's birthday present. It has the light novel we talked about before. Happy birthday~

Sei Okiya 		大河がいてくれるからこその、共同生活です。末長く続けていくためこそ、今日くらいはゆっくりしていてください。
Our living together includes Taiga. In order to keep it going on for a long time, please take it easy today.

Shizuka Fushimi 		ハウスのグリーンを見に行く予定があれば、機本さんに祝いの言葉のひとつでもかけたけど‥‥‥。まあ、今頃散々言われてるか。
I was planning to congratulate Tsukimoto-san when I planned to check on the House's greenery. Though...... well, he must be hearing it a lot right now.

Takeru Mikado 		真面目で実直なことって、報われるはかりじゃないじゃない。そんな中で、不器用にそれを貫いてるとこが、いいのよ。
Being earnest and honest isn't always rewarding, is it? The way he still clumsily sticks to it is nice.

Soyogu Shinkai 		今日はおめでとうございます。現場の仕事って、何事もバックオフィスの支援あってのことなんで。大河さんに頭上がんないっす。
Congratulations for today. Any on-site work can only happen with the back office's support. I can only keep bowing to Taiga-san.

Shinobu Aizawa 		部長会があるならワンチャン機本さんに会えるかも知れないし、なんかプレゼント買ってこよっかな！
There's a chance I might be able to meet Tsukimoto-san since there's a department leaders' meeting. I should buy something as a present for him!

Kiho Arima 		本当に年下とは思えないほどしっかりしているよ。何か困ったことがあればいつでも声かけて。
He really has it together to the point I don't think he's younger than me. Feel free to call me whenever you have trouble.

Kyoya Shido 		大河くんへのお祝いか‥‥‥ 料理もお菓子も上手に作る人だから、食器なんてどうかな。使い勝手も含めて好みがあるだろうから、本人に聞いてみようか。
A celebration for Taiga-kun...... He's skilled in cooking and making sweets, so how about tableware? They have different usage and types, so I guess I should ask the person himself.

Riku Tateshina 		いやあ、いつも曲者な誓を相手に頑張ってくれてるから‥‥‥と思ったけど春日にも手を焼いてたな。２倍労わらないと。
Well, he always does his best to face a tricky guy like Sei...... was what I was thinking, but he also takes care of Haruhi. That's twice the hard work.

Tomose Onda 		槻本に誕生日を祝ったら、随分と恐縮した様子だった。もっと気を抜いてくれていい。
Tsukimoto seems quite awkward when I celebrated his birthday. He should be more relaxed.

Yu Nina 		環野からたまに槻本の料理の腕を聞く。他の人間にも評判がいいみたいだな。
I hear about Tsukimoto's cooking skills from Kanno sometimes. It seems to be well-received by other people, too.

Kamiya 		機本さんへのプレゼントは小さいものだけど、この前Aporiaで隠岐谷さんに預け済み！会う機会はなくてもかなりお世話になってるから、少しでも喜んでもらえたら嬉しいな。
My present for Tsukimoto-san is small, but I've entrusted it to Okiya-san back in Aporia before. I've been quite in his care even though we don't really meet, so I'd be glad if it makes him happy.

Urara Manami 		バカ真面目で生きづらそうな奴。案外、ここじゃ珍しいかもな。
A stupidly earnest guy who lives difficult. Someone surprisingly rare here.
