Description]

Beloved incompetent troublemaker
Simple-minded and honest. Brimming with curiosity, he wants to try doing everything, yet cannot do everything. People strangely don't hate him since he has no evil intentions. Widely connected to questionable people and has a lot of acquaintances. Although he shows a rare glimpse of his ability, no one knows the truth.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Main Office[2]: Ai Kosaka 👑 & Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Roka Suoh 🦎

While there is a relationship of trust, they are not "close friends," so private interactions are limited, and there are no department-wide drinking parties. Suoh also never suggests "let's get together just the department," so relationships between individuals are deeper than those within the department itself.

  • Founding Pair[3]: Roka Suoh 🦎 and Kiho Arima 🔮

The two people founded Aporia. They met at a previous job in a host club [4] and have known each other for over 10 years. Although the free and spontaneous Suoh’s absurdness constantly amazes Arima, Arima never fails to meet the expectations. Arima may seem to be the older one, but Suoh is actually four years older.

  • Ended Up Roommates[5]: Roka Suoh 🦎 and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

After deciding to go on a years-long overseas trip, Suoh lent his apartment to Aizawa for safekeeping. However, Suoh returned much sooner than anticipated, and the two have been living together since then. As Suoh is frequently out, Aizawa essentially lives alone half the time.

  • Ikebukuro Crew[6]: Roka Suoh🦎 & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 & Tomose Onda 🌏 & Yu Nina🚬

The quartet lives in the same apartment block in Ikebukuro. Suoh and Aizawa are roommates, the same as Onda and Nina. The apartments are slightly old, but they’re spacious for the rent, comfortable to live in, and in a good location. It is not a dorm.

Whisky Cola / ウイスキーコーラ
Japanese ウイスキーをコーラで割ったカクテル。王道のコーラの甘さにウイスキーのコクが加わり、一気に大人な味に。Aporiaにはコーラ割りを好むスタッフがいるため、ぜひ、おすすめの割り方を聞いてみては
English A cocktail made by mixing whiskey and cola. The rich flavor of the whiskey complements the classic sweetness of the cola, creating a sophisticated taste. Since some of the staff at Aporia are fans of whiskey and cola, why not ask them for their recommended way to enjoy it?
New York* / ニューヨーク
Japanese 吏来から聞いたんだけれども、このカクテルの名前はニューヨークの日の出をイメージして作られたんだとか！それを聞いたら、実物と並べてみたくなってしまうネッ☆‥‥‥旅が僕を呼んでいる！！
English I heard from Riku that this cocktail was named after the sunrise in New York! Hearing that makes me want to compare it to the real thing☆... A trip is calling for me!!
Greedy ☆ Float* / よくばり☆フロート
Japanese アメリカといえば、ボリューミー、ボリューミーといえば、僕！みたいなところがあるからネッあれもこれも美味しいところを欲張ってしまおう！もっとアイスを乗せたい、という注文も受け付けているよ☆
English When you mention America, it's the volume, if you say volume, then it's me! There's that kind of thing, you know? Wanting to put all the good things makes me greedy! I'm open to additional ice requests ☆
White Swan / ホワイト・スワン
Japanese アマレットと牛乳で作る杏仁豆腐のようなデサートカクテル。鼻から抜ける杏仁の香りとまろやかなミルクが織りなす美味しさは、Aporiaの甘党達がはっちり保証済み
English Made with amaretto and milk, it's a dessert cocktail resembling almond tofu. The aroma of almonds that wafts through the nose and the smoothness of milk create a deliciousness guaranteed by Aporia's sweet tooths.
Banana Fizz* / バナナ・フィズ
Japanese 世界を巡る僕のちょっとした豆知識コーナ☆ “フィズ”とは、炭酸が弾ける音のことなんだって！このカクテルをオーダーしたら、 耳を澄ませてみて。 フィズフィズ、フィズフィズ！って音が聞こえる‥‥‥気がする、かもしれない！トロピカルな1杯をせひとも楽しんでネッ！
English My Little Trivia Corner Around the World☆ “Fizz” refers to the sound of carbonation bubbling up! When you order this cocktail, listen closely. You might hear the fizz, fizz, fizz sounds... or at least that’s how it feels! Make sure to enjoy this tropical drink!
Noble Purple Freeze* / ノーブル・パープル・フリーズ
Japanese 篠と一緒に、家で雑誌を頭に乗せてモデルウォークの練習をしたんだけど なんと！2時間の猛特訓の末に篠が雑誌を落とさず10歩前進することに成功！！この調子で目指せスーバーモデル☆
English While practicing model walks at home with Shino, balancing a magazine on our heads, we succeeded after 2 hours of intense training! Shino managed to take 10 steps forward without dropping the magazine!! At this rate, let's aim for supermodel status!☆
Cassis Orange / カシスオレンジ
Japanese 深いルビー色が印象的で、お酒の場でも定番のロングカクテル。アルコール度数も低いので、のんびり飲む夜にピッタリ。
English With its deep ruby color, this long cocktail is a staple in drinking places. Its low alcohol content makes it perfect for a relaxing night.
French Emerald* / フレンチ・エメラルド
Japanese エメラルドと名のつくお酒なのに、ブルーキュラソーを使っているのはなんとも不思議な感じがするよねっ！少し前に、ハマグリが栗ではないと知った時の衝撃を思い出したよ‥‥‥！え、もしかしてみんな知ってた！？
English It feels quite strange that a drink named "Emerald" uses blue curaçao! It reminds me of the shock I felt when I learned that clams aren't chestnuts...! Wait, did everyone else know that!?
Special ☆ Champagne Tower * / スペシャル☆シャンノヾンタワー
Japanese この間カフェで「シャンバンタワーをしたい」って言ったら、あっけーが特別に小さいタワーを作ってくれたんだ！しかも中身がパインジュースでね。僕でも最後まで飲みきれてすごく嬉しかったんだ。そういう気遣いできる子が、うちのバーカンにいるって幸せだよね☆
English The other day at the café, I said I wanted to try a "champagne tower," and Akke specially made a mini tower for me! Plus, it was filled with pineapple juice. I was really happy that I could finish it all by myself. It's such a joy to have someone who can show that kind of consideration in our bar!☆
