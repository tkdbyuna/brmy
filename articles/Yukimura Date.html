Yukimura Date

Description]

The owner of CAFNÉ, a salon in the same building as Aporia. He is an open-minded person with a loud voice, and when he’s drunk, his laughter is even more explosive. Bright, lively, and good at going with the flow, he somehow gets along well with Suoh. For some reason, he’s not popular.[1]

Etymology]

  • 伊達 Date consists of 伊 da and 達 te, means stylish, elegance
    • 伊 da means Italy, that one
    • 達 te means to reach
  • 幸邑 Yukimura consists of 幸 yuki and 邑 mura
    • 幸 yuki means happiness, good luck
    • 邑 mura means village

Trivia]

  • Every month, the heroine comes to his salon to maintain her hair, and it has been several years since the story began.[2]
  • He has never worked as an employee in a company.[2]
