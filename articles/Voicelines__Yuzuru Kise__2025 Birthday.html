Characters Yuzuru Kise's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		由鶴は他人の世話をしてばかりだからな。だから今日くらいは、平和に過ごさせたい
Since Yuzuru always keeps taking care of other people, I want to at least make him spend today peacefully.

Yuzuru Kise 		皆さんがお祝いしてくれて、とっても賑やかな１日になりました。もちろんこの“みんな”には、貴方も入っているんですよ
Today has become a lively day thanks to everyone's celebration. Of course, you are included in "everyone".

Roka Suoh 		ゆづくんは本日の主役なんだから、もっと主張して良いのだよ！　今日は何を言っても、僕のカリスマ性でもってなんでも叶えよう！
Yuzu-kun is today's main star, so it's fine to be more assertive! I shall make anything come true using my charisma, no matter what you ask for!

Kou Ayato 		誕生日おめでとう、アンド、いつもありがとう。由鶴いなかったら崩壊してると思うよ、割と真面目に
Happy birthday and thank you for always. We would've collapsed if it wasn't for Yuzuru, like seriously.

Mao Ukyo 		由鶴おめでとう。今日くらいは、全体重のっけるくらいの気持ちで僕たちに寄りかかってよ。って言っても、逆に気を使うかな？
Yuzuru, congrats. Lean on us as if you're trusting your whole body weight on us for today. Ah, perhaps telling so made you become more considerate instead?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		由鶴さんが嬉しそうにしてると安心するんよ。安らかな誕生日であることを願ってやまへんわ
I'm relieved to see Yuzuru-san being all happy. I can't stop wishing for you to have a peaceful birthday.

Yuragi Kanno 		由鶴には長生きしてもらわないと困るから‥‥‥なんかいいプレゼントあんた、知ってる？
It'll be troubling to not have Yuzuru live long...... Do you know any nice gifts for him?

Taiga Tsukimoto 		城瀬くんは誕生日まで働き回ってるんでしょうね。なにか時短グッズでも贈るか‥‥‥
I guess Kise-kun will keep working even during his birthday. I should give him some time-saving products.......

Haruhi Ichikawa 		由鶴さんの誕生日‥‥‥LIMEでなんかギフト送っとくか
Yuzuru-san's birthday......I should probably send him a gift through LIME.

Sei Okiya 		由鶴くんには日頃からお世話になっていますからねえ、管理部をあげてお祝いしなくては。楽しいことになりそうです
Yuzuru-kun has been taking care of us daily, so we Administrative Dept. should celebrate him. That sounds like a fun idea.

Shizuka Fushimi 		おめでとう。無理するなといっても人によってキャパなんて違うからね、無理矢理、意識することでもない
Congrats. Even if someone told you not to force yourself, each person has their own capacity, so it's not like you need to force yourself to become aware of it.

Takeru Mikado 		由鶴、いつも曲者達を取り纏めお疲れさま♡肩が凝った時は言ってね、力を込めて揉んであげる
Yuzuru, good job on bringing together all the odd people♡Tell me whenever your shoulders feel stiff, I will massage it using my power.

Soyogu Shinkai 		由鶴さんも相当大変だよな‥‥‥。誕生日だし、なんか差し入れでもするか
Yuzuru-san has it quite difficult...... Since it's his birthday, I should give him some refreshments.

Shinobu Aizawa 		１個上とは思えない包容力、慈悲深さ‥‥‥Aporiaって由鶴さんなしには回ってないですよね
The amount of tolerance and compassion I wouldn't expect from someone a year older...... Aporia certainly wouldn't work without Yuzuru-san.

Kiho Arima 		由鶴、カフェの方の切り盛りをいつもありがとう。‥‥‥芦佳は人に恵まれる才能がずば抜けてるよね
Yuzuru, thank you for always managing the café...... Roka has an outstanding talent in getting blessed by people.

Kyoya Shido 		由鶴くんはこんな日まで誰かのために‥‥‥ここまでくると、性質だろうから指摘するのも悪いかな
Yuzuru-kun keeps doing things for someone even on this day...... At this point, it's probably in his nature, so pointing that out might be bad.

Riku Tateshina 		今日くらいゆっくり過ごして‥‥‥と思ったけど、祝われるのだけでも忙しそうだわ
I hope he can spend today leisurely......though, he seems busy getting celebrated.

Tomose Onda 		有が世話になってる。人手が足りなければ俺にも言ってくれ
Yu has been in your care. Feel free to tell me whenever you need more hands.

Yu Nina 		城瀬はなんでも美味しいと言うと指摘したことがある。大雑把な言葉だが、人をポジティブにさせる言葉だと思う
I once pointed out how Kise says everything is delicious. It's a word with broad meaning, but I think it gives a positive feeling to people.

Kamiya 		由鶴さんのサポートがあるから伸び伸び働けてるなっていつも感謝してる。ってことでありがとうのお菓子どうぞ！
I'm always grateful to Yuzuru-san since his support makes me able to work freely. With that said, please take these sweets as a gratitude!

Urara Manami 		神経すり減らしてまでがんばることか？　お人よしは大変だな
Is it worth working hard even if it wears you out? Good-natured people have it hard.
