Description]

Amnesiac ballerino
Doesn't have any memories before coming to the store. Doesn't even know his own name or age. He is a cheerful, caring, and quick-tempered person. He has a sense of distance from Onda and Nina, and often spends time with Manami, but he is disliked by Manami himself.[2]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • ST Dept.[3]: Tomose Onda 🌏 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Kamiya 🍩 & Urara Manami 🧹

"Though somewhat isolated from others, they surprisingly work well together"—a wolf pack-like team. While Onda and Nina generally avoid communicating with others, they chat casually with Kamiya and show concern for Manami. Though complaints may arise, their opinions never diverge.

  • Ferocious & Magnanimous[4]: Urara Manami🧹 and Kamiya 🍩

Kamiya enjoys looking after Manami for various reasons, particularly because Manami is the only one younger than him. However, Manami views this behavior as hypocritical and finds it "annoying." Despite Manami's aggressive attitude toward him, Kamiya remains unfazed and harbors no ill feelings.

  • Lighthearted and Active[5]: Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 & Kamiya 🍩

Young people with footwork as light as feathers and the stamina to play energetically. They play both day and night. They're adept at discerning the lines they mustn't cross, maintaining an exquisite sense of distance in their relationships.

◆ Personal]

  • Tomose Onda 🌏 and Yu Nina 🚬: He was first found by Onda and Nina when he was covered in wounds. Since he had no recollection of who he was, he was named Kamiya by Suoh based on Arima's tarot reading[6]. He was assigned to Main Office at first, but he decided to join ST Dept.. Later, Manami transferred from Watchdog Dept. and partnered with him since then.

Etymology]

  • 神家 Kamiya derived from 神の家 means "House of God". This is another name for major arcana "The Tower" as mentioned by Arima when Kamiya picked out the card for the reading.[6]
    • 神 kami means god
    • 家 ya means house

Trivia]

  • His designated major arcana card is “XVI: The Tower.” His 2nd Personal Song shows an upright card, which signifies sudden upheaval, broken pride, and disaster.[7]
