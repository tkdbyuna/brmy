SSR Tomose Onda (Here's to Aporia!) Lines
Home ①
 お前の活躍は特務部でも話題に上がるからな、耳に届いている。ウチと言ってもほぼ神家だが
Your achievements have been a topic of discussion even in the ST department; I’ve heard about them. Even if I say “our place,” it’s practically Kamiya.
Home ②
 やたらと祝いの言葉を耳にすると思えば、なるほど、1年か。祝うべきことなら、俺も祝おう
If I keep hearing so many words of celebration, I see, it’s been a year. If it’s something to celebrate, then I’ll celebrate too.
Home ③
 皆がそうしているように、俺もこの機会に礼を言う。思えばこの1年、世話になることもあったからな
Just like everyone else, I’ll take this opportunity to express my gratitude. Thinking back on this past year, there have been times when I received help.
