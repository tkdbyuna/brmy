Characters Soyogu Shinkai's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		そのデカい図体を、またデカく成長させるつもりはないだろうな。人様の邪魔になるような１年にはするなよ
I hope you don't intend to make your big figure even bigger. Don't make this a year where you get in other people's way.

Yuzuru Kise 		戦みたいな人を文武両道って言うんだろうなと思います。二兎追うもの一兎も得ずなんてことわざもありますが、戦の前では関係ありませんね
I think someone like Soyogu is what we call as academic and athletic excellence. There's a saying which goes "chase two rabbits, and you will catch neither," but I guess that doesn't apply to Soyogu.

Roka Suoh 		グッチにはトレーニンググッズをプレゼント！　ついでに僕も使い方を教えてもらって、一緒に鍛えちゃおう作戦も実行だ！
Some training goods as a present for Gucchi! In addition, you should teach me how to use it, and we commence the plan of training together!

Kou Ayato 		ハッピーバースデー戦～。『寮飲み-新開戦バースデースペシャルエディション-』のご招待にきました。篠信たちがすでにありえないくらい盛り上がってるから、早く助けにきて
Happy birthday Soyogu~ I came by the invitation of "Drinking Party -Soyogu Shinkai Birthday Special Edition-" Shinobu and the others are already getting all excited, please come save me soon.

Mao Ukyo 		あそこまで鍛えるには並々ならない努力があっただろうね。まあ、戦なら普通にできちゃいそうだと思えるからすごいよ
Being that trained means he has put unexceptional amount of effort for it. Well, Soyogu makes me think he can surely achieve it, which is amazing on its own.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		戦さんも、せっかくの誕生日なんやしもっとはしゃごうや。今が一番若いってよく言うやろ？
Soyogu-san, you should get more excited about your own birthday. Don't they often say we are the youngest in this moment?

Yuragi Kanno 		この間、戦が猫が集まってるスポットを教えてくれた。ランニング中に見つけたんだって。今度行ってみる
Last time, Soyogu told me a cat gathering spot. He said he found it when he went for a run. I'll try going there next time.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		新開くんがハウスに来ると、春日も誓さんも気持ちシャッキリするというか‥‥‥。普段と違う雰囲気になる気がするよ
l'd say both Haruhi and Sei-san feel kind of refreshed whenever Shinkai-kun visits the House...... The vibes simply feel different than usual.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		本よく読むみたいだしこれあげるわ、本に使えるギフト券。変に選ぶより金券が一番でしょ
I'm giving you this since you seem to read a book often. A gift voucher for books. Coupons should be better than me choosing something weird.

Sei Okiya 		いつもアシスタントありがとうございます。戦くんのお陰で、伸び伸びお仕事をさせてもらっていますよ。どうか心からの感謝を受け取ってもらえたら
Thank you very much for assisting me everytime. I'm able to work quite freely thanks to Soyogu-kun. Please accept this gratitude from my heart.

Shizuka Fushimi 		新開に限って体鍛えすぎて壊れることはないと思うけど、その辺は気を抜かずに。これからも、たくさん動いてもらうから
Shinkai should be the only one who wont break since his body is that trained, but make sure you dont let your guard down on that area. I'll make you work a lot from now on too, after all.

Takeru Mikado 		ほら、主役なんだからもっとテンションあげなさい！　今日は朝まで、いや誕生日が終わるまで、飲みたいところね
Come on, you should be more excited as the main character! We shall drink until morning, no, until your birthday ends for today!

Soyogu Shinkai 		こんなに祝われるっつーのも慣れねえもんだな。けどありがてえよ、そこは素直に言っとく
I can't get used to getting celebrated like this, but thanks, really, I shall say that honestly.

Shinobu Aizawa 		お祝いの気持ちたっぷり込めて、グッチにトレーニングウェアのプレゼント！　“グッチ”って刺繍を入れてもらうのはやめといた。えらい！
Putting in all my feelings of celebration, here's a training wear as a present for you, Gucchi! I decided not to embroider the name "Gucchi"! How admirable of me!

Kiho Arima 		戦に会えたからおめでとうを伝えたけど、ほんと離れたところにいてもすぐにわかるよね。目を引く理由は単にフィジカルだけじゃないと思うよ
I congratulated Soyogu since I was able to meet him earlier, but he's really distinguishable even from afar I do think something that catches someone's eye isn't always something physical.

Kyoya Shido 		新開くんの誕生日にプロテインの詰め合わせはどうかと思ったんだけど。セロリ味‥‥‥本当にこの味も飲めるものなのかな？
I was thinking of giving Shinkai-kun some protein assortments for his birthday. Celery flavor...... Is this flavor really drinkable?

Riku Tateshina 		良い感じの万年筆を見つけてね。なんだか戦のこと思い出したから買っちゃったよ。誕生日ってことで受け取ってくれると良いけど
I found a nice fountain pen, you see. It reminded me of Soyogu, so I bought it. It'd be nice if you accept it for your birthday.

Tomose Onda 		誕生日だろうと普段通りでいいという新開には俺も同意する。何事も、本題に入るのは早い方がいいことが多い
I agree with how Shinkai said that it's fine to keep things as usual even on birthdays. In many cases, it's better to get straight to the point.

Yu Nina 		Aporiaであった時に見たことのない色のプロテインを振っていた。何を摂るかは本人の自由だが
He was carrying a protein in a color I've never seen when I met him at Aporia. Anyone is free to consume whatever they want.

Kamiya 		今度、ランニング行く時に俺も一緒について行きたい！　新開さんと並んで走ったら、すごい鍛えられそう
I want to join you when you go for a run next time! I feel like I can get way more trained when I run next to Shinkai-san.

Urara Manami 		やれやれみてえな顔してんなよ。普段より顔緩んでるくせしてよ
Stop making a conceding face when your face is clearly more relaxed than usual.
