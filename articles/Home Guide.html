Homescreen]

On the Home screen, you can use features such as announcements and the Present Box,

◆How to Play Game]

[PUZZLE]
Main article: Groove Match Puzzle

Select a playlist to play Groove Match Puzzle. When you play a puzzle, you can earn Card Lv. EXP and NICE points, as well as items for strengthening your cards.

[STORY]
Main article: Story Guide

Access the Main Story featuring 21 unique staff members, as well as various other features like puzzles and story modes.

[SPIN]
Main article: Snap'n Spin

You can play the snap photo game “Spin.” Playing Spin lets you earn Story Lv. EXP, which is needed to unlock card stories, items for enhancing Story Lv. and NICE points.

[GACHA]
Main article: Gacha

You can use items like Crystals and Gacha Tickets to obtain staff cards. Use the cards you’ve obtained to play Puzzles and Spin.

[STAFF]
Main article: Cards

You can view a list of the cards you own. Use items obtained from Puzzles and Spin to enhance your cards.

Home settings]

You can change the Live2D models, background images, and card illustrations displayed on your home screen. There are two modes for home settings: card illustrations and Live2D models.

  • [Card illustration]: You can set up to four cards.
  • [Live2D model]: You can set up to two staff Live2D characters and a home background.

The mode displayed on the home screen can be selected by tapping Switch between the [Card Illustration] and [Live2D Model] tabs. When you return to the home screen, the last settings you viewed will be applied.

Announcements]

In the [Announcements] section, you can check the latest game information, including ongoing events, campaigns, and update details.

Announcements are divided into these categories:

  • Important
  • Exchange Shop: Exchange Shop's monthly renewal
  • Event: related to Events, Marverlous Challenge's renewal
  • Campaign: related to campaigns
  • Malfunction: acknowledgment of bug
  • Update: Game version updates
  • Miscellaneous: Story related release, Bremile pass, real-life event, Personal Song, etc.

Mission]

Main article: Mission

You can earn rewards based on your gameplay progress through missions.

◆ Mission Types]

[Daily Missions]

Progress resets every day at 4:00 AM JST.

[Main Missions]

These appear based on your gameplay progress.

※Unlike Daily Missions, these do not reset.

[Achievements]
See also: Titles

These appear based on your gameplay progress.

※Unlike Daily Missions, these do not reset.

[Special Missions]

These appear during campaign periods and other events.

[Bremile Missions]

Bremile are points that accumulate based on the Stamina you consume through activities like Puzzles and Spin.

※You earn 1 Bremile for every 1 Stamina consumed.

You can claim rewards in the [Bremile] section of the missions based on the Bremile you’ve accumulated.

※Bremile Missions reset at 4:00 AM JST on the 1st of every month.

Additionally, purchasing a Bremile Pass from the Shop unlocks Premium Missions, where you can earn extra luxurious rewards.

◆ Mission Rewards]

  • Tap the [Receive] button to claim your rewards.
  • The [Collect All] button allows you to claim all completed rewards for each mission type at once.
  • Any mission rewards not claimed by the end of the mission period will be sent to your Present Box the next day or later when you log in.

※Please note that the Present Box has a claim deadline.

  • Missions for which you have already claimed rewards will be moved to the bottom of the list.

◆ Notes on Puzzle Missions]

Puzzle stages that do not require Stamina consumption, such as Marverlous Challenge, are not counted toward mission progress.

Present Box]

In the Present Box, you can claim various login bonuses and presents distributed by the developers.

◆ Present]

  • You can claim presents by tapping the [Claim] button or the [Claim All] button.
  • Distributed presents have a claim deadline. Please check the [Claim Deadline] displayed on each gift.

※Please note that presents will be deleted once their expiration date has passed.
※If the number of unclaimed presents exceeds 200, the oldest ones will be deleted.

◆ Claim History]

  • You can view the presents you have already claimed by selecting [View Claim History].
  • Up to 200 presents claimed within the last 7 days will be displayed.

Stamina]

Stamina is consumed when you play puzzles or spin the wheel.

◆ Stamina Recovery]

  • Stamina recovers by 1 every 10 minutes.

※Stamina does not recover over time when your stamina is 20 or higher.

◆ Recovery Using Crystals and Items]

  • Tap the plus "+" button next to the Stamina display to recover Stamina using crystals or items.
  • When using crystals or items, you can restore up to 40 Stamina.

※You cannot restore Stamina beyond the maximum limit.

◆ Stamina Maximum Limit]

  • If Stamina is restored due to a rank-up or returned when you give up during a puzzle game, you cannot hold more than 100 Stamina.

Coin]

You can use them to upgrade cards or exchange them for items at the exchange.

◆ How to Earn Coins]

  • You can earn them as rewards for playing Puzzles, Spin, and completing missions.
  • You can earn them at Exchange Shop such as the Crystal Exchange.

Crystal]

Crystals are items that can primarily be used for the following purposes:

  • Gacha
  • Restoring Stamina
  • Exchanging for items at the Crystal Exchange

◆ How to Obtain Crystals]

In addition to purchasing them in the Shop, you can also earn them as in-game rewards from missions and other sources.

◆ About Paid and Free Crystals]

There are two types of crystals: paid crystals and free crystals. Some gacha draws, such as Step-Up gacha, may only be played using paid crystals.

※When consuming crystals without specifying whether they are paid or free, free crystals will be used first.

User Rank]

By earning User Rank XP through playing puzzles and spins, you can increase your User Rank. Raising your User Rank increases the maximum number of friends you can follow and unlocks the main story. You can check your current User Rank in the top-left corner of the Home screen.

◆ User Rank Cap]

  • There is a cap on your User Rank.[Note 1]
  • Once you reach the cap, instead of User Rank XP, you will receive an item called a Rank Reward based on the Stamina you consume, 1 Rank Reward per 1 Stamina.

Rank Reward]

When your user rank reaches the maximum level, this item is awarded based on the amount of stamina you’ve consumed (1 Rank Reward per 1 Stamina), instead of user rank experience points.

You can exchange the rank rewards you’ve earned for items at the Exchange Shop.

<img>

◆ About Rank Reward Acquisition Limits]

  • There is a weekly limit of 1,000 Rank Rewards.
  • You can check your current acquisition status at the Rank Reward Exchange.

※The Rank Reward acquisition limit resets every Monday at 4:00 AM JST.

Login Bonus]

Rewards are distributed every time you log in.

◆ Types of Login Bonuses]

[Daily Login Bonus]

You can claim a reward once per day.

※Resets daily at 4:00 AM JST.

[Cumulative Login Bonus]

You can claim rewards based on the total number of days you’ve logged in.

[Special Login Bonus]

Available during special campaigns and events.

[Login Story]
Further information: Login Story

Limited story available during special campaigns and events. You can only read the past login story during the campaign/event period.

Check]

Tap the [CHECK] button on the home screen to view a list of TOP NEWS, current campaigns, gacha events, and limited voices.

Notes]

  1. ↑ As of Apr 1st, 2026, the user rank cap is 40.
