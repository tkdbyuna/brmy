Gameplay

Break My Case's system has 3 main gameplay modes, which are Groove Match Puzzle, Snap'n Spin, and Story Mode.

Groove Match Puzzle]

A new gaming experience that combines music and match puzzles.

Main article: Groove Match Puzzle


When you "match" pieces of the same color, a seek bar moves in time with the music to erase the pieces!

Aim for a high score with your carefully selected team composition while enjoying the colorful background music that reflects the image of the Aporia staff.

Snap'n Spin]

Get to know them better through these snapshots.

Main article: Snap'n Spin


A mode to strengthen the story level required to unlock "Card Story."

Tap the spinning reel to visit the photo spot where the staff are.

If you press the shutter button to take a snapshot, you might discover a surprising side of them.

Story Mode]

A moving storytelling experience.

Main articles: Story and Story Guide


You can enjoy the ‘Main Story’, which centres on the heroine as she takes on the role of acting deputy owner at the café-bar ‘Aporia’, as well as the ‘Personal Story’, which allow you to get to know the Aporia staff more deeply.

There are also ‘Card Story’, which are unlocked based on your Story Lv., and ‘Event Story’, which are released alongside the in-game events.
