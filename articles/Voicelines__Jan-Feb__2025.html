Characters Jan - Feb 2025

Ai Kosaka 		体調管理を怠るな。お前も暖かくしろ
Don’t neglect your health. Go stay warm.

Yuzuru Kise 		外に出たら息が白くて。暖房の温度、皆さん大丈夫かな‥‥‥。ちょっと聞いてきますね
My breath was so white when I went out. I wonder if everyone is fine with the heater's temperature....... I'll go and ask around.

Roka Suoh 		雪が積もったら雪合戦をしよう！　君と僕が組めば最強バッテリーだ！
Let's have a snowball fight when the snow piles up! The two of us together will become the strongest battery!

Kou Ayato 		この時期さすがにバイクはなー。早くあったかくなってほしい
Riding a bike during this season is obviously not nice. I want it to get warmer soon.

Mao Ukyo 		この時期着物着てる人も多くて、つい見ちゃう。君も着たくなったら言って、手伝うから
There are lots of people wearing kimonos during this season, it makes me unconsciously look at them. Tell me when you want to wear one, I'll help out.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		今年の目標立てたりするん？　俺はそういうのあんまりせえへんから気になって
Did you come up with some resolutions for this year? I don't really do something like that, so I'm curious.

Yuragi Kanno 		冬、嫌いじゃない。チョコいっぱい売ってるから
I don't hate winters. Lots of chocolates are being sold after all.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		足元冷えてないですか？　よければこちらのブランケットを‥‥‥あ、洗濯したばかりのものなので、そこはご安心を
Aren't your feet cold? Please use this blanket...... Ah, it's something that I just washed, so please rest assured.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		さむ‥‥‥おこげ、こっちきて‥‥‥
Cold...... Okoge, come here......

Sei Okiya 		おや、大河が私を呼んでいますね。申し訳ないのですが、この炬燵が私を離したくないようで‥‥‥
Oh, Taiga is calling me. I’m very sorry, but it seems this kotatsu doesn’t want to let me go......

Shizuka Fushimi 		寒いって言ったらあいつら自分が着てるもの１枚ずつ寄越してきて、ダルマみたいになった。君も発言には気をつけた方がいい
When I said it was cold, those guys quickly dropped a layer of their clothing on me, making me look like a daruma. You'd better be careful of what you say, too.

Takeru Mikado 		この時期はとにかく乾燥するから、全身念入りに保湿ケアしてあげなくちゃいけなくて毎日のお風呂上がりが大変よ‥‥‥
It gets so dry during this time of the year, so I have to moisturize my body thoroughly. Getting out of the bath every day is dreadful......

Soyogu Shinkai 		寒い日は意外とランニングが捗る。適度に体あったまって丁度いいんだわ
Running on cold days is suprisingly productive. It's perfect to moderately warm up the body.

Shinobu Aizawa 		さっむ‥‥‥！　けど俺、この感じ嫌いじゃないんですよね、空気が凛としてる感じ
So cold......! But I don't really hate this feeling of chilly air.

Kiho Arima 		まだしばらくベッドから出たくない季節が続くね。寒い寒い
The season that makes you not want to get out of bed will go on for a while. So cold, so cold.

Kyoya Shido 		雪が解ければ見えてくるものがある‥‥‥か。俺にとっての君はどうだろう？
There is something that will become visible when the snow melts. ..... What are you to me?

Riku Tateshina 		はー‥‥‥寒くて参るねえ。家から出るのが億劫になるよ
Hahh...... It’s so cold. Leaving the house is too much hassle.

Tomose Onda 		冷えると思わぬところで体を痛めやすい、十分に気をつけろ
You're more likely to injure yourself in unexpected places when you're cold, so be careful.

Yu Nina 		手が悴んでは任務に支障が出る。暖かくしておけ
Cold hands will hinder the mission. Keep warm.

Kamiya 		はー、寒い！　こんな寒いのに麗なんかたまにダメージデニム穿いてて、若さってやつ‥‥‥？
Hahh, so cold! It's so cold yet Urara still wear damaged jeans sometimes, is that what they call youth......?

Urara Manami 		冬の鳥はなんかまるまるしてんな。かわ‥‥‥チッ
Birds in winter are so round and fluffy. Cu-...... Tch.
