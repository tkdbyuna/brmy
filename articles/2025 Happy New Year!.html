Overview]

Happy New Year!
It might have happened somewhere—
We present six stories about a day they spent celebrating New Year's.

We look forward to your continued support in 2025.

Play Activity]

Play normal puzzle song to earn Pt Kagami Mochi.
Each stamina in event stage 1 corresponds to 2025 Pts. You can use triple stamina, which will triple the corresponding Pts.

Daily Mission]

  1. Open the Event Top page (Reward Stamina Charge [1] x1)
  2. Clear puzzles 1 times in the event playlist (Reward Urgent File Request x1 )
