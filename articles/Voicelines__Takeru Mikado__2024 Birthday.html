Characters Takeru Mikado's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		ミカの誕生日か‥‥‥。何でもいいから何か一言？　そうだな。これを機に『逢ちゃん』と呼ぶのをやめろ
Mika's birthday huh...... Anything I want to say? Right. Then using this chance, stop calling me "Ai-chan".

Yuzuru Kise 		さっきミカさんにお祝いをしたら、俺にも日頃の感謝を伝えてくれて‥‥‥人の心まで華やかにする、すごい人です
When I celebrated Mika-san earlier, he even told me his daily gratitude for me...... He's an amazing person who can brighten someone's heart.

Roka Suoh 		いつも明るく切り盛りしてくれる君の存在は、間違いなくAporiaのミラーボールさ！
Your existence, which runs the place cheerfully, is without a doubt Aporia's disco ball!

Kou Ayato 		プレゼント、真央に聞いてコスメとか‥‥‥って思ったけど相手の得意ジャンルで攻めるのはハイリスクだよなー
I asked Mao what kind of present I should give, and he said something like cosmetics...... though, giving something the other party is specialized at is very high risk.

Mao Ukyo 		ミカ姉おめでと。コスメでミカ姉に似合いそうだなって思ってるのあって、好みも合わせて聞きたいから今度見に行かない？
Mika-nee, congrats. There's this cosmetic which I think would suit Mika-nee and I'd like to hear your preferences, so why don't we go look at it together next time?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		ミカ姉のバーテンダーとしての技術は本当に繊細で力強いんよなあ、憧れるわー。誕生日は樋宮スペシャルつくりましょか
Mika-nee's skill as a bartender is really delicate and powerful, it's admirable— Should I make Hinomiya Special for his birthday?

Yuragi Kanno 		ミカ。カクテルに使うあの甘いさくらんぼ、余ったらまたちょうだい
Mika, give me those sweet cherries used for cocktails when there's leftovers.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		御門さん、たまに会うとすごい勢いで絡まれるんだよな、どうしてだ‥‥‥？
Mikado-san gets all over me energetically when we meet sometimes, why is it ......?

Haruhi Ichikawa 		ミカさんのつくる酒、美味いよね。あのためだったら、たまには家からでてもいい
The alcohol Mika-san makes is good. I don't mind going out of the house for once to get that.

Sei Okiya 		ミカさんの誕生日ですね。大河と春日のことをいたく気にかけてくれているようで。ふふ、気が合いそうです
It's Mika-san's birthday. Seems like he really cares about Taiga and Haruhi. Fufu, we might get along well.

Shizuka Fushimi 		１杯やるか。今日くらいカウンターの中じゃなくて、外に出て座って飲むのもいいだろ
I should give you a glass. It should be fine to at least be the one who sits outside at the counter to drink rather than the one inside for today.

Takeru Mikado 		今日は１人でバイクを乗り回すつもりだったけど、お嬢も乗る？　そしたら素敵なプレゼントになるわ
I plan to ride my bike around alone today, but is ojou going to join me? If yes, then it'll be a wonderful present for me.

Soyogu Shinkai 		あの人の堂々とした佇まいってのは人間としての芯の強さを感じるよな。かっけえ
That person's imposing presence makes me feel his inner strength as a human being. Cool.

Shinobu Aizawa 		ミカ姉の誕生日‥‥‥なのに今日全然つかまんないんですよね。忙しい人だからなー
Mika-nee's birthday......But I couldn't catch him at all this whole

day. He's a busy person after all.


Kiho Arima 		ミカのバーテンダーとしての技術は、バイト時代からの積み重ねだからね。今日はミカが美味しいお酒を飲めているといいな
Mika's skills as a bartender are an accumulation through part-time jobs. I hope Mika can drink nice alcohol today.

Kyoya Shido 		ミカさんには化粧品をと思いましたが、玄人に得意分野を贈るのは難易度が高いですからね。一緒に買いに行きませんか？
I was thinking of giving cosmetics to Mika-san, but giving an expert something he specializes in makes it difficult. Won't you go together with me to buy it?

Riku Tateshina 		ミカの誕生日‥‥‥だけど、俺は痛い目みてるからねー。お祝いは慎重に行かないと
Mika's birthday...... But I'm suffering. I have to celebrate it carefully.

Tomose Onda 		何かと話しかけられるが、ミカが俺のどこに面白さを見出しているのか謎だ
Mika talks to me, but what he finds interesting in me is a mystery.

Yu Nina 		ミカの誕生日だな。シフトが入っているなら、申し送りついでに何か作るか
It's Mika's birthday. If he's on shift, then I should make something after the delivery.

Kamiya 		スパルタ教育大歓迎だから、ゆくゆくはバーカンの仕事も教えてほしいなーって思ってて。あそこにいるミカさん、かっこいいから
I welcome Spartan teachings, so I'd like to learn about a bar counter job eventually. Mika-san who stands there is cool after all.

Urara Manami 		暗闇でもあれだけ派手だとわかりやすくていいな
Being easy to spot in the dark since he's that flashy is nice.
