Interview Report for Deputy Owner (オーナー代行の取材代行, ōnā daikō shuzai daikō?) is a campaign celebrating the 1.5th Anniversary. [1]

Deputy owners/Players can ask three questions for any characters in the list, and the questions are collected from 2025 Nov 6th to 2025 Dec 17th.

Aporia's staff and characters' answers are released on 2025 May 25th.
