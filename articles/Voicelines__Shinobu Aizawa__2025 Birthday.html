Characters Shinobu Aizawa's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		『誕生日おめでとう』の一言が欲しいならそう言えばすむものを、クイズ形式で迫ってきた。似なくていいところが芦佳に似てきたな
He came to me and give me some kind of quiz when he simply wanted me to say "happy birthday" to him, He imitated Roka on something he shouldn't have imitated.

Yuzuru Kise 		おしゃれな篠信へのプレゼントって確かに迷いますけど、きっとどんな物でも笑顔で受け取ってくれるだろうなって安心もしています。そう思わせてくれるのも篠信の魅力ですよね
Giving a gift to someone fashionable like Shinobu certainly makes me feel at loss, but knowing that he'll receive anything with a smile is reassuring. I'm sure being able to make someone feel so is part of Shinobu's charm.

Roka Suoh 		篠、いつも留守番をありがとう！　篠のお陰で僕の家の秩序が保たれているよ‥‥‥。ということで、お土産をたくさん買ってきたからいっぱい飾ろう☆
Shino, thank you for always taking care of my house! Thanks to Shino, the order in my house is maintained...... With that said, I've bought lots of souvenirs for you, so let's decorate together☆

Kou Ayato 		篠信誕生日おめでとう。多分強行部で飲むだろうと思って俺からはお酒の差し入れ。よかったらみんなで飲んで
Shinobu, happy birthday. I believe you'll be drinking with the Watchdog Dept., so here's a drink as a gift for me. Feel free to share it with everyone.

Mao Ukyo 		篠信に贈るなら小物がいいかな。メインのアイテムを邪魔せず引き立たせるようなやつ。あの取り入れ方は僕も参考にさせてもらってる
A small article should be a fine gift for Shinobu. Something that can complement his main item without disrupting it. I've also been using that approach as a reference.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		篠くんハッピーバースデー！　いつも遊んでくれてありがとな～。今度新しく見つけたバー、一緒にいかへん？　かみやんも誘って夜いこ～
Shino-kun, happy birthday! Thanks for always hanging out with me~ Why don't we check out the new bar together next time? Let's invite Kamiyan and go at night~

Yuragi Kanno 		今度、明星と篠信で猫をテーマにしたドラマを見るんだって。俺も呼ばれたから、見に行く予定
I was told that Akehoshi and Shinobu will be going to watch a drama about cats next time. They invited me, so I'm planning to go.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		人気者そうだし、今日はいろんな人にお祝いされるんだろうな。俺も、菓子でも用意して次に新開くんが来たら届けてもらうか
He seems to be popular, so I bet he's getting celebrated by many kinds of people today. I should prepare some sweets and have Shinkai-kun pass it to him the next time he comes here.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		芦佳さんと一緒に住んでるって聞いた時は、マジで尊敬したよね。耳栓でもプレゼントしようかな
Hearing that he lives with Roka-san makes me respect him for real. I probably should give him some earplugs as a gift.

Sei Okiya 		いつも場を明るくしてくれる彼の誕生日をお祝いしたい、という思いがAporia中に溢れているようです。かくいう私もちょっとした焼き菓子をご用意いたしました。よかったらどうぞ
The feeling of wanting to celebrate his birthday as he always brighten up the place seems to be flowing through Aporia, I indeed have prepared some baked snacks. Please have some.

Shizuka Fushimi 		一緒に服を買いには行かない。誕生日だからって何でも許されるわけじゃないから。まあでも、飯くらいは行くか
I won't go shopping for clothes with you. A birthday doesn't mean you can do whatever you want. But, well, going for a meal is fine.

Takeru Mikado 		危なげがどんどん無くなっていくじゃない？　若手の成長を近くで見てると、私も負けてられないって思えるの。だから、いつもありがとね
The danger is gradually disappearing, no? Closely watching over the young growing makes me feel that I shouldn't lose. That's why, thank you for always.

Soyogu Shinkai 		馬もそれなりにあう、仕事もやりやすい、そういうタメの奴が同じ部でよかったわ。ま、これからもよろしく
We hit it off well, and it's easy to work together, I'm glad I have that kind of guy in my department. Well, I'll be in your care from now on too.

Shinobu Aizawa 		実感はやっぱないけど、みんながお祝いしてくれるのは嬉しいなって思います！　その気持ちをもらえるだけで、すっごい特別だなって
It doesn't exactly feel real, but I'm very glad that everyone's celebrating me! Receiving those feelings already feels very special.

Kiho Arima 		篠信のお眼鏡に適うか、わからないんだけど、似合いそうなアクセサリーを見つけてね。好みにあったら使って
I don't know whether Shinobu will accept it well, but I found an accessory that'll fit you. Use it if it's to your preference.

Kyoya Shido 		篠信くんの誕生日プレゼントはやっぱり服がいいかなと思って。いつも喜んで着てくれるから、贈る側としても嬉しいんだよね
I'm thinking that clothes should be the perfect birthday gift for Shinobu-kun. He always wears it happily, making the ones who give it to him also happy.

Riku Tateshina 		頑張ってる篠信には何がいいかなって考えたんだけど。きっと俺がどうこうするより、強行部でわいわいする時間が増える方が良いよな
I was thinking about what I should give to the hardworking Shinobu. Though I'm sure it's better to let him have more time having fun with the Watchdog Dept. rather than having me try to do something for him.

Tomose Onda 		相沢は運動神経がよく、伸び代もあるようだな。神家が良く評価している
Aizawa has good motor skills and growth potential. Kamiya rates it well.

Yu Nina 		ファッションについて聞かれることがある。特別こだわりがあるわけではないが、相沢はそうじゃないようだ
He ever asked me about fashion. It's not like I'm being particular about it, but Aizawa doesn't seem to be the same as me.

Kamiya 		篠信くんのお誕生日、今年は何する？　買い物もいいし、何かの食べ放題もいいよね。あっ遊園地とかもあり？　‥‥‥あはは、全部行っちゃおっか！
What should we do for your birthday this year, Shinobu-kun? Shopping sounds nice, going to a buffet is also nice. Ah, an amusement park is also a good idea? ......Ahaha, we might as well do everything!

Urara Manami 		なんでオレがてめえのこと祝うんだよ。いつもの奴らと馴れ合ってろや
Why do I have to celebrate you? Just go out with those you usually get along with.
