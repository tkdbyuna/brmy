Valentine 2025

  • Release Period: February 10, 2025 00:00 JST - February 17, 2025 23:59 JST

Translated by Misora

Characters Valentine 2025

Ai Kosaka 		チョコレートか、ありがとう。疑うも何も、お前がこれに変なものを入れる考えがないことくらい、わかってる
Chocolates, huh...... Thanks. There's no need to doubt this since I at least know that you don't have any intention of putting something strange in it.

Yuzuru Kise 		このチョコですか？　いつものお礼にってみんながくれて。ふふ、どれから食べようか迷っちゃうな
These chocolates? Everyone gave me as a daily gratitude. I'm at loss on which one I should eat first.

Roka Suoh 		もし誰かにチョコを強請られて困ったら、僕を頼るといい。それ以上の熱量で強請ることで、すべてをかき消してみせるよ！
You should rely on me when someone tries to demand chocolates from you. I'll demand them back with much more intensity and make sure they don't do that anymore!

Kou Ayato 		バレンタインねぇー‥‥‥真央との作戦会議は今年もばっちりよ。でもお前がくれるっていうなら受け取りたいから、その時はチョコ以外でよろしく
Valentine, huh...... The strategy meeting with Mao is also perfect for this year. But if you're saying you're giving me then I want to accept it, so make sure that you give me something other than chocolates.

Mao Ukyo 		チョコありがとう。君ってモノを選ぶのうまいよね、相手のことよく見てるからかな？　‥‥‥これを選ぶ時、どんなこと考えてたの？
Thanks for the choco. You're good at choosing things. Is it because you watch others closely? ...... What were you thinking when you were choosing this?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		Aporiaが忙しくなるやろな。デザートメニューがたくさん出る日やし、頑張るかあ
I bet Aporia will become busy. It's the day where lots of desserts will be served, so I guess I should do my best

Yuragi Kanno 		このチョコ俺に？　ありがと、もらっとく。これって普段くれるやつ？　それともバレンタインだから？
This choco for me? Thanks, I'll take it. Are you casually giving me like usual? Or is it for Valentine's?

Taiga Tsukimoto 		よかったらこれ、作ったので持っていってください。あ、これだと逆チョコになりますね‥‥‥
If you like it, please take what I made. Ah, this means it's a reverse choco.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		突然のおかき何？　‥‥‥今日？　ああ‥‥‥ふーん。甘くないの助かる、もらっとく
What's with the sudden rice crackers? ...... Today? Ahh...... hmm. Thanks for giving something not sweet. I'll take it.

Sei Okiya 		愛の言葉はチョコレートに溶かしてください、そうしたら余すことなく飲み込みますよ
Please melt your words of love into chocolates. I will then swallow it all without leaving any of it behind.

Shizuka Fushimi 		義理とか本命とか、浮かれる年齢でもないだろ。浮かれた俺が見たいなら、君が頑張るしかないんじゃない？
I'm not at the age where I get excited by obligatory or honmei, am I? If you want to see me get excited then you should do your best, am I wrong?

Takeru Mikado 		私からはこの１杯をどうぞ。いい香りでしょ？　チョコレートリキュールでバレンタイン気分を味わって♡
Enjoy this one glass from me. Doesn't it have a nice smell? Taste the Valentine vibes from the chocolate liqueur ♡

Soyogu Shinkai 		このチョコか？　姉御から。あの人ほんとマメだから、多分お前の分も用意してるぞ
This choco? It's from Anego. That person is really diligent, so he should've prepared some for you too.

Shinobu Aizawa 		俺に？　うわあ～、ありがとうございます！　自慢してまわろって思ったけど、多分みんなも貰ってますよね、てへー
For me? Whoa, thank you! I wanted to go around to boast about it, but I guess everyone is also getting it, tehe.

Kiho Arima 		今日、安全に過ごせそう？　いや、君から何かもらえないかってうろうろしてるのが何人かいるみたいだから
Do you feel like you can spend today safely? No, there were some people who were wandering aimlessly as they were hoping to get something from you.

Kyoya Shido 		百貨店が賑わっていたんだけどバレンタインだからか。君も興味があるのかな？　あるとなれば、仔細を聞きたいところだね
So the department stores were crowded because it's Valentine. Are you interested in it too? If you are, I'd like to hear your reasons.

Riku Tateshina 		わざわざ甘くないもの用意してくれたの？　大丈夫？　好きになるけど
Did you go out of your way to prepare something not sweet? Are you okay? I'm going to start liking you though.

Tomose Onda 		この日のチョコレートには、もの以上に価値が込められていると聞いた
I heard chocolates have values beyond a mere thing today.

Yu Nina 		チョコレート、ダークとミルクどちらがいい。客へのサービスの余りだから遠慮せず選んで良い
What chocolates do you like, dark or milk? They're leftovers from the customer bonus, so feel free to choose.

Kamiya 		バレンタイン限定チョコがけドーナツシリーズ‥‥‥去年即完売してたから今年こそ！
The limited Valentine choco-dipped donut series was immediately sold out last year, so I should get it this year!

Urara Manami 		甘いの無理。いらねえ‥‥‥は？　別に飴ならいいとかねえし
Sweet things are impossible for me. I don't need it....... Hah? It's not like candies are fine either.

Characters Valentine's Day 2025

Happy Valentine!

Happy Valentine. ......Leaving aside whether it's actually happy though.

Happy Valentine. I'm more of the savory type, but I also like sweet things a normal amount.

Happy Valentine. Do you have one for me?

Yuragi Kanno 		チョコ
Choco.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		世間はバレンタインみたいですね。貴方は誰かに‥‥‥いや、なんでもないです！　すみません！
Seems like the society is celebrating Valentine. Do you plan to give...... No, it's nothing! I'm sorry!

Happy Valentine. It's not actually a happy thing, though.

Sei Okiya 		ハッピーバレンタイン。貴方からのチョコを期待してもいいですか？
Happy Valentine. May I expect some choco from you?

Shizuka Fushimi 		ハッピーバレンタイン。‥‥‥特にハッピーでもなんでもないけど
Happy Valentine. ...... It's not especially happy or anything though.

I will first properly accept things given to me.

Shinobu Aizawa 		ハッピーラッキーバレンタイン！
Happy Lucky Valentine!

Kiho Arima 		バレンタインか‥‥‥いや、昔を少し思い出していただけだよ
Valentine, huh...... No, I just remembered something from the past.

Kyoya Shido 		好意を無碍にするわけにはいかないからね。大切に貰うよ
I can't turn a blind eye to the favor, so I'll treasure it.

Riku Tateshina 		ハッピーバレンタイン。年甲斐もなく今年はソワソワしちゃってますよ
Happy Valentine. I feel nervous this year despite my age.

Tomose Onda 		バレンタインデー‥‥‥カレンダーの記載を読み上げただけだ
Valentine's Day...... I was just reading the calendar's entry.

Yu Nina 		バレンタイン？　メニューに変更があるなら早めに頼む
Valentine? Do tell me as soon as possible if there are any changes in the menu.

Kamiya 		もしかして俺にバレンタインくれる？
Are you perhaps giving me something for Valentine's?

Urara Manami 		店ん中、甘いにおいが残ってそうだな
Seems like the store will be covered in a sweet smell.
