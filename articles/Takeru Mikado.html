◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Watchdog Dept.[2] Shizuka Fushimi 💻 & Takeru Mikado 🌹 & Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

While they have individual friendships, they primarily build relationships as a group of four. When someone suggests going out for drinks with two others, it usually becomes a group invitation for the other two as well. They have immense trust in Fushimi as our leader who makes sound judgments and gives clear direction, yet they maintain a healthy dynamic where everyone feels comfortable voicing their opinions.

  • New and Former Department Head[3]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

The former and current heads of the Watchdog Dept.. They share the responsibility of protecting the other members. Though their preferences and lifestyles are completely different, so they don't spend their days off together, they have many opportunities to talk and share various information. They both find each other dependable.

  • ULTRA BIG[4]: Takeru Mikado 🌹 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

Overwhelmingly physically dominant. Both are large-scale and powerful in mind and body. They communicate things directly with each other, building an incredibly comfortable relationship. As heavy drinkers, their one-on-one drinks before holidays that often last until noon.

Watchdog Dept. used to only consist of Mika and Soyogu before expanding with the addition of Shinobu and Urara. Mika took the leader role after the expansion and before Shizuka was scouted. The two often go drinking together and tell each other stories.

  • Cosmetic Dept.[5] Mao Ukyo 🧩 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

The two share a keen interest in fashion and beauty, especially cosmetics. Due to the last train schedule, Ukyo, who can't make it home if he works until closing, usually stays at Mikado's place. Ayato sometimes joins them there.

They often hang out shopping as both of them enjoy cosmetics.

  • Bar Counter Trio[6]: Takeru Mikado🌹 Akehoshi Hinomiya⭐️ Shinobu Aizawa🐍

They are in charge of making drinks and serving customers sitting at the bar counter, both day and night. The three of them often go out to drink together under the pretext of researching cocktails.

  • Friendship Between Adults[7]: Riku Tateshina 🥃 and Takeru Mikado 🌹

On top of the two of them being starting members, they are alike in that they are both indebted to Suoh. Because they are different types of people, they can view each other clearly and support each other appropriately. Despite the age difference, it is an equal friendship between adults.
