Description]

Art lecturer full of elegance and confidence
Art teacher from a prominent historical family. He is a man of refined taste in his belongings, words, and actions, and is confident in everything he does, which sometimes makes him naturally feel superior. He has no malicious intent at all, but his words and actions often leave Arima and Tateshina stunned.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Negotiation Dept.[2]: Kiho Arima 🔮 & Kyoya Shido 🎨 & Riku Tateshina 🥃

Though they appear to be a collection of individualists, they actually get along quite well. They maintain a comfortable distance, meeting regularly for drinks as a department activity, allowing them to interact without awkwardness. They enjoy talking and drinking together, occasionally going on trips as well.

  • Fabulous[3]: Kiho Arima 🔮 and Kyoya Shido 🎨

Through an acquaintance, Shido first met Arima as a client and fortune teller. Even now, after joining Aporia at Arima’s invitation, Shido continues to have his fortune read by him regularly. He has absolute faith in Arima’s fortunes and looks up to him as his senior in the same department.

  • TOP2[4]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ and Kyoya Shido 🎨

They are part of Aporia’s “Reboot Group”, the generation with the most members, and became friends through a mutual interest in fashion. Though their preferences differ slightly, their perceptions are similar, and they casually invite each other to go shopping on their days off or to drink after work. When those two go drinking, it’s almost certain that they’ll be approached by someone.

Japanese 黒く透き通るような美しいカクテル。鼻を抜けるコーヒーの香ばしさがテキーラの強い味わいを和らげている。度数は高いが甘口で飲みやすいお酒。Aporiaではコーヒーリキュールにもこだわっていて、いくつか種類が用意されている
English A beautiful cocktail that is dark and translucent. The aromatic richness of the coffee softens the strong flavor of the tequila. It has a high alcohol content but is sweet and easy to drink. Aporia is particular about its coffee liquor, offering several varieties.
Mimosa* / ミモザ
Japanese Aporiaに飲みに行くと季節に合わせて提供しているメューもあったりしてね。なかなか目移りしてしまうけど、こういう定番メニューの土台がしっかりしているからこそどんなカクテルでも美味しく仕上がるんだと思うよ。君はどんなものがお好みかな？
English When you go to Aporia, there are also seasonal menu items available. It can be quite distracting, but I think the reason any cocktail tastes great is because they have a solid foundation with their classic menu. What kind of drinks do you prefer?
Resurrecting Magic Glass* / 蘇りのマジックグラス
Japanese 恩田くんは頻繁にカフェのシフトに入っているメンバーではないのに、いつもキッチンを円滑に回してると聞くよ。今回のイベント中も常にてきばきと動いていたしね。もし俺なら‥‥‥か。もちろん期待に応えられるとは思うけど、ああいうのは適材適所だ。恩田くんや新名くんにまずは相談してみようかな？
English Even though Onda-kun is not a member who frequently takes shifts at the café, I've heard he always keeps the kitchen running smoothly. He was also moving briskly during this event. If it were me... well, I believe I could meet expectations, but situations like that require the right person for the job. Maybe I should consult Onda-kun and Nina-kun first?
Milkshake / ミルクセーキ
Japanese 喫茶店の定番、懐かしの甘いドリンク。 チョコレートやイチゴでの味付け、 アーモンドミルクや豆乳への変更など 様々なアレンジが存在する
English A café classic and nostalgic sweet drink. There are various arrangements, such as flavoring with chocolate or strawberry, and substituting with almond milk or soy milk.
My Melody's Mellow Pancake* / マイメロディのめろぱちけ～き
Japanese 恋くんとの食事に メロデイも来てもらったんだけど、 思った以上に打ち解けててよかったよ。 まあ、元より何も心配はしてなかったんだけど。 彼女って親しみやすいし、 自然な気遣いもできる子だから
English I invited Melody to dine with Kou-kun, and she got along surprisingly well. Well, I wasn't worried at all. She's easy to approach and naturally considerate.
