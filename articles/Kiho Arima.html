Description]

A self-aware magical fortune teller
A person with a graceful demeanor and fleeting aura. He also has the strength to take pride in it and live with it to the fullest. Enjoys doing fortune-telling as a side job, and his serious tarot skill is frighteningly accurate. As if "he can see everything".[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Negotiation Dept.[2]: Kiho Arima 🔮 & Kyoya Shido 🎨 & Riku Tateshina 🥃

Though they appear to be a collection of individualists, they actually get along quite well. They maintain a comfortable distance, meeting regularly for drinks as a department activity, allowing them to interact without awkwardness. They enjoy talking and drinking together, occasionally going on trips as well.

  • Fabulous[3]: Kiho Arima 🔮 and Kyoya Shido 🎨

Through an acquaintance, Shido first met Arima as a client and fortune teller. Even now, after joining Aporia at Arima’s invitation, Shido continues to have his fortune read by him regularly. He has absolute faith in Arima’s fortunes and looks up to him as his senior in the same department.

  • Founding Pair[4]: Roka Suoh 🦎 and Kiho Arima 🔮

The pair who founded Aporia. They met at a previous job in a host club [5] and have known each other for over 10 years. Although the free and spontaneous Suoh’s absurdness constantly amazes Arima, never has Arima failed to meet expectations. Arima may seem to be the older one, but Suoh is actually four years older.

  • Suspicious Pair[6]: Sei Okiya 📘 and Kiho Arima 🔮

Among the veterans, Okiya and Arima get along particularly well. Even if they’re just making small talk, people will say that it “looks like something fishy is going on”. They met through Okiya’s relative, who was a client of Arima’s, and became close after that.
