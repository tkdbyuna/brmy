Bremile Mission (ブレマイルミッション, Buremairu misshon?) resets progress monthly at 4:00 AM JST on the first day of the month.

Bremile is a point that accumulates according to the stamina consumed in Puzzles or Spins.

※ You earn 1 Bremile for every 1 stamina consumed.

Additional Premium Missions that grant extra rewards will be unlocked. Furthermore, if you purchase a Bremile Pass in the shop, you can receive luxurious rewards.

You can claim rewards in the [Bremile] section of Mission based on the amount of Bremile you’ve accumulated.

Total crystals are earned every month: 500 from clearing 1000 miles and an additional 500 unlocked by Bremile Pass.

Bremile Pass]

Main article: Bremile Pass

With 1000¥ per month, Bremile Pass will unlock the additional 500 crystals rewards in Bremile Mission, as well as Outfit and Mini Outfit of two different characters.

Bremile Pass also:

  • Daily skip ticket increase from 1 to 3
  • Slightly boost the auto-play feature in puzzle game. (You will see this icon near cp active icon: )

※ Bremile Pass insights:

  • You can purchase anytime within the month, however, the effect of the auto-play boost is only available until the end of the month, so it's preferable to buy it as soon as possible.
  • If you clear 1000 miles before purchase the pass, 500 crystals are sent to you right away.
  • If the skip tickets have been used yet while the bremile pass is active, the skip ticket are still three until you use them. The number will be reduced and refilled as one per day, provided that the bremile pass is inactive.
