Description]

A safe and secure skillful assistant
A sincere good person. Kosaka's skillful assistant. Doesn't complain about whatever work is pushed onto him. Among the store full of problematic people, he is a "conscience" so no one hates Kise. Despite his delicate appearance, he is actually bold and has a lot of guts. Is weak against hunger.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Main Office[2]: Ai Kosaka 👑 & Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Roka Suoh 🦎

While there is a relationship of trust, they are not "close friends," so private interactions are limited, and there are no department-wide drinking parties. Suoh also never suggests "let's get together just the department," so relationships between individuals are deeper than those within the department itself.

  • Unspoken Understanding[3]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Yuzuru Kise 🍴

The leader who commands the entire Aporia operation and the right-hand man who supports him. Kise respects Kosaka, and while Kosaka never openly favors or coddles Kise, he never fails to show consideration and gratitude. They understand each other deeply, communicating effortlessly without needing many words.

  • Pastel Prince[4]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 and Kou Ayato 🎞️

They're classmates and close friends who get along well. Both love ramen and have tried most shops around Shibuya. At their first dress-up event themed around princes, they wore soft-colored outfits as "Spring Prince" and "Autumn Prince," earning them these nicknames from some longtime regulars.

  • Excursion Buddies[5]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Mao Ukyo 🧩 & Yuragi Kanno 🍬

Kise gets along equally well with everyone except Kosaka, but he is actually remarkably close to Ukyo and Kanno. On their days off, they go to buffets, go shopping, or spend time at the park. A harmonious trio that takes turns doing what each of them wants to do.

  • Friends on the Veranda[6]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 and Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙

Two earnest people who respect each other for their personalities and reliability in their work. They collaborate frequently as members of the Main Office and Administrative Dept. respectively, and they chat often thanks to their mutual interest in cooking. Ichikawa has described their warm dynamic as "elders sitting on the veranda”.

  • Kitchen Crew[7]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Tomose Onda 🌏

Aporia's kitchen staff. They converse little but maintain a good working relationship. Typically, either Kise or Nina works the shift, with Onda having fewer shifts.

  • Staff Lunchbox[8]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Urara Manami 🧹

The people who prepare the lunchboxes for the late-night shift staff and the person who eats them. Manami notes his meal preferences in a notebook, and either Kise or Nina will cook based on his request. If any ingredient that Manami dislikes is included, even in small amounts, he will complain with all his might, saying “Don’t add that kinda shit”. Even so, he never leaves a crumb.

Peach & Melon / ピーチ＆メロン
Japanese ピーチとメロンリキュールを使ったフルーティかつ爽やかな後味の一品。ピーチという名前がついているものの、色味は完全にメロンの緑色
English A drink made out of peach and melon liquor with a refreshing fruity aftertaste. Even though it has "peach" in its name, its color is completely melon green.
Black Manhattan* / ブラックマンハッタン
Japanese 恋くんと話していたんですけど‥‥‥ブラックマンハッタンって、どこか逢さんっぽくありませんか？色味や『カクテルの女王』という異名の高貴さとかが特に。そう思うと、一層作る手に気合が入ります
English I was talking with Kou-kun, and... doesn't Black Manhattan feel a bit like Ai-san? Especially the color and that noble aura from its nickname, "Queen of Cocktails." Thinking that makes me even more determined to perfect it.
Prom Night* / プロムナイト
Japanese デザートのメニューを揺と一緒に決めた時、俺じゃ思いつかないアイデアをいっぱいもらっちゃった。フルーツの種類や置き方とか、やっぱり好きな人だから見える景色があるんだろうね。それに、試食も誰かと一緒だと、さらに美味しく感じるし‥‥‥またお願いしようかな
English I got so many ideas that I couldn't possibly come up with when I was deciding on the dessert menus with Yuragi. Such as the variety of fruits and the placement. I knew there were things that only people who like them can see. Moreover, being together with someone during taste-testing makes the food more delicious…… I should invite him again next time.
Old Fashioned / オールドファッションド
Japanese アメリカで作られたという、古き良き時代を象徴するカクテル。角砂糖とフルーツがビターズを引き立てる、ちょっぴり大人な味わいが特徴。Aporiaでも年長組に好評らしい
English A cocktail said to have originated in the United States, symbolizing the good old days. It features a slightly sophisticated flavor, with sugar cubes and fruit bringing out the bitterness. It seems to be a hit with the older crowd at Aporia as well.
Casino* / カジノ
Japanese 先ほど試飲をさせてもらったんですが、後味のほろ苦さがちょうどいい、クセになる味で気づいたらなくなっちゃってました。あ、ただ度数が４０度近くあるらしいので、おすすめする際には、一言添えてあげられるとよいかもしれません
English I just had a tasting earlier, and the slightly bitter aftertaste is just right, it’s so addictive that I finished the whole bottle before I knew it. Ah, but since the alcohol content is apparently close to 40%, so if you’re recommending it to someone, you might want to mention that.
Good Luck* / グッドラック
Japanese この間、帰宅して気づいたら財布が見当たらなくて‥‥‥。うつかりしてたなと思ったら、全く降りたことのない駅の交番に届けられてたんです。幸運でしたけど、不思議な体験でした
English The other day, I got home and realized my wallet was missing... I thought I had just been careless, but it turned out someone had turned it into a police box at a station I’ve never even gotten off at. I was lucky, but it was a strange experience.
