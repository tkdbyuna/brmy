BureGimi (ブレぎみマイケース, Bure‑gimi Mai Kēsu?, lit. Bre-off My Case) is a 4-panel manga series about the daily life of BREAK MY CASE. It was posted on the official BREAK MY CASE Twitter with the hashtag #ぎみマイ on every Tuesday of the 2nd and 4th week of the month.

Misora, Aca, and several Twitter users provide English translations for comics.
