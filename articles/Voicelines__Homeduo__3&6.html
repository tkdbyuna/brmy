Voicelines/Homeduo/3&6


Roka Suoh
あっけーあっけー、ほな邪魔するでー！
Akke, Akke, well then, I’ll be intruding![Note 1]

Akehoshi Hinomiya
邪魔するんやったら帰って～‥‥‥って、俺はそこまでコテコテやないよ[Note 2]
If you’re gonna intrude, then go home~[Note 3]...... nah, I’m not that Kansai cliché. [Note 2]

Translation Note

  1. ↑ 邪魔するで (jamasurude, lit. “I’ll intrude”): In Kansai culture, people often say this when entering someone’s home, place or space.
  2. ↑ 2.0 2.1 コテコテ (kotekote) in Kansai slang means “thick, heavy, exaggerated, stereotypical.” So he’s saying: “I’m not that stereotypically Kansai” or “I’m not that hardcore Kansai style.”
  3. ↑ 邪魔するんやったら帰って～ (jama surun yattara kaette~, lit. “If you’re going to intrude, then leave/go home.”): This is a playful twist on the Kansai phrase mentioned before.
