Someone must find the hidden “password” within the game—Aporia takes on the task of exploration.
A world that doesn’t overlap with reality, yet is connected to it. The answer lies with “his other self.”

Play Activity]

Play normal puzzle song or event-exclusive stages to earn Pt "Kusudama" (くす玉, kusudama?, lit. traditional Japanese paper balloon or festive paper orb)). Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts, but card level, coins, and drops are still 1 times.
