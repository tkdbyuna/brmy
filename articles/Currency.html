Main currency]

Stamina]

Further information: Home Guide § Stamina

Stamina is used to play puzzles and Snap'n Spin.

Once it drops below 20, stamina recovers by 1 every 10 minutes.

Coin]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Coin, and Home Guide § Coin

Coin (コイン, koin) can be earned in the puzzle game, exchanged from items in Item pane. The coins are used to:

  • Upgrade cards' status level
  • Buy items in Exchange Shop

You can check your number on Coin at the status bar at the top of most of the in-game screens.

Crystal]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Crystal, and Home Guide § Crystal

Crystal (クリスタル, kurisutaru) can be earned by clearing game missions or bought in money in Shop. Crystal is used for:

  • Buy gacha ticket to pull gacha.

Each normal pull is 50 crystals; however, the players can exchange gacha tickets for 40 crystals in Exchange Shop, a maximum of 5 times per month. The redeemable tickets will be reset at 4:00 AM JST on the first day of the month.

  • Exchange other items in Exchange Shop.

There are 2 types of crystals: free crystals and paid crystals. You can earn free crystals by clearing mission, daily login bonus, etc, while paid crystals can only be bought through Shop.

You can check your number on Crystal at the status bar at the top of most of the in-game screens.

Other currency]

Support Point]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Support Point

Support Point or in short Support pt (サポートpt, sabõto pt) Each time players use the support card from friends and successfully clear the puzzles, the support pt will be rewarded. Next to the support card in the choosing pane, there will show the amount of support points that can be earned (10pt or 15pt).

You can only see your Support pt at the top of Support pt Exchange Shop.

Rank Reward]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Rank Reward

Rank Reward (ランクリワード, rankuriwãdo) is available after reaching the maximum User Rank. Rank reward is earned after clearing a puzzle, and is used to exchange in Exchange Shop.

Rank Reward Drop Rate

1 1

You can only see your Rank Reward at the top of Rank Reward Exchange Shop.

Pink Feather]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Pink Feather

Pink Feather (ピンクの羽根, pinku no hane) is automatically exchanged from expired gacha pts or exchanged from duplicate cards in Waiting Deck. You can see your own Pink Feather in the CHANGE > Pink Feather section.

You can see your Pink Feather at the top of Pink Feather Exchange Shop or in Waiting Deck.

Event Point]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Event Point

Event Point, or in short Event pt (イベントpt, ebento pt), is limitedly available during the event's period. After the event ends, event pts cannot be earned anymore, but there's still some time left to be exchanged at Exchange Shop (usually until 3:59 AM JST on 3 days later since the event end date) After the Exchange Shop's deadline, it will be deleted and cannot be exchanged.

Different events will have unique Event pt.

You can see your Event pt at the top of Event Pt Exchange Shop or in Event top screen.

Gacha Point]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Gacha Point

Gacha Point or in short Gacha pt (ガチャpt, gacha pt)

When you use a gacha ticket in a specific gacha banner, you will receive gacha pt.

1 1

If gacha pt isn't used, they will be automatically converted to Pink Feather.

Gacha pt to Pink Feather Conversion
Gacha pt Pink Feather
1 1

There are so far 10 types of gacha points:

  • : turquoise
  • : pink
  • : aqua
  • : orange
  • : green
  • : yellow
  • : pale purple
  • : purple
  • : blue
  • :grey

The color of the gacha pt isn't fixed to any specific type of event. (like the Prequel series needs to be assigned to the turquoise one).
However, if many gacha or gacha events are happening at the same time, they will surely be different colors.

You can check your number on Gacha pt at the top of Gacha or Gacha pt Exchange Shop screens.

Select Ticket]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Select Ticket

Select Ticket (セレクトチケット, serekuto chiketto) can be used to select the cards in the pool that Select Ticket provide. There are 3 rarities (SSR, SR, and R) for Select Ticket, and 4 versions so far. The later version will provide more cards to select.

You can see your Select Ticket at the top of Select Ticket Exchange Shop or in Items/List.

Challenge Coin]

Further information: Exchange Shop § Challenge Coin

Challenge coin (チャレンジコイン, charenji koin ) can be earned by clearing a limited-puzzle in the Marvellous Challenge.

You can see your Challenge Coin at the top of Challenge Coin Exchange Shop or in Items/List.
