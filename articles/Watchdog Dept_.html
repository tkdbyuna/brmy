Watchdog Department (強行部, kyoukou-bu), often shortened to Watchdog Dept., is the department in charge of force. They are rough agents; however, they only undertake response, capture, and recapture. Requests such as physical retaliation (revenge) are not allowed. In addition, they can accompany other departments’ agency work and escort the staff.
When there is no business, they usually tend to the cafe’s bar.
