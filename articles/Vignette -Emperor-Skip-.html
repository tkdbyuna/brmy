Play Activity]

Using normal stamina, play puzzles to earn Event Pt Bookmark to unlock rewards. Event Pts can be earned in all staff's playlists (See more in Event Bonus) throughout the event.

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina players use will multiply the drop rate of Pts.
