Play Activities]

During the event period, stages featuring 6 REMIX tracks will be added daily to the event-exclusive playlist.

Play normal playlists and event-exclusive playlists to meet the special mission and Total Play number Mission's target, then you can unlock luxurious rewards.

Clear event stages in the first time to get Crystals ×25.

Event Mission]

※ Total earn: 340 Crystals (190 from Mission, 150 from clearing Event-exclusive puzzle) and 8 Gacha tickets

Special Mission]

  1. Clear 1000 Sun (Red) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  2. Clear 5000 Sun (Red) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  3. Clear 1000 Sun (Pink) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  4. Clear 5000 Sun (Pink) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  5. Clear 1000 Moon (Aqua) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  6. Clear 5000 Moon (Aqua) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  7. Clear 1000 Moon (Blue) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  8. Clear 5000 Moon (Blue) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  9. Clear 1000 Star (Yellow) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  10. Clear 5000 Star (Yellow) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  11. Clear 1000 Star (Green) pieces (Reward Crystal ×5)
  12. Clear 5000 Star (Green) pieces (Reward Crystal ×10)
  13. Clear 100 Combo Pieces (Reward Skill Key ×3)
  14. Clear 100 Combo Pieces (Reward Revision Key ×3)
  15. Clear puzzles 10 times in the event playlist (Reward Event Pin ×1)
  16. Clear puzzles 30 times in the event playlist (Reward Crystal ×10)
  17. Clear Sun Attribute's stages 10 times (Reward Supply Sun L ×1)
  18. Clear Moon Attribute's stages 10 times (Reward Supply Moon L ×1)
  19. Clear Star Attribute's stages 10 times (Reward Supply Star L ×1)
  20. Complete 19 main tasks (Reward SR Gacha Ticket Ver.3 ×1)

Total Play number Mission]

The total number of puzzle plays by all players will unlock the rewards.

For 1 day, the 1M target is quickly met, so Coly upgrades the milestones and adds more rewards.[1]
