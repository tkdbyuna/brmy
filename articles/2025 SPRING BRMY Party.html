2025 SPRING BRMY Party (2025 SPRING ブレマイパーティ, 2025 SPRING Buremai Pāti?, lit. 2025 SPRING Bremai Party) is a Gacha Event that happens in the Puzzle Training 2025 SPRING Campaign.

Cards illustrated by Utako Yukihiro, the game's character designer, for Mao Ukyo and Shinobu Aizawa are introduced.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.

Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): March 10, 2025 18:00 JST - March 14, 2025 12:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "2025 SPRING BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 draws for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 draws for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.2)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.

※Note: For STEP 3 & 4, you also need to draw 10 gacha to earn the rewards.
