Overview

──And what comes next, I believe, is your destiny.

The sweet scent of chocolate wafts through the air, marking the season of love.
In such a time, Ukyo receives a request to write a love letter.
Acting as a substitute for creating “destiny” for a close friend.
What conclusion does Ukyo arrive at regarding those words...

Play Activity

Play a normal puzzle song to earn Pt Rose and Rose Medal. The medal will be used to enter the limited event puzzle to earn more Pt .

Pts' drop rate in normal puzzle using Stamina

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.
