Play Activity

Play a normal puzzle song to earn Pt Paint and Paint Medal. The medal will be used to enter the limited event puzzle to earn more Pt .

Pts' drop rate in normal puzzle using Stamina

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Medals.

Trivia

The card illustrations are inspired by famous paintings:

  • Shizuka Fushimi: Road with Cypress and Star by Vincent van Gogh
  • Kyoya Shido: The Kiss by Gustav Klimt
  • Takeru Mikado: The Swing by Jean-Honoré Fragonad
  • Haruhi Ichikawa: Woman with a Parasol by Claude Monet
  • Sei Okiya: Impression, Sunrise by Claude Monet
