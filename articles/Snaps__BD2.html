Second Birthday Snaps was introduced first on 2025/05/14. The collection has three cards, each equivalent to a rarity: SSR, SR, and R, of which only the SSR card includes comments from the department's fellow members.

Second birthday snaps are taken only at the Space Casino spots.

Since the title on the snap (or cheki) is the same among the rarities, only SSR cards are showcased in this page. The gallery is arranged by department, instead of release day (characters' birthday).

Requirements: Characters wear the Birthday (2nd) Mini Styles, no matter which character's card is used.
