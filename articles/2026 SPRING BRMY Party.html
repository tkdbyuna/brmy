2026 SPRING BRMY Party

2026 SPRING BRMY Party (2026 SPRING ブレマイパーティ, 2026 SPRING Buremai Pāti?, lit. 2026 SPRING Bremai Party) is a Gacha Event happens alongside with the BMC RMX FES Vol.3 Event.

The cards illustrated by Utako Yukihiro, the game's character designer, that are released before appear in PU.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.

Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): February 24, 2026 18:00 JST - March 02, 2026 15:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "2026 SPRING BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 pulls for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 pulls for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.4)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.
