AR Camera]

You can place staff members into real-world scenes to take photos or play back voice messages.

※To use the AR camera, your device must be compatible, and the app must be granted access to the camera and your photo library.

When using headphones or earbuds, you will hear the voice coming from the direction where the staff member is standing.
Please be mindful of your surroundings and privacy, and make sure to play in a safe location.

※If you get too close to the staff member or tilt the camera too much, the model may temporarily disappear.

How to Use]

  1. Tap the [AR Camera] button in the upper-right corner of the title screen to launch the AR camera.
  2. After launching, start the surface recognition mode. Point the camera at a flat surface and move it slowly. When a surface is recognized, the color of that area will change.

※In dark places or areas with no objects, it may be difficult to recognize a surface.

  1. Tap the area where the color has changed to make a staff member appear there.
  2. After the staff member appears, you can change the staff member, play poses, or play a video.

List of buttons]

Capture button
You can take a photo.

※To save photos, the app must be allowed to access your Camera Roll.

Model Change button
You can change the staff member or their style.
Position Change button
You can change the staff member’s standing position.
Voice button
Plays a voice.
Pose button
Tap to have the staff member strike a pose. Tap again to return to the previous pose.
UI Off button
Hides the UI. Tapping anywhere on the screen will display the UI again.
Help button
Displays the help screen.
mini button
Tapping the [mini button] switches to a mode that displays mini-sized staff members. Tapping the same button again returns to 2D mode.
