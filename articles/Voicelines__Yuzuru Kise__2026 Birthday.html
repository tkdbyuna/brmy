Characters Yuzuru Kise's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		何か食べたいものはあるか。今週どこかで連れて行く。ケーキは別で用意しているから、それは加味せず検討しろ
Do you have anything you want to eat? Let me bring you there this week. I've separately prepared a cake, so consider it without taking the cake into account.

Yuzuru Kise 		皆さん、お忙しいのにわざわざ声をかけてくれて、時間を割いてくれて。申し訳ないですが、嬉しいなって‥‥‥本当にありがとうございます
Everyone went out of their way to call me and spare time for me despite being busy. I feel bad but glad at the same time...... Thank you very much.

Roka Suoh 		じゃーん、ゆづくん仕様の特大ケーキ！近くのケーキ屋で予約しておいたんだ☆ゆづくんは何でも食べてくれるから贈りがいがあるねっ！
Tada, this is Yuzu-kun style special big cake! I ordered it from a cake shop nearby Yuzu-kun will eat everything, so it's worth giving it!

Kou Ayato 		ゆーづる。誕生日にまで残業する勢いじゃん。だめでしよ。今日は由鶴の好きそうなお店調べておいたからこれから一緒にどう?
Yuzuru, you're planning to work overtime even on your birthday, aren't you? You can't do that, I've looked up a store you might like, so how about we go together after this?

Mao Ukyo 		由鶴に休んでって言うのは、若干僕らの我儘にもなるからな。でも、できれば今日くらいはゆっくりしててほしくない？
Telling Yuzuru to rest will slightly be our selfishness. However, we do hope that he can at least take it easy for today, no?

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		由鶴さんお誕生日おめでとう~。いつもお世話になりつばなしやし、今日くらいは俺に任せてゆっくりしてな？
Yuzuru-san, happy birthday~ I'm always in your care most of the time, so leave it to me and take it easy for today, okay?

Yuragi Kanno 		はいこれ。カルシウムたつぶりのお菓子。由鶴が倒れないようにいつばい食べて
Here you go. A snack with lots of calcium. Eat a lot so you don't faint, Yuzuru.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		いろいろ考えてはみたんだが、やつばり城瀬くんにはこれがいいかと。前に話していたお菓子、持ってきたからよければ貰ってくれ
I thought about it a lot, and after all I think this is best for Kise-kun. I brought the sweets I mentioned before, do have some if you'd like.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		いつもマメに連絡くれるよね。お陰で、リモートでも大体の状況を把握できて助かってる
He always contacts me diligently. Thanks to him, I'm able to catch up with what's happening even if I work remotely.

Sei Okiya 		彼に休んでほしい‥‥‥と願ってしまうのは、我々のエゴなのかもしれませんね。なんせお仕事中の彼はすごく生き生きしていますから
The way we wish for him to rest...... might be us being egoistic, as he looks very lively when he's working.

Shizuka Fushimi 		今日くらいはのんびり過ごしてとか、城瀬にとって意味をなさない話だってのはわかってるから、せめて何か差し入れるか
I know that it's meaningless to tell Kise to at least spend his time leisurely today, so I should give him some refreshments instead.

Takeru Mikado 		由鶴のお祝いがしたくてたまらない私たちのためと思って、今日は座ってのんびりしてて
I think Yuzuru is taking his time to sit leisurely for the sake of us who can't wait to celebrate him.

Soyogu Shinkai 		今日くらいは由鶴さんの負担を減らしてえが、代わりが利かないことばっかで、本当すごい人だわ
I wanted to at least lessen Yuzuru-san's workload for today, but the fact that most of his work can't be taken over makes him truly an amazing person.

Shinobu Aizawa 		誕生日の主役はゆっくり休んでっていうのが由鶴さんの場合できなそうだから、せめてバンとか焼き菓子とか美味しいものいつばい買ってプレゼントしよっかな!
Since it seems useless to tell today's birthday boy, Yuzuru-san to take it easy, I guess I should at least buy him bread, baked sweets, or other delicious stuff as a present for him!

Kiho Arima 		いつも細やかにありがとう。今日は折角だし俺が由鶴に何かしてあげたいんだ。まずはお茶でもどうかな。とっておきのおやつを用意したよ
Thank you for always being meticulous, Since we're at it. I want to do something for you, Yuzuru, How about we start off with a tea? I've prepared the best snacks.

I wonder if Yuzuru-kun can immediately end a whole cake? If he can, then I'd like to give him a few of them to celebrate his birthday...... Well, it's Yuzuru-kun to be happy receiving them all.

It's because Yuzuru will gladly accept anything. How about a coupon "to use me as he likes"? I can help him anytime.

A popular person will naturally gather blessings, I'll take part in it, even if it's just words. Happy birthday.

Yu Nina 		キッチンでは助かっている。今日くらい休んだらどうだ
You're helpful in the kitchen. How about you take it easy for today?

I'm quite free this month, so I'm planning to have more shifts. I hope I can make things a bit easier for Yuzuru-san with this.
