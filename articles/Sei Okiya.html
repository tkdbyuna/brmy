Description]

A compassionate translator
A gentle-spoken gentleman who uses formal speech with everyone. He always carries a smile on his face, but no one can read his true intentions, which makes him suspicious. He is full of warped compassion for pitiful people, so he loves both Tsukimoto and Ichikawa.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • House[2]: Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙 & Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 & Sei Okiya 📘

Three people are living under one roof, named House. House is a 5LDK share house that belongs to Sei Okiya and was inherited from his grandmother.[3] Their relationship is balanced between Tsukimoto, who takes good care of the others, Ichikawa, who is selfish only with Tsukimoto, and Okiya, who warmly watches over them. They all get along well and normally gather in the living room to play games and so on.

  • Indoor duo[4]: Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 and Sei Okiya 📘

Due to the nature of their work as a designer and a translator, they work from home a lot.

  • Neither too close nor too distant[5]: Sei Okiya 📘 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

Shinkai met Okiya, a translator, when he was introduced to assist with Okiya’s work as a short-term part-time job. He continues to help Okiya regularly. While they share no particular common interests beyond translation, they hold mutual respect. Neither adopts the other's hobbies or preferences, yet they maintain a relationship of mutual recognition.

  • Suspicious Pair[6]: Sei Okiya 📘 and Kiho Arima 🔮

Among the veterans, Okiya and Arima get along particularly well. Even if they’re just making small talk, people will say that "it looks like something fishy is going on”. They met through Okiya’s relative, who was a client of Arima’s, and became close after that.
