Story]

You can read various stories in Break My Case.

◆ Story Types]

The following types of stories are available on the Story screen.

[Main Story]
Main article: Main Story

You can read the Main Story featuring 21 unique Aporia staff members.
The Main Story is unlocked as you raise your User Rank. You can raise your User Rank by collecting User Rank EXP, which can be earned through puzzles and spins.

[Personal Story]
Main article: Personal Story

You can get to know the Aporia staff better by reading their personal stories.
Personal stories are unlocked by collecting “NICE” from staff members.

Staff members can earn “NICE” by participating in puzzles and spins.

[Card Story]
Main article: Card Story

You can read the card stories associated with each card.
Card stories are unlocked as you raise your Story Lv. You can increase your Story Lv. by playing spins or by using [Disc], an item designed to boost your Story Lv., in the card enhancement screen.

[Event Story]
Main article: Event Story

You can read the event stories released during the event.
Stories unlocked during the event can be re-read from this screen even after the event has ended. You’ll need a story key to unlock past stories that haven’t been unlocked yet.
※Event stories are available only for a limited time and must be unlocked within their release period.


Other stories that aren't featured in the Story screen:

[Login Story]
Main article: Login Story

Available during a limited campaigns

◆ Favorite]

Tap the [Favorite] button on the Story Top screen to view the stories you’ve added to your favorites.

To add a story to your favorites, tap the menu button in the upper-right corner while the story is playing, then tap the [Favorite] button in the menu that appears.

◆ Bookmark]

Tap the [Bookmark List] button on the Story Top screen to view stories you’ve bookmarked. While a story is playing, tap the menu button in the top right corner, then tap the [Bookmark] button in the menu that opens to set a bookmark and save your progress.

※You can set only one bookmark per story.

◆ Story Playback Screen]

You can advance the story by tapping the screen while it is playing, or by tapping the [AUTO] button in the top-right corner to enable auto-play. Additionally, while a story is playing, you can use the following features from the menu button above the [AUTO] button:

[Settings]

You can change various story settings.

[UI OFF]

Hides text, buttons, and other elements. Tapping the screen while hidden will restore the UI.

[LOG]

View a log of the stories you have played.

[Bookmark]

Set a bookmark for the currently playing story. Setting a bookmark saves the current view and pauses story playback.

[Favorite]

Add the currently playing story to your favorites.

[SKIP]

Skip to the end of the currently playing story.

◆ Story Puzzles]

As you progress through the main story, puzzles may occur within the story. Achieve targets such as a score rank or clearing specific pieces to clear the puzzle, and you can continue reading the story.
※The story plays automatically during puzzles.

※Story puzzles can be skipped once they have been cleared.
※Please note that cleared story puzzles are not eligible for missions related to those puzzles.

Story Key]

This item is used to unlock event stories that have not yet been unlocked.
※Event stories are available only for a limited time and must be unlocked within their release period.
