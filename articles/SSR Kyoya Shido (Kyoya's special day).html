Home ①
 日付が変わった瞬間通知なんて十中八九、兄さん達からだろうな。一応、後でお礼の電話を入れておこうか。
The moment the date changes, it’s almost certainly a notification from my brothers. I guess I should call to thank them later.
Home ②
 今日という日は、どこで何度祝われても嬉しいものだね。お祝いありがとう、“誕生日”らしい誕生日を迎えられたよ。
Today is a day that makes me happy, no matter where and how many times I’m celebrated. Thank you for the wishes; I’ve had a birthday that truly feels like a birthday.
Home ③
 誕生日だからって我儘を許してくれる人達がそばにいるのは得難いことだよね。今日は吏来さんに美味しいお酒でもリクエストしようかな。
It’s rare to have people around who allow me to be a little selfish just because it’s my birthday. Maybe I’ll request some delicious sake from Riku-san today.
