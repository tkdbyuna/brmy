Characters Roka Suoh's Birthday (2nd)

Ai Kosaka 		普通、大人になると歳を重ねるごとに落ち着くはずだが‥‥‥芦佳は騒がしさが年齢に比例している。早く反比例しろ
Adults normally become calmer as they age but...... Roka's noisiness is proportional to his age. Quickly inverse proportion it.

Yuzuru Kise 		芦佳さんに出会えたことにも、芦佳さんが出会ってきた人達に出会わせてくれたことにも、感謝が尽きません。お誕生日、本当におめでとうございます
I can't stop being grateful to have met Roka-san and been introduced to the people he has met. Happy birthday, I wish you all the best.

Roka Suoh 		みんなからのおめでとうは何度聞いても嬉しいものだね。録音して僕の目覚ましにしたいぐらいだよ。‥‥‥ハッ、我ながらナイスアイデアでは！？
The congratulations from everyone always make me happy, no matter how many times I've heard it. So much that I want to record and set it as my alarm. ......Hah, I might've come up with a nice idea!?

Kou Ayato 		あーあー、相変わらず元気そうで。祝わなくても祝われた気になってくれそうじゃない？　‥‥‥ま、後で追いかけ回されても困るし、プレゼント置いて静かに退散しますか
Aa~ah, you're as cheerful as always. You'll still feel celebrated even if I don't celebrate it, right? ......Well, getting chased around would be troubling, so I should just leave a present and retreat silently.

Mao Ukyo 		芦佳さんは、普段から落ち着きがなくて目が離せないけど、誰かを傷つけるような人じゃないから、そこは安心してる
We can't really take our eyes off Roka-san since he's usually never calm, but he's not someone who'd hurt others, and that part of him is reassuring.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		芦佳さんていつも全力！　って感じがして、見てて気持ちええよなあ。誕生日も祝い甲斐があるわ
Roka-san is always full power! Or so I feel, and it's fun to watch him. His birthday is worth celebrating.

Yuragi Kanno 		祝っても祝わなくても、芦佳はうるさい‥‥‥。お菓子あげるから、しばらく静かにしてほしい
Whether celebrated or not. Roka is always noisy...... I'll give you some sweets, so I want you to stay quiet for a while.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		レアがよく須王さんから覚えた言葉を話してるんだが、いつも楽しそうで微笑ましくなるんだよな
Rare often say words it learned from Roka-san and always look like it's having fun, hearing it makes me smile.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		芦佳さんって、配信者向いてそうだよね。マイク渡したら永遠にハイテンションで喋りそうだし‥‥‥。いや、渡さなくてもか
Roka-san might be suited to be a streamer. It looks like he'd excitedly talk nonstop when given a mic..... No, I don't even need to give him one, huh.

Sei Okiya 		人気者の芦佳さんですが、どんな、誰のお祝いの声も区別せず受け取ってくださるので年毎にその数は増えていくのだと思います
The popular Roka-san would accept any congratulatory words from anyone without differentiating them, so I think that number would increase every year.

Shizuka Fushimi 		芦佳さん、おめでとうございます。年に一度のノルマだとは思ってませんよ、普通に祝ってます
Roka-san, congratulations. I don't think of it as a once-in-a-year quota, I'm just celebrating normally.

Takeru Mikado 		見て、あのはしゃぎっぷり。毎年思うんだけど、歳をとったっていうより、若返ってるんじゃってくらい元気なのよね。だとしたら羨ましいわ
Look at how excited he is. I always think about this every year though, he might be getting younger rather than getting older with that cheerfulness. I'm envious of it if that's the case.

Soyogu Shinkai 		年長者が誕生日にこんなに元気でいてくれんのは、いいことだ。ただ小学生の頃の俺よりはしゃいでそうなんだよな‥‥‥
Having the eldest be this cheerful on his birthday is a nice thing. However, he seems to be more excited than I was back in elementary school......

Shinobu Aizawa 		芦佳さん帰ってくるって言ってたから、今日は盛大にバースデーパーティかな～。リビングのドア開けた瞬間クラッカー鳴らすのもアリかも
Roka-san told me that he's coming home, so I guess I should hold a grand birthday party~ Popping crackers at the moment he opens the door might be a good idea.

Kiho Arima 		今日も賑やかで安心したよ。これからも変わらない芦佳で‥‥‥、末永くよろしくね
I'm relieved to see you as lively as always today. May you stay unchanging, Roka...... I'll be in your care for a long time.

Kyoya Shido 		芦佳さんは、本質を見極める力を持っているよね。人を見る目は本物だと思うよ
Roka-san has the ability to discern the true nature of things. I think his way of seeing people is genuine.

Riku Tateshina 		欲しい物聞いたら「お祝いの気持ち」だってさ。本当、目に見えないものばっかり欲しがるんだから
When I ask what he wants, he said "feelings of celebration". He really keeps wanting invisible things.

Tomose Onda 		芦佳は祝い事でクラッカーを鳴らすのが好きだったな。‥‥‥あれで代用できるか。少し待て
I remember that Roka likes to pop off crackers to celebrate. ......That might work as a substitute. Wait for a bit.

Yu Nina 		周りの祝い以上に騒がしかった。‥‥‥思い返してみればいつものことか
He was louder than the celebrations around him. ......Now that I think about it again, it's actually the same as usual.

Kamiya 		はは、チャッタスが盛り上がってる。芦佳さんが賑やかだと、こっちまで楽しくなってくるんだよな
Haha, it's getting hyped up in Chattask. Roka-san being lively really makes us feel the fun.

Urara Manami 		いつにも増してうるせえ！　こいつもこいつを調子に乗らせるやつらも、全員オレから遠いとこに隔離しろ
It's noisier than ever! This guy and all of those who let this guy get carried away, go isolate yourselves far away from me.
