Description]

A competitive intelligent fighter
A martial artist with a well-rounded physique and natural sense. Ultimately competitive. He also has an intelligent side and works as an assistant to Okiya's translation business. A secretly popular man who is polite and casually considerate. The only one who is not on the same page as Kosaka and is in a cat-and-dog relationship with him.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Watchdog Dept.[2] Shizuka Fushimi 💻 & Takeru Mikado 🌹 & Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

While they have individual friendships, they primarily build relationships as a group of four. When someone suggests going out for drinks with two others, it usually becomes a group invitation for the other two as well. They have immense trust in Fushimi as our leader who makes sound judgments and gives clear direction, yet they maintain a healthy dynamic where everyone feels comfortable voicing their opinions.

  • Watchdog Dept.’s Intellectual Pair[3]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

In the same department and living in the same dorm, these two are fellow intellectuals and neighbours. If one were to say what work they are in charge of, it would be “stillness” and “motion” respectively. Although those are total opposites, they have a mutual respect for one another. Even from those within Aporia, Fushimi is someone Shinkai holds in particularly high esteem. A clear head and sharp mind duo.

  • ULTRA BIG[4]: Takeru Mikado 🌹 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

Overwhelmingly physically dominant. Both are large-scale and powerful in mind and body. They communicate things directly with each other, building an incredibly comfortable relationship. As heavy drinkers, their one-on-one drinks before holidays that often last until noon.

Watchdog Dept. used to only consists of Mika and Soyogu before expanding with the addition of Shinobu and Urara. Mika took the leader role after the expansion and before Shizuka was scouted. The two often go drinking together and tell each other stories.

  • The Ultimate Duo[5]: Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

They're not exactly inseparable, but they often pair up for the Watchdog Dept.'s work and get along well enough to hang out together in their free time. Though their personalities differ, they both have strong communication skills, so they click.

  • Oil and Water[6]: Ai Kosaka 👑 and Soyogu Shinkai⚔️

Shinkai, who treats everyone equally regardless of age or status, and Kosaka, who values being respected by others, are very mismatched. They often complain about each other's actions, and each feels the other is to blame. Nonetheless, they do have a certain level of trust in their work together.

  • Ex-Classmates[7]: Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

They were in the same class for two years in middle school.[8] Though they weren’t particularly close, they met several years after graduation again at a class reunion. This then led to Ichikawa joining Aporia.

  • Neither too close nor too distant[9]: Sei Okiya 📘 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

Shinkai met Okiya, a translator, when he was introduced to assist with Okiya’s work as a short-term part-time job. He continues to help Okiya regularly. While they share no particular common interests beyond translation, they hold mutual respect. Neither adopts the other's hobbies or preferences, yet they maintain a relationship of mutual recognition.

English Gin, blue curaçao, and orange juice blend into a vivid turtle‑like green. Despite its striking look, the flavor is citrusy and surprisingly approachable.
Grape Pine* / グレープパイン
Japanese 柑橘でさつばりしたロ当たりの酒は飲みやすいよな。　その分知らすとペース上がっちまうから気をつけろよ。　俺も気にしとくけど、結局自己管理が1番大事だ
English Citrus‑based drinks go down so smoothly, right? But that makes it easy to drink too fast, so be careful. I’ll watch myself too, but in the end, self‑control is what matters most.
