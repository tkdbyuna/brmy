Deeply good at being pampered
Friendly dog-type man. Has a caring nature and is good at being pampered. Doted by people older or younger than him. Has a simple and somewhat extreme way of thinking. Placed in the top ranks of "looks proper but is actually terrible" among the people of the store. Roommates with Kanno in the dormitory. Usually get yelled at for being too caring. [1]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Simulation Dept.[2]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ & Mao Ukyo 🧩 & Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Yuragi Kanno 🍬

They have more opportunities to work together compared to other departments, and they socialize outside of work too, but the sense of unity and camaraderie within their department is weak. Plans for fun activities like "Let's do something with the four of us from the Simulation Dept." rarely materialize, but if the four of them do end up going out, they can still enjoy themselves.

  • Yellow Maker[3]: Kou Ayato 🎞️ and Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️

Two people who tend to take on "Yellow" clients — those with warning signs in character acting. They're kindred spirits who share a lot of common ground. They bond over their shared love of movies. While their priorities differ, their tastes in films strangely align, so they often recommend titles to each other.

  • Cat and Dog Duo[4]: Yuragi Kanno ⭐️ and Akehoshi Hinomiya 🍬

Due to the alignment of circumstances and timing, these two share a room in the dorm. Although the dog-like Hinomiya and cat-like Kanno aren’t hostile to one another, they’re not particularly close either, and maintain a distance such that they don’t interfere too deeply in each other’s affairs. According to Hinomiya, “It’s kinda like I have a one-sided crush".

  • Bar Counter Trio[5]: Takeru Mikado 🌹 , Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

The trio is in charge of making drinks and serving customers sitting at the bar counter, both day and night. They often go out to drink together under the pretext of researching cocktails.

  • Lighthearted and Active[6]: Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 & Kamiya 🍩

Young people with footwork as light as feathers and the stamina to play energetically. They play both day and night. They're adept at discerning the lines they mustn't cross, maintaining an exquisite sense of distance in their relationships.

Etymology]

  • 樋 hino means water pipe
  • 宮 miya means shrine, palace
  • 明星 akehoshi with a different pronunciation, the word as a phrase can mean ‘Venus’.
    • 明 ake means bright
    • 星 hoshi means star
