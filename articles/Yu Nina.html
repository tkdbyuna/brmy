Description]

A Right-hand man that swears absolute loyalty
A man with cold, piercing eyes. Earnest, loyal, and never forgets a favor received. In the past, when he was leading a shabby life, he met Onda and was saved. His world is composed of "Tomose Onda" and "humans other than him", he also thinks of dying for Onda someday.[1]

Relationships]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • ST Dept.[2]: Tomose Onda 🌏 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Kamiya 🍩 & Urara Manami 🧹

"Though somewhat isolated from others, they surprisingly work well together"—a wolf pack-like team. While Onda and Nina generally avoid communicating with others, they chat casually with Kamiya and show concern for Manami. Though complaints may arise, their opinions never diverge.

  • Cause and Effect[3]: Tomose Onda 🌏 and Yu Nina🚬

The two of them met under unique circumstances and have been doing harsh work together as master and servant since they were young. Although their relationship now is one of equals, they maintain absolute trust in each other. Even in the present, they live in the same house out of long-standing habit.

  • Kitchen Crew [4]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Tomose Onda 🌏

Aporia's kitchen staff. They converse little but maintain a good working relationship. Typically, either Kise or Nina works the shift, with Onda having fewer shifts.

  • Staff Lunchbox[5]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Urara Manami 🧹

The people who make the lunchboxes for the late-night shift staff and the person who eats them. Manami writes down what he wants to eat in a notebook, and Kise or Nina will make a meal for him based on that. If what Manami dislikes is included, even just a little, he will complain with all his might, saying “Don’t add that kinda shit”. Even so, he never leaves a crumb.

  • Ikebukuro Crew[6]: Roka Suoh🦎 , Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 , Tomose Onda 🌏 and Yu Nina🚬

The quartet lives in the same apartment block in Ikebukuro. Suoh and Aizawa are roommates, the same as Onda and Nina. The apartments are slightly old, but they’re spacious for the rent, comfortable to live in, and in a good location. It is not a dorm.

Kentucky Coffee / ケンタッキー·コーヒー
Japanese バーボンという、ケンタッキー州で作られるトウモロコシを主原料としたアメリカンウイスキーにコーヒーを加えホイップをたっぷり乗せた飲み物。甘そうに見えるが、 しっかり芳酵な苦味が広がる。ちなみに、このお酒と某有名フライドチキン店は一切関係がない
English A drink made out of American whiskey, which is made out of bourbon, a corn-based ingredient from Kentucky, mixed with coffee with plenty of whipped cream on top. It looks sweet, but it actually has a rich bitter taste. By the way, this drink and a certain famous fried chicken chain are completely unrelated.
Old Fashioned* / オールド·ファッションド
Japanese この酒はマドラーでフルーツを潰して飲む。以前灯世にもそう伝えたら、皮ごと細かくなるまで砕いていた。フルーツの皮は栄養豊富だ。 何も問題はない
English This liquor is drunk together with fruits crushed using a muddler. When I told Tomose about this, he crushed it until the whole fruit, including the skin, was finely ground. Fruits' skins are nutritious. There's nothing wrong with it.
Caroline Smash* / キャロライン・スマッシュ
Japanese 最近、灯世が綾戸から借りた映画を見ていた。映画にはその時代の文化を映したものが多い。その意味では学ぶこともあるのかもしれないが‥‥‥主人公の気持ちの移り変わりや恋愛模様は、俺には理解し難い
English Tomose has been watching the movie he borrowed from Ayato recently. The movie showed a lot of the culture in that era. I could learn something from it though…… It's hard to understand the main character's changes in feelings and romantic behavior.
Sol Cubano / ソルクバーノ
Japanese ラムとグレープフルーツジュースを混せ、トニックウォーターを注いで作る。　グレープフルーツと香草の爽やかな苦味が美味しい
English Made by mixing rum and grapefruit juice, then topping with tonic water. The refreshing bitterness of grapefruit and herbs is delicious.
Ocean Blue Fizz* / オーシャンブルーフィズ
Japanese 『フィズ』とは炭酸水を使ったカクテルのことを言う。　スピリツツに糖やレモンを混せサイダーで割ったものが一般的だが、日本では、このカクテルのようにリキュールを割ることも多いようだ
English “Fizz” refers to cocktails made with carbonated water. Typically, spirits are mixed with sugar and lemon, then topped with soda, but in Japan, it’s common to mix liqueurs this way too, like in this cocktail.
Crown* / クラウン
Japanese 表情が少し柔らかかったように思う。　最後まで回りきれなかったが息抜きになったようで、よかった。　‥‥‥灯世は、俺こそそうだったと言うが
English I thought his expression looked a little softer. He didn’t make it all the way through, but it seemed like a good break, so I’m glad. …Tomose said I was the one who was like that.
Black Russian / ブラック・ルシアン
Japanese コーヒーリキュールべースで甘いロ当たりのカクテルだが、度数は高いので要注意。甘党のお客様には生クリームのせで「ホワイト・ルシアン」にもできる
English This is a sweet, light cocktail made with coffee liqueur, but be careful: it has a high alcohol content. For those with a sweet tooth, we can serve it with whipped cream as a "White Russian."
Vodka Rickey* / ウォッカ・リッキー
Japanese 材料がシンプルであるぶん、どのウォッカをどの程度注ぐかで味わいも濃さも大きく変わる。好みがあるなら注文前に指定した方がいいだろう
English Since the ingredients are simple, the flavor and strength can vary greatly depending on which vodka you choose and how much you add. If you have a preference, it’s best to specify it before ordering.
Disposable* / ディスポーザブル
Japanese カジノ用品は不正を防ぐために基本的にはすべて使い捨てだ。環境にいいやり方ではないが、カジノにおいてそれが安全策なのは理解できる。そのぶん有名カジノの未使用デッキには熱心なコレクターもいると聞いた
English Casino supplies are generally all disposable to prevent cheating. It’s not exactly an eco-friendly approach, but I can understand why it’s a safety measure in casinos. That said, I’ve heard there are avid collectors of unused decks from famous casinos.
