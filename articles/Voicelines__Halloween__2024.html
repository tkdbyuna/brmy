Halloween 2024

  • Release Period: October 22, 2024 00:00 JST - November 07, 2024 23:59 JST

Translated by Misora

Characters Halloween 2024

Ai Kosaka 		ハロウィンだなんだと騒ぐ人間の頭の方がよほど賑やかだと思うが。まあ店が繁盛する分には許容しよう
I think the heads of people who make a fuss about Halloween are way livelier than the occasion itself. Well, I shall permit the art where it makes our store prosper.

Yuzuru Kise 		トリートのお菓子は１つで大丈夫なんですけど‥‥‥こんなにたくさん。なんだかすみません、でもありがとうございます
I'm fine with one candy as the treat though‥‥‥ I somehow feel bad to receive so much, but thank you.

Roka Suoh 		特別メニューでカボチャの煮物なんてどうだろう？　競合他社に勝つビビッドなアイデアだと思わないかい？　え、冬至？
How about having boiled pumpkins in our special menu? Don't you think it's a vivid idea that will beat our competitors? Eh, winter solstice?

Kou Ayato 		お菓子くれないと悪戯するぞ～って、距離感バカに言われたら逃げとけ。特に明星とか、明星とか
"I'll prank you if you don't give me sweets~", run away when an overly friendly fool tells you that. Especially Akehoshi or Akehoshi.

Mao Ukyo 		仮装とかするの？特殊なメイクが必要だったら言って。ああいうの、興味ある
Are you going to wear a disguise? Tell me if you need a unique makeup. I'm interested in that kind of thing.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		毎年、店の周り完全に異世界やねんて。見に行く時は危ないから俺と一緒な
The surrounding store just transform to isekai every year. It's dangerous to look around by yourself, so go with me when you do.

Yuragi Kanno 		悪戯とかどうでもいいけど、お菓子はちょうだい
I don't care about pranks though, give me some sweets.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		今日はご近所のお子さんもくることがあるので、たくさんお菓子を用意してて。貴方もどうですか
The kids next door will be visiting today, so I've prepared lots of sweets. How about you have some too?

Haruhi Ichikawa 		バズってたアレ見た？　百均のさ、猫用の仮装グッズ出てるやつ。寄ることあったらお願いしてい？
Have you seen the thing that went viral? A 100 yen shop released disguise goods for cats. Can I ask you to buy it if you ever stop by over there?

Sei Okiya 		魑魅魍魎が跋扈する今日‥‥‥なんて、最も得体の知れないのは、人間だと思いますが
Ghosts and monsters go rampant today......I think the most mysterious beings are humans, though.

Shizuka Fushimi 		どうりで騒がしいわけだ。もしかして、君もトリックオアトリートって言う口？
No wonder it's noisy. Are you perhaps the type to say "trick or treat" too?

Takeru Mikado 		こんな日だと普段接点ない子でも話しかけやすいわよね。大河ちゃんとか灯世ちゃんとか運良く来ないかしら
Usually, those whom I rarely come in contact with will be easy to talk to during this day. Maybe I can get lucky enough to have Taiga-chan or Tomose-chan come?

Soyogu Shinkai 		仮装だなんだって、なんでもがめつく楽しもうとしすぎだろ。喧騒に紛れて物騒な輩も増えるしな。ま、どいつも相手になんねえけど
People are trying too hard to have fun with everything like wearing disguises or whatever it is. The number of dangerous people increases in the midst of the hustle and bustle by that. Well, none of them is a worthy opponent, though.

Shinobu Aizawa 		いつからですかねーハロウィンがこんな大規模になったの。楽しめる範囲で俺たちも楽しみましょ
I wonder since when Halloween became large-scale like now? We should enjoy it as much as we can.

Kiho Arima 		ハロウィンか。昔の仕事の名残で、こういうイベントに大きな抵抗はないんだ
Halloween, huh. As a remnant from my former job, I don't really oppose this kind of event.

Kyoya Shido 		仮装って一括りに言っても、すごく幅があるよね。やっぱり、やるなら本気でなきゃ
Even if they are lumped together as "disguise", there are many kinds of it. As I thought, I need to do it seriously if I'm doing it.

Riku Tateshina 		トリックオアトリート。なんて、若者文化に触れてみただけだからお菓子はいいよ、君が食べな
Trick or treat. Just kidding, I was only trying to feel the youth culture so I don't need any sweets. You can eat it.

Tomose Onda 		トリックオアトリート。俺はいたずらも菓子もいらないが、これを言う日だと聞いてる
Trick or treat. I don't need any pranks or sweets, but I heard today is the day when you say that.

Yu Nina 		菓子がほしいのか‥‥‥？
Do you want sweets......?

Kamiya 		ハッピーハロウィン！　ハピドをくれないといたずらするぞ、なんて～！　ははっ
Happy Halloween! I'll prank you if you don't give me Happido~Just kidding~! Haha

Urara Manami 		菓子もいらねえから出てけ。掃除の邪魔
I don't need sweets so get out, you're bothering me cleaning.

Tomose Onda 		ハロウィンは‥‥‥こういう催しだったか？
