Overview]

The confectionery manufacturer “Aporia Co., Ltd.” The team in charge of “Bachi Bachi Star”, led by Shinkai, was handed a notice regarding the discontinuation of the product. As they scramble to prevent this, in the office where sparks are flying, what do Kosaka and Shinkai realise about each other...? The person they’ve joined forces with is ‘that bloke’—the one they had the worst compatiblity with...

Play Activity]

OJT Event: Challenge Aporia staff members acting as trainers or other players on the event page.

  1. Surpass their score (Break) while playing a stage.
    ※ Your own high scores may become trainers for other users, and you can earn medals if other players cannot 'BREAK!' your high score.
  2. Can rent a full deck of event cards 3 times per day after the training phase,
  3. Can open four "prize boxes" provided in each shift by collecting "prize medals".
    ※Prize medals are shared in all shifts, and you can't access the past prize boxes within the shifts.
  4. Complete the Aporia staff chart special to this event by collecting the "chart stamp" from prize boxes

Playing OJT stages and regular stages both grant prize medals. Bonuses are primarily influenced by cards and Break combo counts.
(See #Event Bonus and #Advance Phase for more details)
Prize Medals can be used to draw items from the lottery box (including SR cards, gacha tickets, crystals, and chart stamps).
(See #Activity Reward for more details).
Four boxes are available during each shift, refreshing at the start of each new shift.

Training Phase]

  • Equivalent to tutorial stages, does not accumulate Break combo counts
  • Divided into three phases, upon completion, players may challenge others
  • Shift-based scheduling (minimally affects card bonuses)

Advance Phase]

  • Opponents are randomly matched, with 5 manual refreshes available daily.
  • Up to 10x Stamina can be used (Boost Bonus).
  • Break Bonus accumulates, granting a 5% bonus per +1 break, capped at 50%, failed challenges reset the break bonus count.
  • The Break Combo Leaderboard is viewable on the event page, showing the progress of the maximum break bonus and accumulated break bonus.
  • Special Stage (SP MATCH) may trigger randomly, granting an additional 10x bonus if successful, and a 2x bonus if fail.
  • Borrow the system's full event card (All skills are MAX) deck 3 times daily. You cannot change the rental cards' formation, and the formation will change based on stages and shifts.
  • Recommended: Achieve 10+ Break Combos. Play 3 times daily using 10x Stamina + Rental formation. Resetting shifts resets attempts (allowing 6 plays per day).

Medals' drop rate]

  • Event stage: Base value: 90 medals for a successful challenge (Break), 60 medals for a failed challenge (No Break)
Base value (90 or 60) × Boost Bonus (up to 10) × SP MATCH Bonus (10 or 2) × [1+ Event bonus (see next section)] × [1+ Break bonus (up to 50%) ]
  • Normal Stage: Base value: 45 medals (regardless of side and ratings)
Base value (45) × Boost Bonus (up to 3) × [1+ Event bonus (see next section)]

Prize Box]

  • During a single shift, 4 boxes are available, refreshing after each shift change.
  • Each box contains both stock items and random non-stock items.
  • Switching boxes does not require emptying the current one, but you cannot return to a previous box after switching; similarly, you cannot return to boxes from a prior shift after changing shifts.
  • In the lottery settings at the top right, you can adjust the limit for 10 consecutive draws (maximum 100 draws per session) or set automatically stop upon obtaining specific items.
  • One draw costs 80 medals.
