Characters Sei Okiya's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		誓の舌に合いそうな店を紹介するのはいいんだが‥‥‥その後長文の感想が送られてくるまでがワンセットだからな
Introducing Sei to some stores that'll suit his taste should be nice but‥‥‥ Him sending me a long paragraph of review after that is a set with that.

Yuzuru Kise 		誓さん、お誕生日おめでとうございます。管理部のお仕事は手伝えないものも多くて恐縮ですが、だからこそいつもありがとうございます
Sei-san, happy birthday. I'm afraid there are many Administrative Dept.'s work that I can't help out but that's why thank you for always.

Roka Suoh 		誓、誕生日おめでとう。君の働きが今日のAporiaを円満にしてくれていると言っても過言じゃないのさッ！　末永く頼むよ！
Sei, happy birthday. Saying that your work makes Aporia peaceful today is not an exaggeration! I'll be counting on you for a long time!

Kou Ayato 		隠岐谷さんとは関わる機会が少ないんだよな。だからと言って積極的に関わりたいかというと‥‥‥謎多すぎて畏怖って感じ
I rarely have the chance to get involved with Okiya-san, even so, if you ask me whether I want to interact more or not...... He's too mysterious, so I feel kind of frightened.

Mao Ukyo 		隠岐谷さんにも誕生日とかあるんだ。いやそのままの意味で‥‥‥人の子だもんね、そりゃそうか
So even Okiya-san has a birthday. No, it's as what it is...... He's a son of someone after all, that's obvious.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		誕生日オメデトウゴザイマス。いや、丁寧にもなるやろ。怒らせたら一番あかん人やで。笑顔が怖い人が最強説、唱えとくわ
Happy birthday. Well, I should be all polite. He's the number one person you shouldn't anger. I need to recite that people with scary smiles are the strongest.

Yuragi Kanno 		誓はいつも俺が食べてるの黙って見てるだけだけど、今日は誓の誕生日だし俺の分のお菓子、あげてもいい
Sei always watches me eating my snacks quietly though, I'm fine with giving him some of it since it's his birthday today.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		誓さん、いつもおいしそうに食べてくれてありがとう。今日は特別手が込んでるから期待してくれ
Sei-san, thank you for always eating the meals deliciously. I'm making something special today, so look forward to it.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		誓とは一緒に生活してても、マジで謎。‥‥‥なんか評判のケーキでも調べるか。で、大河に行かせる
Even if I live together with Sei, he's seriously still a mystery...... I should probably search up some popular cakes. Then ask Taiga to pick it up.

Sei Okiya 		お祝いをありがとうございます。人から祝福をもらうというのは、なんともこそばゆい心地です
Thank you very much for celebrating me. Getting everyone's blessings gives me a ticklish feeling.

Shizuka Fushimi 		これは偏見でもあるけど、あの人のことは話題にしただけで筒抜けになるような緊張感ないか？
This might be a prejudice, but don't you feel your nervousness gets fully maxed just by bringing that person up in a conversation?

Takeru Mikado 		おもてなしするから、私の作ったカクテルでも飲みにきてちょうだい
I'll be serving you, so please come to drink the cocktail I made.

Soyogu Shinkai 		誓さんにはかなり世話になっている‥‥‥が、ただ感謝を伝えるのも捻りがねえからな‥‥‥
I've pretty much been in Sei-san's care...... But, simply saying my gratitude has no elements of surprise......

Shinobu Aizawa 		誓さんの誕生日プレゼント考えてるんですけど、意外となんでも喜んでくれそうな気もするし、悩めば悩むほどドツボにハマってるー
I'm thinking about a present for Sei-san but he seems like he'll be happy no matter what he gets. At this point, I'm just digging myself into a hole.

Kiho Arima 		誓、おめでとう。君の締め切りが明けたら飲みに行かないか
Sei, congrats. Why don't we go for a drink after you meet your deadlines?

Kyoya Shido 		誓さんには何が良いか悩んだけど、さまざまな産地のコーヒー豆にしてみたんだ。淹れるのは大河くんだと思うけど
I was troubled with what to give Sei-san but I end up with coffee beans from various regions. I think the one who will make it is Taiga-kun though.

Riku Tateshina 		ツテでいい装丁の洋書が手に入ったから誓にあげるか。こういうのは価値がわかる人間の元にあるのが幸せだろ
I found a nicely formatted foreign book, so I should give it to Sei. This kind of thing would be happier in the hands of someone who knows its value.

Tomose Onda 		感情が読み取りづらい人間は、次の一手も読みづらい。隠岐谷は戦いたい相手ではないな
The next action of a human whose feelings are hard to read is also hard to tell. Okiya is not someone you want to fight with.

Yu Nina 		隠岐谷は何を考えているかわからないし、本人もわからせようとしていない‥‥‥というのは勘だ
I don't know what Okiya is thinking, and the person himself doesn't want others to know...... is what my instinct says.

Kamiya 		隠岐谷さんはかな～り謎多き人‥‥‥だから、突然アタックするよりは情報集めから始めまっす
Okiya-san is a ve~ry mysterious person...... That's why I'd start from gathering information rather than a sudden attack.

Urara Manami 		関わりたくねえ‥‥‥あいつこそやべえやつだろ
I don't want to get involved with him...... He's a dangerous guy.
