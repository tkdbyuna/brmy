Achievement is a type of mission that accumulates over time, as you play the puzzles and spin. The titles have text to commemorate your achievement.

The in-game achievement tabs mostly cover titles; however, there are also pins, another kind of achievement. Unlike titles, pins can be achieved by a variety of tasks, from simple to complex tasks, and they mostly don't have text.

Both titles and pins are used to display on a player's profile. One title and up to six pins for players to choose to showcase. To unlock them, players have to fulfill their requirements.

For a summary list, where it can be filtered, see more in Titles' List and Pins' List
