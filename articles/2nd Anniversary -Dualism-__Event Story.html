Kosaka is planning to bring his parents on a trip as a gift. In a café he visited on his busy days, he happened to reunite with Ayato, his junior during university, whom he hasn't met after a long time.
Kise who is about to join a new workplace after quitting his very busy former job. On his way home from a health check, he coincidentally meets with his acquaintance, Okiya…
Suoh runs a café with Arima. However, on top of being shy towards customers other than regulars, he keeps making mistake after mistake. He wishes to become like Arima…
Ayato came to look around in a café as a preparation for a date with the person he likes. After a certain happening, he was approached by a man who was on a date beside his seat.
Ukyo, who works as a beauty consultant, gets an invitation to go shopping together from Mikado. The purpose is to find a present for Ukyo's sister, Mayu.
Hinomiya works as a newbie actor under the name "Akehoshi Ninomiya". While engrossed in various jobs ranging from variety shows to dramas, unexpected good news arrives.
Kanno happened to meet a lost cat on the way home from shopping. With capturing the cat as a trigger, he forms an unusual friendship with a person named Ichikawa.
Tsukimoto lives as the "young head" in the yakuza world. While some underlings successfully graduate from living and working for the yakuza, there is someone who seems to be unsuited to this world……
Ichikawa works as an illustrator for a company. In an uncomfortable reunion, he reunites with a certain classmate and exchanges words……
A letter gets delivered to Okiya who lives alone while feeling disconnected from his family. The one who picked up the letter which got blown away by the wind was Tsukimoto.
Fushimi works as a freelance SE in the midst of uneventful days. One day at his business partner, Aporia, he gets invited to a meal by Kosaka……?
Mikado received a consultation from his colleague at the apparel shop, who confessed, “I ended up hitting my boyfriend.” While talking with Tateshina, Mikado begins to imagine, “What if it were me?”
Shinkai, who works as a desk editor at a newspaper company, begins to tell his drinking companions Mikado and Aizawa the reasons why he chose his current path.
Aizawa, who enjoys a fulfilling private life—such as making plans to drink with his “best friend since student days”—finds himself at his usual bar, listening to the stories of the patrons…
Former host Arima reunites with Suoh, who did a trial shift in the former club before. Initially indifferent, he gradually becomes aware of Suoh's charm……
Shido meets Arima on the way to a bar and ends up going for a drink together. As the conversation flows, Shido asks Arima regarding "the correct answer in this world"……
Tateshina gets asked about marriage in the middle of a counseling session. As Tateshina listens attentively to their trouble, the symbol of a promise made with his loved one on his left ring finger gleamed.
Onda spends fulfilling days together with his adoptive brother, Nina. They seem to be discussing what kind of birthday present they should give to their father……
Nina carries out his work as a mafia emotionlessly. While cornering his target, he crosses paths with 3 people who seem to be a family.
Kamiya is an outstanding student with a promising future. What stayed in mind of that Kamiya was a certain ballet performance.
Having lost his grandmother, Yachiyo, Manami lives silently with his father. When he was visiting her grave alone, Suoh suddenly appeared—
