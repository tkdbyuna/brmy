Overview]

List of all voicelines or non-audio lines that are used in games or associated with Break My Case:

Standard Voicelines]

Standard Voicelines contains voicelines used in Home, puzzles, SPIN, and other miscellaneous elements in the game.

Home Voicelines are used on Home, including common staff voicelines and Home Duo Voicelines. Voices naming "Home" can be heard in both [Card illustration] and [Live2D Model] modes, while Duo Voicelines are only available in [Live2D Model] mode. You can access common Home Voicelines in STAFF DATA: MENU > STAFF DATA > Choose staff > Voice > Common

See Characters' Voicelines to navigate each staff's voicelines.

Card Voicelines]

Card voicelines include Home voicelines and lines when the card's SP skill is activated in Puzzles. You can access cards' Home voicelines in STAFF DATA: MENU > STAFF DATA > Choose staff > Voice > Card

Limited Voicelines]

Limited Home Voicelines are available for a period of time. You can access them in the "CHECK" button: CHECK > LIMITED VOICE.
