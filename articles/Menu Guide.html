Global Menu]

Tap the [MENU] button in the upper-right corner of the screen to open the global menu. From the global menu, you can access the following features:

  • Shop
  • Profile
  • Friend
  • Exchange Shop (English name is Change)
  • Items
  • Staff Data
  • Setting
  • Jukebox
  • Account Management
  • Help
  • Support

Shop]

Main article: Shop

You can purchase items such as crystals in the Shop.

◆ Age Verification]

You must register your date of birth when making your first purchase in the Shop. Unlike the birthday setting in your profile, when registering your date of birth in the Shop, please be sure to enter your correct date of birth. ※Once confirmed, your date of birth cannot be changed.

◆ Monthly Purchase Limits]

There are monthly purchase limits based on your age.

  • Under 16: Up to 5,000 yen (tax included)
  • Under 18: Up to 20,000 yen (tax included)
  • 18 and older: No limit

◆ Bremile Pass]

Purchasing a Bremile Pass allows you to obtain items such as styles, and unlocks Bremile Missions, where you can earn additional luxurious rewards through Bremile Pass Missions.

Profile]

You can create your own profile by setting your user information, title, and favorite cards.

After tapping the [Confirm] button in the upper-right corner and selecting the [Share] button, You can save the profile image you created or share it on social media.

※You can also choose to keep some information private.

◆ Setting Favorite Cards]

Tap the box in the bottom-left corner of the profile top screen to set up to 4 favorite cards.

◆ Setting Support Cards]

Tap the card icon in the bottom-right corner of the profile top screen to change your support cards.

You can set up to one support card per attribute.

※TIPS: Your support and favorite cards stats can be accessed by hard pressing the card on the main profile page.

◆Other Users' Profiles]

You can view other users' profiles from the Friends or Support Selection screen.

Edit Profile]

Tap the [Edit] button in the upper-right corner of the profile top screen to set or change various settings.

◆ Profile Settings]

[Profile Card]
You can change the card illustration displayed on the left side of the profile top screen.
[Username]
You can change your username.

※Your username will be visible to other users, such as your friends.

[Comment]
You can change your comment.
[Birthday]
You can set any birthday you like.

※This is different from the date of birth you set when making your first purchase in the shop.
※You can change your birthday only once.

※Regarding Birthday Gifts: You can receive a birthday gift up to once within a one-year period starting from the service launch date (May 9). If your registered birthday has already passed within the one-year period starting from the service launch date, you will only receive a login bonus upon your first login after registration.

[Puzzle Achievements]
You can select the playlist to display in your Puzzle Achievements.

The stage with the highest score in the playlist will be displayed.

※The number of SS ranks earned includes those from all playlists.

◆ Title Settings]

You can change the title displayed on your profile. See also Titles' List

◆ Pins Settings]

You can set the pins to display on your profile. See also Pins' List

◆ BGM Settings]

You can change the BGM set for your profile.

◆ Privacy Settings]

You can change the information visible to other users. The following items can be set to public or private:

  • Join Date
  • Birthday
  • Main Story: Main Story's latest episode read
  • Total Logins: total number of login dates
  • Score Rank SS: Number of Puzzles rank SS
  • HIGH SCORE: Choose a specific staff member's high score

Friend]

In the Friends section, you can view the users you follow and the users who follow you.

◆ Following Friends]

You can find friends from [Recommend] or search by Friend ID.
You can check your own Friend ID in your profile.
※There is a limit to the number of friends you can follow.[Note 1]


Using a support card from a user you follow
will earn you more Support pt (15pt) than using one from a guest (10pt).
※You can also earn Support pt when a friend uses a Support Card you have set.

◆ Unfollow]

To unfollow a friend, tap the [Following] button on their profile. Once you unfollow them, the button will change to [Follow].

◆ Unfollowing Others]

To unfollow another user, select the user you want to unfollow from the “Followers” list, then tap the [Unfollow] button on their profile screen.

Exchange Shop]

Main article: Exchange Shop

You can exchange coins, crystals, and various points for items and cards.

◆ Types of Exchange Shops]

The following types of exchange shops are available:

  • Crystal Exchange Shop
  • Support pt Exchange Shop
  • Rank Reward Exchange Shop
  • Coin Exchange Shop
  • Gacha pt Exchange Shop
  • Pink Feather Exchange Shop
  • Select Ticket Exchange Shop

Item]

Main article: Items

On the Items screen, you can view a list of the items you currently possess. When you select an item, its description will appear on the left side of the screen.

Some items have an expiration date.

※Items that have expired will not appear in the item list.

Staff Data]

In the Staff Data section, you can view the cards, voice, styles, and NICE numbers of selected staff members.

◆ Top Screen]

You can switch between staff members by scrolling through the names displayed on the left side of the screen. Tap the [BONUS] button to view rewards that are unlocked based on a staff member’s Nice count.

◆ Album Feature]

Among the four buttons lined up on the right side of the Staff Data top screen, you can open the Album screen by tapping the top button.

The Album feature allows you to view a list of the staff member’s cards. You can switch between two views: thumbnail and large icon

◆ Voice]

Among the four buttons lined up on the right side of the Staff Data top screen, you can open the Voice screen by tapping the second button from the top.

On the Voice screen, you can play two types of Voice for the staff member: [General] and [Card-Exclusive].

◆ Style]

Among the four buttons lined up on the right side of the Staff Data Top screen, you can open the Style screen by tapping the third button from the top.

On the Style screen, you can view a list of staff styles. Use the [Switch] button to toggle between Style and Mini Style.

◆ Profile]

Among the four buttons lined up on the right side of the Staff Data Top screen, you can open the Profile screen by tapping the bottom button.

On the Profile screen, you can view detailed information about staff members.

Jukebox]

You can play the songs you've earned in the Jukebox.

※Downloading is required to play songs.

You can earn songs as rewards from missions and other activities.

The songs you’ve earned will be displayed in a list in the Jukebox. Select a song and download it to make it playable. The Jukebox supports background playback. You can enjoy your music even while your phone is off or you’re using another app.

※Playback will stop if you navigate to other features within the game.

[Play/Pause]
You can pause the currently playing song or resume playback.
[Repeat]
Tapping the Repeat button once enables repeat mode, playing the songs in the list consecutively in the order they appear.
Tapping the Repeat button again enables single-song repeat, continuously playing the currently playing song.
[Shuffle]
Tapping the Shuffle button enables shuffle mode, playing songs from the list in random order.
[Lyrics Display]
When playing songs with lyrics, such as “Personal Song,” you can display the lyrics simultaneously.
[Setlist]
On the song list screen, swipe a song or press the Setlist button to add your favorite songs to a setlist.
You can add up to 100 songs to a setlist.
You can also add the same song multiple times.

In the setlist, you can reorder songs by long-pressing them and delete them by swiping.

Account Management]

In Account Management, you can configure transfer settings and data synchronization.

By setting up transfer and data synchronization, you can play with the same data on a different device from the one you are currently using. You can configure transfer settings and data synchronization using the following methods.

※Your crystal and item counts will also be transferred.
※Once you transfer your data to a new device, you will no longer be able to play on your previous device.

◆ ColyID Account Linking]

  1. Select [Global Menu] on the home screen.
  2. Select [Account Management].
  3. Select the external service linked to ColylD or enter the registered email address, then select [Log In to Transfer]. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the linking process.

◆ Setting a Password]

  1. Select [Global Menu] on the home screen.
  2. Select [Account Management].
  3. After the colylD screen appears, scroll down and select [Other Methods].
  4. Enter a password of your choice in the [Password] field and select [Register].
  5. Confirm the password you set, then select [OK].

※Please use a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and numbers to create a password between 8 and 16 characters long.
【Important Notes Regarding User ID and Password】

  • Please record your User ID and password in a note or screenshot and keep them in a safe place.
  • Do not disclose your User ID and password to third parties or make them public.

◆ Regarding Apple/Google/LINE Account Linking]

※Note: New account linking settings have been disabled. in version 1.2.0 and later.

  1. Select [Global Menu] on the home screen.
  2. Select [Account Management].
  3. Tap [Sign in with Apple]/[Sign in with Google]/[Link with LINE ID]. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the linking process.

Support]

You can contact Support regarding game issues or to provide feedback. You can also review the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

◆ About Account Deletion]

You can request account deletion by clicking the [Delete Account] button in Support.

  • Once you delete your account, you will no longer be able to recover your game data.
  • All crystals in your possession will be lost.

If you agree to the above and wish to delete your account, please enter the text specified in the account deletion window.

Once you delete your account, your game data cannot be recovered. Unlike deleting user data, even if you have set up account transfer or linking settings in advance, you will not be able to restore your data.

◆ Difference Between Account Deletion and User Data Deletion]

If you select [Delete User Data] from the [Title Menu] in the upper-right corner of the title screen, your user data will be deleted.

After deleting your user data, if you log in again from the title screen, you can start playing from the tutorial with new user data.

※Once you complete the tutorial, you can skip it in the future.

If you have set up data transfer via Apple/Google/LINE accounts, username and password, or colyID account linking, you can resume playing with your deleted user data by linking your account or using your username and password.

※If you have not set up data transfer, deleted user data cannot be restored.

If you select [Delete Account], all game data—including your crystals and game progress will be deleted.

After deleting your account, even if you had set up data transfer via an Apple/Google/LINE account or your user ID and password, you will not be able to recover your game data.

Please note that we cannot provide support regarding user data deleted via [Account Deletion], even if you contact us. Please exercise caution when using the [Account Deletion] feature.
