Travelogue Series Event (Travelogueシリーズイベント, Travelogue shirīzu ibento?, lit. Travelogue Series Event) is the successor of the Prequel Series Event. This event type is later succeeded by Vignette Series Event.

How to Play]

Using normal stamina, play puzzles to earn Event Pt and Travel Coins (in short trv.coins). to exchange rewards. Event Pts can be earned in the playlists for targeted staff and targeted attributes (See more in Event Bonus) throughout the event, while trv.coins can only be earned in the second half of the event and from the event-exclusive stage.

During the first half of the event, only the new song stages are playable. The Another Story stages released during the second half can be unlocked by clearing the puzzle stages added to the event-exclusive playlist.

Players can use up to 3 stamina to clear a puzzle. The amount of stamina players use will multiply the drop rate of Pts and Travel coins.
