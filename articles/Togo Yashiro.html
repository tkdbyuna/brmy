Togo Yashiro

Description]

Ito's older brother. He has no obvious flaws other than being overly anxious and overprotective towards his sister, and a bit unlucky. A bright and sociable extraverted person. Has a habit of collecting things, so he is always collecting something (includes wide range of items, from tea freebies to luxury items).[1]
