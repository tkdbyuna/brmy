Description]

Omnidirectional intimidating "devil child"
Aggressive and rabid. He thinks everyone hates him, and he takes the stance that he should hate them, too. Being unconditionally kind to him will bug him. Although he has a bad mouth and attitude, he handles things with care. Is actually a lonely person who wants to be pampered. Annoyed when Kamiya comes to pamper him.[1]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • ST Dept.[2]: Tomose Onda 🌏 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Kamiya 🍩 & Urara Manami 🧹

"Though somewhat isolated from others, they surprisingly work well together"—a wolf pack-like team. While Onda and Nina generally avoid communicating with others, they chat casually with Kamiya and show concern for Manami. Though complaints may arise, their opinions never diverge.

  • Ferocious & Magnanimous[3]: Urara Manami🧹 and Kamiya 🍩

Kamiya enjoys looking after Manami for various reasons, particularly because Manami is the only one younger than him. However, Manami views this behavior as hypocritical and finds it "annoying." Despite Manami's aggressive attitude toward him, Kamiya remains unfazed and harbors no ill feelings. They often work together on requests.

  • Staff Lunchbox[4]: Yuzuru Kise 🍴 & Yu Nina 🚬 & Urara Manami 🧹

The people who make the lunchboxes for the late-night shift staff and the person who eats them. Manami writes down what he wants to eat in a notebook, and either Kise or Nina will make a meal for him based on that. If what Manami dislikes is included, no matter how little it is, he will complain fiercely, saying “Don’t add that kinda shit”. Even so, he never leaves a crumb.

Japanese 紹興酒とウーロン茶を混ぜたチャイニーズカクテルを代表する一杯。味の調和だけでなく、琥珀色でシンプルな見た目と、中華料理の華やかな色合いとの相性が良い。料理と一緒に写真に収めてみては
English A glass of mixed Shaoxing wine and oolong tea, representing a Chinese Cocktail. Not only a harmony of flavors, its simple amber color also goes well with the vibrant colors of Chinese cuisine. How about taking pictures of it along with the dishes?

English I seriously don't get why they would stay up late to come up with café menus at any given opportunity. Forcing themselves to do it will only ruin their tongues. Hurry up and go home, you're bothering me cleaning.
Panda's Dream* / 熊猫の夢
Japanese ‥‥‥なんであいつら揃いも揃って、パンダまんを俺宛に差し入れてくんだよ。誰も食べたいなんて言ってねーだろ。中身が甘いもんだったら、あの無能にでも押し付けようかと思ったが‥‥‥これなら食えるな
English ……Why did all of them give me panda buns? No one was saying that they wanted to eat it. I should give all of them to that incompetent guy if the fillings turn out sweet, but…… with this I can try it.
Red Eye / レッドアイ
Japanese ビールとトマトジュースで作るカクテル。目にも鮮やかな赤が印象的。Aporiaにはトマトが苦手なスタッフもいるとか。あなたはいかが……?
English A cocktail made with beer and tomato juice. Its vibrant red color is impressive. I hear there are staff at Aporia who aren’t fond of tomatoes. How about you?
Azure* / アズール

Shandy Gab* / シャンディ・ガブ
Japanese 空き缶と空き瓶の量やべえな。バーカンの奴ら、どんだけ飲んだんだ？新メニューの開発だって言いながら、ほぼほぼ酒盛りになってんだろ……

Brave Bull / ブレイブ・ブル
Japanese カルーアとテキーラを合わせたカクテル。「勇敢な雄牛」という名前に反して甘さと深みのある味わいで、女性のお客様が多いAporiaでも人気の1杯
