Overview]

You can read the card stories associated with each card. Card stories are unlocked as you raise your Story Lv. You can increase your Story Lv. by playing spins or by using [Disc], an item designed to boost your Story Lv., in the card enhancement screen.

Only SSR Cards, SR Cards and R Cards have Card Story.
