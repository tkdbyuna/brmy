0.5th Anniversary BRMY Party (0.5th Anniversary ブレマイパーティ, 0.5 Anniversary Buremai Pāti?, lit. 0.5th Anniversary Bremai Party) is an Gacha Event happens in the 0.5th Anniversary Campaign.

From this event, the cards illustrated by Yukihiro Utako, the game's character designer, are introduced. The first group of characters is Ai Kosaka, Kyoya Shido and Urara Manami.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.
