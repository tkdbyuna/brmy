2025 SUMMER BRMY Party

2025 SUMMER BRMY Party (2025 SUMMER ブレマイパーティ, 2025 SUMMER Buremai Pāti?, lit. 2025 SUMMER Bremai Party) is a Gacha Event happens alongside with the BMC RMX FES Vol.1 Event.

Cards illustrated by Utako Yukihiro that are released before also appear in PU.

This is a limited gacha!
They are only available during revival banners and special campaigns. When no such campaigns are active, these cards cannot be obtained.

Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): July 27, 2025 16:00 JST - July 30, 2025 12:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "2025 SUMMER BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 pulls for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 pulls for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.3)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.

※Note: For STEP 3 & 4, you also need to draw 10 gacha to earn the rewards.
