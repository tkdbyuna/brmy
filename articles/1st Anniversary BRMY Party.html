Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): May 03, 2026 20:00 JST - May 22, 2026 13:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal to draw 10 gachas, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "1st Anniversary BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning a 1st Anniversary SSR card.

  • STEP 01: SSR appearance rate is 6%, guarantee 1 SSR card and above
  • STEP 02: SSR appearance rate is 7%, rewards R Limit Break Piece ×1
  • STEP 03: SSR appearance rate is 8%, rewards SR Limit Break Piece ×1
  • STEP 04: SSR appearance rate is 9%, rewards SSR Limit Break Piece ×1
  • STEP 05: SSR appearance rate is 10%, guaranteeing the earning of a 1st Anniversary SSR card

After completing step 05, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.
