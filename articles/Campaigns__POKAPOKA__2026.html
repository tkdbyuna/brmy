Don’t let the cold win!
Here’s the announcement for the “Let’s Warm Up Together Campaign”!

Starting from February 6, the special gacha [POKAPOKA Archives] will appear, featuring cards that are perfect for warming up and feeling cozy.

In addition, there will be:

  • Special missions where you can earn exclusive [POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets
  • A special login bonus
  • A limited‑time lineup in the Coin Exchange Shop, offering exclusive gacha tickets and other items to make you feel even warmer

Why not enjoy BRMY while staying cozy and wait for the arrival of spring’s first breeze?

Special Mission]

The limited-time mission related to puzzles is divided into two halves:

First Half: “Let’s Warm Up Together - POKAPOKA MISSION” February 06, 2026 18:00 JST - February 09, 2026 15:59 JST
Second Half: “Let’s Warm Up Together - ATSUATSU MISSION” February 09, 2026 16:00 JST - February 12, 2026 12:59 JST

By completing missions, you’ll have the chance to earn [POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets to play the special gacha! Additionally, if you clear all missions in both the first and second halves, you’ll receive 100 Crystals.

※ Notes regarding puzzle missions apply.

  • Playing already‑cleared story puzzles does not count toward mission progress.*
  • If SP Skills changes the color of pieces, the pieces before the change do not count toward the clear tally.

Special Gacha]

A special gacha, [POKAPOKA Archives], is now available. It can be played using exclusive [POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets.

From the permanent Crystal Gacha lineup, warm and cozy‑themed SSR cards are featured.

  • Only the featured SSR cards will appear.
  • This gacha can only be played with exclusive [POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets.
  • Crystals and regular gacha tickets cannot be used.
  • No gacha points are awarded for playing.
  • SR and lower cards are drawn from the permanent Crystal lineup.
  • There is no guaranteed SR‑or‑higher slot.

[POKAPOKA Archives] Exclusive Gacha Tickets]

[POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets are items that allow you to play the limited‑time special gacha [POKAPOKA Archives].

[POKAPOKA Archives] Gacha Tickets can be obtained during the “Let’s Warm Up Together Campaign” through the Exchange Shop, mission rewards, and login bonuses.

Important Notice

  • These gacha tickets can only be used until February 13, 3:59.
  • Tickets are used by selecting the exclusive gacha from the gacha screen.
