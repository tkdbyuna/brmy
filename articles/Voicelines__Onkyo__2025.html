Overview

Collaboration wireless earbud between Break My Case and ONKYO DIRECT. Features 13 new guidance voicelines: voice prompts for operations such as pairing and connecting. The production order and distribution periods for three times:

Departments Order Period Distribution Period
Main Office & Simulation Dept.[1] 30 July 2025 15:00 JST - 10 September 2025 15:00 JST Mid November 2025 - Late November 2025
Administrative Dept. & Watchdog Dept.[2] 24 September 2025 15:00 JST - 31 October 2025 15:00 JST Late December 2025 - Early January 2026
Negotiation Dept. & ST Dept.[3] 21 November 2025 15:00 JST - 09 January 2026 15:00 JST Mid March 2026 - Late March 2026
