INTRODUCTION]

The second compilation album of personal songs that express the feelings of the staff who appear in "Break My Case"

In the second installment, you can enjoy songs by seven artists: Kiho Arima, Yuzuru Kise, Yu Nina, Takeru Mikado, Yuragi Kanno, Urara Manami, and Taiga Tsukimoto.

Please enjoy the vocal songs sung by the Aporia staff cast.

Includes cast comments exclusive to the CD.[1]
