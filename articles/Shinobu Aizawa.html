◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • Watchdog Dept.[2] Shizuka Fushimi 💻 & Takeru Mikado 🌹 & Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

While they have individual friendships, they primarily build relationships as a group of four. When someone suggests going out for drinks with two others, it usually becomes a group invitation for the other two as well. They have immense trust in Fushimi as our leader who makes sound judgments and gives clear direction, yet they maintain a healthy dynamic where everyone feels comfortable voicing their opinions.

  • Lifeline[3]: Shizuka Fushimi 💻 and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

Though he seems indifferent, Fushimi somehow always looks out for his comrades, and loyal dog Aizawa adores him for it. As the department head, Fushimi protects Aizawa's safety daily, while Aizawa regularly supports Fushimi's life by cleaning his room and doing his laundry for a fee.

  • The Ultimate Duo[4]: Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️ and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

They're not exactly inseparable, but they often pair up for the Watchdog Dept.'s work and get along well enough to hang out together in their free time. Though their personalities differ, they both have strong communication skills, so they click.

  • Bar Counter Trio[5]: Takeru Mikado🌹 & Akehoshi Hinomiya⭐️ & Shinobu Aizawa🐍

They are in charge of making drinks and serving customers sitting at the bar counter, both day and night. The three of them often go out to drink together under the pretext of researching cocktails.

  • Lighthearted and Active[6]: Akehoshi Hinomiya ⭐️ & Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 & Kamiya 🍩

Young people with footwork as light as feathers and the stamina to play energetically. They play both day and night. They're adept at discerning the lines they mustn't cross, maintaining an exquisite sense of distance in their relationships.

  • Ended Up Roommates[7]: Roka Suoh 🦎 and Shinobu Aizawa 🐍

After deciding to go on a years-long overseas trip, Suoh lent his apartment to Aizawa for safekeeping. However, Suoh returned much sooner than anticipated, and the two have been living together since then. As Suoh is frequently out, Aizawa essentially lives alone half the time.

  • Ikebukuro Crew[8]: Roka Suoh🦎 , Shinobu Aizawa 🐍 , Tomose Onda 🌏 and Yu Nina🚬

The quartet lives in the same apartment block in Ikebukuro. Suoh and Aizawa are roommates, the same as Onda and Nina. The apartments are slightly old, but they’re spacious for the rent, comfortable to live in, and in a good location. It is not a dorm.
