Description]

Works as Aporia's acting (deputy) owner. A prudent, sincere, but timid person. She freezes up when she's surprised, so people tend to think of her as someone who's unfazed towards anything.
She has a mindset of "anyone can replace me" which, on the good side, sounds humble and modest, but on the bad side sounds passive. [1]

Etymology]

  • Her default first name 衣都 (Ito) is a homophone of the word 糸 (ito) which means thread; a big theme of the game.
  • Her last name contains:
    • 弥 ya means increasingly
    • 代 shiro means substitute, generation, which alternative reading is shi, the same kanji incorporating her current position Deputy (代理 dairi)

Trivia]

  • Her age was first mentioned to be a year older than Akehoshi by himself in Thread 1: Irresponsible Pink. Her age was once again confirmed by herself when she said that Shinobu and Soyogu are the same age as her.
