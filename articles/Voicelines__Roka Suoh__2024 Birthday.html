Characters Roka Suoh's Birthday (1st)

Ai Kosaka 		ケーキを用意しておいた、１ホールだ。それを食って口の中の水分を奪われれば、小一時間は静かにしてるだろ
I prepared a cake for you, a whole cake. When your mouth gets dry from eating that, I bet you'll be silenced for a bit.

Yuzuru Kise 		芦佳さんのことだから、きっと行く先々でお祝いされてますよね。つくづく不思議な魅力に溢れた人だなあと思います
It's Roka-san so I'm sure he'll get celebrated wherever he goes. I think he's a person with a truly mysterious charm.

Roka Suoh 		今日はなんの日でしょうクイズだ！　１年に一度、いろんな人から言葉をもらえるというのは‥‥‥いくつになっても嬉しいものだね
A quiz on what day today is! Getting greetings from many kinds of people once a year...... makes me happy no matter how old I become.

Kou Ayato 		芦佳さんおめでとう。はいこれ、この前道の駅で見つけたいい感じに変なキーホルダー。どーぞ
Roka-san, congrats. Here, it's a nicely strange keyholder. Please take it.

Mao Ukyo 		芦佳さんの誕生日か。ちゃらんぽらんってあの人のための言葉なんじゃって思うけど、それだけだったらあの場所にいないよね
Roka-san's birthday, huh. I do think that the word "carefree" is exactly for him, though if that's all he were, it'd be surprising he holds his position.

Akehoshi Hinomiya 		芦佳さんて３６４日前から誕生日アピ凄すぎて流石に忘れられへんわ。それに当日はいつもの５倍喋るし壊れたラジオやねん
Roka-san kept mentioning his birthday since 364 days ago, so of course I can't forget it. Moreover, on the d-day he just talks five times more than usual; he's a broken radio.

Yuragi Kanno 		一昨日も昨日も誕生日の話してた、うるさすぎ‥‥‥明日も誕生日の話してたらさすがに無視する
He talked about his birthday both two days ago and yesterday... If he talks about his birthday again tomorrow, I'll surely ignore him.

Taiga Tsukimoto 		須王さん、お誕生日おめでとうございます。ありきたりですみませんが、砂糖たっぷり特製パイを焼いてきました
Suoh-san, happy birthday. Sorry that it's ordinary, but I baked a special pie with lots of sugar for you.

Haruhi Ichikawa 		何この通知‥‥‥チャッタス大盛り上がりじゃんって思ったら芦佳さんの誕生日か。しかも全部自演
What are these notifications... I was wondering why they're getting all excited in Chattask, but turns out it's Roka-san's birthday. Moreover, it's all by him.

Sei Okiya 		芦佳さんの誕生日ともなると、今日はお祭りのような賑やかさでしょうねえ。彼が
Since it's Roka-san's birthday, I guess today will become as lively as a festival. Himself, that is.

Shizuka Fushimi 		芦佳さんおめでたいですね。別に、皮肉じゃありませんよ
Roka-san, congratulations. It's not sarcasm.

Takeru Mikado 		芦佳の長所と短所は落ち着きがないところ。芦佳だからって理由でなんでも許されるところ。変わらずにいてちょうだい
Both Roka's strong and weak points are that he can't stay calm and that he'll get forgiven for anything because he's Roka. I hope he stays that way.

Soyogu Shinkai 		芦佳さん逆に凄いよな‥‥‥。あんだけベラベラしゃべっても噛まねーし
Roka-san is actually amazing...... He talks so much, yet he doesn't fumble his words.

Shinobu Aizawa 		この前寄った店に芦佳さんが言ってたガチャあったんで回そ‥‥‥と思ったんですけど、ダブり防止で場所だけメモりました。俺出来る子なんで！
Roka-san said that the store we went to together had a gacha machine, so I was thinking of pulling... Though I only remembered the place to avoid duplicates. Since I'm a capable boy!

Kiho Arima 		芦佳の自由な風みたいな性格には慣れたつもりでいたけど、それでも突風が来ることもあって。まったく、底知れないな
I intend to get used to Roka's personality which is like a free wind, but sometimes squalls also come. Geez, he's unfathomable.

Kyoya Shido 		芦佳さん、今日は一段とご機嫌だな。それは何よりだよね。あとで大きなケーキでも届けよう
Roka-san, you're in an even better mood today. I'm very glad to see that. I should send you a big cake later.

Riku Tateshina 		芦佳の誕生日だな。アピる割には何も欲しがらないというか‥‥‥ほんと、芦佳は芦佳って生き物としか言えないのよ
It's Roka's birthday. The fact that he keeps mentioning it yet doesn't ask for anything...... I can only say that Roka is really a creature called Roka.

Tomose Onda 		芦佳の誕生日だろう。数日前からあれだけ騒いでいれば覚える
I guess it's Roka's birthday. He's been making a fuss about it for days ago so I remember.

Yu Nina 		芦佳は騒々しいが、それを見守っている人間の顔から嫌な感情は感じない
Roka is noisy, but I don't feel any hatred from the faces of the people who watch over him.

Kamiya 		はは、芦佳さんすごいはしゃいでんね。俺も便乗してこよっかな。行ってきまーす！
Haha, Roka-san is getting so excited. I probably should join in the fun, here I go!

Urara Manami 		永遠に紙吹雪バラまく壊れたクラッカーみたいなやつだな、幾つになったら落ち着くんだ
A guy who's like broken crackers that scatter confetti forever, at what age will he calm down?
