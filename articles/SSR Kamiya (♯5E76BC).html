 いろんな人に感謝されて変な感じ。俺の方が感謝したいくらいなのになぁ。
It feels strange to be thanked by so many people. I feel like I should be the one thanking everyone.

I’m not really used to being celebrated. It feels a bit unreal, or like it’s someone else’s situation, and it makes me restless.
