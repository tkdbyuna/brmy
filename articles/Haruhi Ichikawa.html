Description]

A popular streamer with weak constitution
A super indoor type who has a weak constitution, which makes sunlight his natural enemy. He is also active as the streamer demu while working in Administrative Dept.. He eats small meals and is picky as much as Kanno. He is selfish towards Tsukimoto, but they share a deep trusting bond.[1]

◆ Official Notable Relationships]

  • House[2]: Taiga Tsukimoto 🌙 & Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 & Sei Okiya 📘

Three people are living under one roof, named House. House is a 5LDK share house belongs to Sei Okiya and was inherited from his grandmother.[3] Their relationship is balanced between Tsukimoto, who takes good care of the others, Ichikawa, who is selfish only with Tsukimoto, and Okiya, who warmly watches over them. They all get along well and normally gather in the living room to play games and so on.

  • Indoor duo[4]: Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 and Sei Okiya 📘

Due to their works' nature of their work as a designer and a translator, they work from home a lot.

  • Cat Friend[5]: Yuragi Kanno 🍬 and Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧

The day after hearing that "a cat (and a new staff member living with it) had arrived at the House," Kanno made an unannounced visit. This sparked an instant connection between the two cat lovers. Even now, Kanno frequently visits the house to see Ichikawa's pet cat, Okoge.

  • Former Classmate[6]: Haruhi Ichikawa 🎧 and Soyogu Shinkai ⚔️

They were in the same class for two years in middle school.[7] Though they weren’t particularly close, they met several years after graduation at a class reunion. That's what led to Ichikawa working at Aporia.
