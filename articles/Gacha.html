How to draw gacha]

You can use items such as crystals and gacha tickets to obtain staff cards. You can view the list of cards available in each gacha and their drop rates by tapping [Gacha Details].

[Style]
To check the style and mini style of the staff cards available in the pool

Note: Crystal Gacha doesn't have this option.

[Gacha Detail]
Show the cards' maximum status level, available time period, drop rate, and conditions
[Gacha History]
Show the maximum 3 latest draws within 7 latest days

Gacha pt]

In certain gacha events, you can earn Gacha pt based on the number of times you draw.

Gacha pt can be exchanged for cards and items at the Gacha pt Exchange.

◆ Important Notes]

  • Some gacha draws are not eligible for Gacha pt.
  • Gacha pt has an expiration date.

Once they expire, they will be converted into pink feathers.

  • The types of Gacha pt vary by eligible gacha draw and cannot be combined.

Crystal Gacha]

Crystal Gacha is a permanent pool where you can play using crystals or gacha tickets to obtain new staff cards to strategically conquer the puzzles.
All cards come with a card story and unique voice lines! SSR cards also allow you to enjoy live clips (animated card illustrations).
In a 10-time play, at least one card of rarity SR or higher is guaranteed to be drawn.

◆ Card Rarity Drop Rates (Normal)]

Rarity Drop Rate
SSR 3.00%
SR 17.00%
R 80.00%

◆ Card Rarity Drop Rates (Guaranteed SR or Higher)]

Rarity Drop Rate
SSR 3.00%
SR 97.00%
  • The drop rates for cards of the same rarity are set equally.
  • Please check the following drop card list for the cards that can be obtained.

Notes

  • Crystals (paid) will be consumed only after Crystals (free).
  • When playing "10 times," the card drawn on the 10th time will be drawn according to the guaranteed SR or higher drop rate.
  • The availability of the "1 Play per Day Limited Gacha" that can be played with paid crystals resets daily at 4:00 AM JST.
  • There may be instances where cards you already own are drawn.
  • Cards are drawn one by one based on the drop rate. For example, just because a card has a drop rate of 1 does not mean it will definitely be drawn once in every 100 draws.
  • The content, event duration, and other details of this gacha may change without notice.

Event's Gacha]

Event's Gacha is a gacha that is only available during the event period.

Five cards are introduced and picked up (in short, PU): three SSR, one SR, and one R card.

Cards from some events are added to Crystal Gacha after the event, while others aren't. Under special occasions (for example, an anniversary), the gacha for these cards will be opened. List of events whose cards aren't added to Crystal Gacha:
