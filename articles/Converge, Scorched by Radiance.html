Play Activity

In the cafe-bar event, you will make a drink using the ingredients collected in the puzzle and earn sales in "EN".

Drinks can be made from the MAKING button on the event's top screen.

When they are completed, they will be lined up at the counter, so tap or swipe to serve the drink.

When EN accumulates, you can release the event story and earn rewards such as event-limited rewards cards and Gacha tickets.

In addition, you can earn more ingredients than usual in event-limited playlists, as well as special ingredients related to this event. You can also use the background play function in event-limited playlists.

During the event period, staff will take turns in charge of each shift. Achieve sales targets and total sales targets for each shift and earn a variety of rewards.

Note:

  • In the café-bar event, you can play event-limited playlists by using regular stamina instead of event medals.
  • There is no event Pt or event Pt exchange in this event. In addition, there is no total high score ranking or event Pt ranking.

SPECIAL TIME

During the newly introduced SPECIAL TIME in this event, other characters will assist in the making process. During SPECIAL TIME, the making time is halved! SPECIAL TIME occurs daily in the timeframe 12:00 - 13:00 JST and 20:00 - 21:00 JST.
