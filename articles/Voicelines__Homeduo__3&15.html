Voicelines/Homeduo/3&15


Roka Suoh
樹帆とは阿吽の呼吸でやってきたからね、本当に心強いよ！
I’ve been working in perfect sync with Kiho[Note 1], so it’s really reassuring!

Kiho Arima
この機会に、どっちが阿でどっちが吽なのかでも決めておく？
Should we decide which of us is “A”[Note 2] and which is “Un”[Note 2]this time?

Translation Note]

  1. ↑ 阿吽の呼吸 (あうんのこきゅう, aun no kokyū) literally means “the breathing of A‑Un.” Figuratively, it describes perfect harmony or tacit understanding between two people — acting in sync without needing words.
  2. ↑ 2.0 2.1 阿 (あ, A) and 吽 (うん, Un) are the first and last sounds in Sanskrit mantras. In Buddhist tradition, they symbolize the beginning and the end, the inhalation and exhalation, or the opening and closing of the mouth.
