American Diner (アメリカンダイナー, Amerikan Dainā?) was a Snap'n Spin spot, which is first introduced on 2025/3/31, alongside with the event Coming Up! Assorted Special Menu.

Only one character appears here and attempts an action.

Every character has two types of actions, and the actions share the same nature between the normal spots (Space Casino and American Diner), yet with different props. For example, at Space Casino, Ai stacks the pyramid of trumps, while at American Diner, he stacks the popcorns.

The same as Space Casino spot, the characters have 2 two pairs for each rarity: SSR, SR, and R. However, this gallery collects only the unique content of cheki, and only Ai Kosaka, Shizuka Fushimi, and Kyoya Shido are acknowledged as having one unique R snap so far.

Requirements: none
