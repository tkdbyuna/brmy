Cartoon Factory (カートゥーンファクトリー, Kātūn Fakutorī?) is a rare spot featuring two characters interacting with each other.

There is only one fixed interacting action per couple, and it yields an extra three snaps (SSR, SR, and R) when both characters appear in the snap. Hence, it would be 9 snaps per couple.

Requirements: the lineup card team should have both of them.

Cartoon Factory Snaps' gallery is divided into 3 parts.
