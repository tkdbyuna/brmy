It probably applies to more than just art, but hearing from someone with deep knowledge changes your perspective.
Home ②
 絵画で見る植物は、本物を前にした時と同じとはいかない。どっちがいいかじゃなくて、単純に別物だろう。
The plants you see in paintings don’t look the same when you’re faced with the real thing. It’s not a matter of which is better; they’re simply different.

As you can see, I have no connection to art. I don’t possess the talent or skills for that.
