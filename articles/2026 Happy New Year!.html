Full Course]

Gacha Period (JP): January 01, 2026 00:00 JST - January 07, 2026 17:59 JST

Using Paid Crystal, you can enter a step-up gacha, namely "2026 SPRING BRMY Party・Full Course", to guarantee earning an SSR card.

  • STEP 01: 5 pulls for 250 100 paid crystal
  • STEP 02: 10 pulls for 500 350 paid crystal
  • STEP 03: Earn SSR Gacha Ticket (ver.4)
  • STEP 04: Earn an SSR card

After completing step 04, you aren't eligible to participate in the Full Course again.
