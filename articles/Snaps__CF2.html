From version 1.5.0, Cartoon Factory (カートゥーンファクトリー, Kātūn Fakutorī?) has new couples added to games.

Requirements:

  • The lineup card team should have both of them.
  • Duos in default mini styles, or mini styles from the 2nd Anniversary onwards.

Cartoon Factory Snaps' gallery is divided into 3 parts.
