Keywords]

Aporia]

On one surface, a café-bar, on the other, an agency that offers a “multi-proxy” service suitable for any request. That is, as long as it can be handled by a living person. With work split between the agency’s several departments, many of the staff hold jobs outside of Aporia.

One Million Incident]

That is the nickname given to the incident in which around one hundred million yen’s worth of cash, art, and jewels were stolen from an influential government member’s family’s villa. With the corpse of a trespasser was left at the scene of the crime, it made quite a splash in the news. A criminal group turned themselves in and testified to fighting with their leader, who wished to keep the stolen goods for themself. However, the location of the group’s leader and the money are still unknown. Years later, a member of the Diet, accused of receiving illegal funds, resigned and disappeared amid suspicions of being the mastermind behind the One Million Incident. Known as an unsolved case full of ambiguity, the truth to this incident is shrouded in mystery to this day.
